📖 Vocabulary
我现在有一点事。Wǒ xiànzài yǒu yīdiǎn shì.I have something to do right now.
昨天我有事，所以不能去看表演。Zuótiān wǒ yǒu shì, suǒyǐ bù néng qù kàn biǎoyǎn.I had something to do yesterday, so I couldn't go see the performance.
你想跟我去看下个表演吗？Nǐ xiǎng gēn wǒ qù kàn xià gè biǎoyǎn ma?Do you want to go see the next performance with me?
下个歌手的表演，我可以跟你去。Xià gè gēshǒu de biǎoyǎn, wǒ kěyǐ gēn nǐ qù.I can go with you to the next singer's performance.
晚上你方便去吗？Wǎnshang nǐ fāngbiàn qù ma?Are you free to go in the evening?
下午还是晚上更方便？Xiàwǔ háishì wǎnshang gèng fāngbiàn?Which is more convenient, afternoon or evening?
下午或者晚上都方便。Xiàwǔ huòzhě wǎnshang dōu fāngbiàn.Afternoon or evening, either is fine for me.
从来没去过。Cónglái méi qù guò.I have never been there before.
刚才你去哪儿了？Gāngcái nǐ qù nǎr le?Where did you go just now?
我跟直美逛商店去了。Wǒ gēn Zhíměi guàng shāngdiàn qù le.I went shopping with Naomi.
你买东西了吗？Nǐ mǎi dōngxi le ma?Did you buy anything?
我没买东西，直美买了。Wǒ méi mǎi dōngxi, Zhíměi mǎi le.I didn't buy anything, Naomi did.
她买了什么东西？Tā mǎi le shénme dōngxi?What did she buy?
她买了三件衬衣、两条裙子，还有一顶帽子。Tā mǎi le sān jiàn chènyī, liǎng tiáo qúnzi, hái yǒu yī dǐng màozi.She bought three shirts, two skirts, and one hat.
哇，买了这么多！Wa, mǎi le zhème duō!Wow, bought so much!
