📚 Grammar
💬 Sentences
More and more: 越来越 + adjChanging degree
记忆力越来越差。jì yì lì yuè lái yuè chàMemory is getting worse and worse.
身体越来越好。shēn tǐ yuè lái yuè hǎoHealth is getting better.
Although / but: 虽然……但是……Concession
虽然他有时摔跤，但是他的身体很好。suī rán tā yǒu shí shuāi jiāo, dàn shì tā de shēn tǐ hěn hǎoAlthough he sometimes falls, his body is good.
However: 不过Contrast (milder than 但是)
他的身体很好，不过他的脑子越来越不好。tā de shēn tǐ hěn hǎo, bù guò tā de nǎo zi yuè lái yuè bù hǎoHis body is good, but his mind is getting worse.
Interested in: 对……有兴趣Expressing interest
我对新技术有兴趣。wǒ duì xīn jì shù yǒu xìng qùI'm interested in new technology.
From… to…: 从……到……Distance / time range
从我弟弟家到老人院只要一个小时。cóng wǒ dì di jiā dào lǎo rén yuàn zhǐ yào yī gè xiǎo shíFrom my brother's house to the nursing home is only an hour.
Feel / think: 觉得Expressing feeling
他觉得很无聊。tā jué de hěn wú liáoHe feels very bored.
他平衡感不好，容易摔倒。tā píng héng gǎn bù hǎo, róng yì shuāi dǎoHis sense of balance is poor, prone to falling.
