💬 Sentences
你的周末怎么样？Nǐ de zhōumò zěnmeyàng?How was your weekend?
我们很累，我们在家休息。Wǒmen hěn lèi, wǒmen zàijiā xiūxi.We are very tired, we are resting at home.
他很卷。Tā hěn juǎn.He is very intense/competitive. (slang)
我们学校有很多学生。Wǒmen xuéxiào yǒu hěnduō xuéshēng.Our school 有 many students.
我们学校的学生很多。Wǒmen xuéxiào de xuéshēng hěnduō.The students in our school are many.
我们公司有很多人。Wǒmen gōngsī yǒu hěnduō rén.Our company 有 many people.
我们公司人很多。Wǒmen gōngsī rén hěnduō.Our company 有 many people. (colloquial)
马斯克有很多钱。Mǎsīkè yǒu hěnduō qián.Musk 有 a lot of money.
马斯克钱很多。Mǎsīkè qián hěnduō.Musk 有 a lot of money. (colloquial)
你错了吗？Nǐ cuòle ma?Were you wrong? / Did you make a mistake?
我错了。Wǒ cuòle.I was wrong. / I made a mistake.
你哪儿错了？Nǐ nǎr cuòle?Where did you go wrong? / What part was your mistake?
我哪儿都错了。Wǒ nǎr dōu cuòle.I was wrong everywhere. / Everything I did was wrong.
我说错了。Wǒ shuō cuòle.I said it wrong. / I misspoke.
你最近工作多不多？Nǐ zuìjìn gōngzuò duō bù duō?Have you been busy with work lately?
我最近工作很多。Wǒ zuìjìn gōngzuò hěnduō.I have a lot of work lately.
我爱人头发比较长。Wǒ àiren tóufa bǐjiào cháng.My spouse's hair is relatively long.
我头发太短了。Wǒ tóufa tài duǎn le.My hair is too short.
你比我高。Nǐ bǐ wǒ gāo.You are taller than me.
我比你矮。Wǒ bǐ nǐ ǎi.I am shorter than you.
她眼睛很漂亮。Tā yǎnjing hěn piàoliang.Her eyes are very beautiful.
他个子很高。Tā gèzi hěn gāo.He is very tall.
