📚 Grammar
Same kind: 一样 + 一个/杯/支……
One of each kind
一人一杯。yī rén yī bēiOne cup per person.
一样一个。yī yàng yī gèOne of each kind.
Same: A 和 B 一样 + adj
Equality comparison
我们是一样的。wǒ men shì yī yàng deWe are the same.
我们一样大。wǒ men yī yàng dàWe're the same age.
他们一样坏。tā men yī yàng huàiThey're equally bad.
烤羊肉和生鱼块一样好吃。kǎo yáng ròu hé shēng yú kuài yī yàng hǎo chīRoasted lamb is as delicious as raw fish.
Other: 别的 + noun
Other things / people
别的学校。bié de xué xiàoOther schools.
别的公司。bié de gōng sīOther companies.
别人。bié rénOther people.
别人家的孩子。bié rén jiā de hái ziOther people's kids.
Change of state: 不 + V + 了
Not anymore
不要了。bù yào le(I) don't want it anymore.
我不去了。wǒ bù qù leI'm not going anymore.
How to: 怎么 + V
How to do something
怎么写？zěn me xiěHow to write?
怎么读？zěn me dúHow to read?
怎么打？zěn me dǎHow to play?
怎么吃？zěn me chīHow to eat?
Again / more: 再 + V
Do again or do more
再要一个书包。zài yào yī gè shū bāoWant one more backpack.
再喝一瓶。zài hē yī píngDrink one more bottle.
再说一下。zài shuō yī xiàSay it again.
再吃一点。zài chī yī diǎnEat a bit more.
