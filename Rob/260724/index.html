📖 Vocabulary
换 huàn to exchange; to change

只 zhī single (measure word for one of a pair)
件 jiàn piece (measure word for clothing)
T恤 T xù T-shirt
衬衫 chèn shān shirt
衬衣 chèn yī shirt
衣服 yī fu clothes
左右 zuǒ yòu more or less; about
更 gèng more; even more
📚 Grammar
双 / 只 / 件（量词） pair / single / piece — measure words
一双鞋 yī shuāng xié a pair of shoes
一只鞋 yī zhī xié one shoe
两只狗 liǎng zhī gǒu two dogs
一件衣服 yī jiàn yī fu a piece of clothing
T恤 / 衬衫 / 衬衣 T-shirt / shirt
你穿多大号的？ nǐ chuān duō dà hào de What size do you wear?
课文：买鞋对话 dialogue: buying shoes
你好，我想买一双鞋。 nǐ hǎo, wǒ xiǎng mǎi yī shuāng xié Hello, I'd like to buy a pair of shoes.
您好，我们有很多鞋，您看看。 nín hǎo, wǒ men yǒu hěn duō xié, nín kàn kàn Hello, we have many shoes, take a look.
这双鞋不错，多少钱？ zhè shuāng xié bù cuò, duō shǎo qián These shoes are nice, how much?
500块。 wǔ bǎi kuài 500 yuan.
太贵了，有便宜一点的吗？ tài guì le, yǒu pián yi yī diǎn de ma Too expensive, do you have something cheaper?
有，这双质量又好，样子又好看。只要400块。 yǒu, zhè shuāng zhì liàng yòu hǎo, yàng zi yòu hǎo kàn. zhǐ yào sì bǎi kuài Yes, these are both good quality and good looking. Only 400 yuan.
还是太贵了，有更便宜一点的吗？ hái shì tài guì le, yǒu gèng pián yi yī diǎn de ma Still too expensive, do you have something even cheaper?
您要买多少钱的？ nín yào mǎi duō shǎo qián de What price range are you looking for?
200左右的。 èr bǎi zuǒ yòu de Around 200.
有，您看看这双，只要190块。 yǒu, nín kàn kàn zhè shuāng, zhǐ yào yī bǎi jiǔ shí kuài Yes, take a look at these, only 190 yuan.
这双还可以，我可以试试吗？ zhè shuāng hái kě yǐ, wǒ kě yǐ shì shì ma These are okay, can I try them on?
可以，您穿多大号的？ kě yǐ, nín chuān duō dà hào de Sure, what size do you wear?
42号的。 sì shí èr hào de Size 42.
好的，请等一下。 hǎo de, qǐng děng yī xià OK, please wait a moment.
您试一下，看看合不合适。 nín shì yī xià, kàn kàn hé bù hé shì Try them on, see if they fit.
这双有一点大，有小一点的吗？ zhè shuāng yǒu yī diǎn dà, yǒu xiǎo yī diǎn de ma These are a bit big, do you have a smaller pair?
有，这双是41号的，您试试。 yǒu, zhè shuāng shì sì shí yī hào de, nín shì shì Yes, these are size 41, try them on.
这双不大不小，很合适。我买了！ zhè shuāng bù dà bù xiǎo, hěn hé shì. wǒ mǎi le These are just right, very suitable. I'll take them!
💬 Sentences
我想换一双大一点的。 wǒ xiǎng huàn yī shuāng dà yī diǎn de I want to exchange for a bigger pair.
