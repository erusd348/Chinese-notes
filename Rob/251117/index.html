📚 Grammar
在 V = be doing (Action in Progress)
我在读书。Wǒ zài dú shū.I am reading.
我在吃生鱼片。Wǒ zài chī shēngyúpiàn.I am eating sashimi.
我在喝啤酒。Wǒ zài hē píjiǔ.I am drinking beer.
问地点 & 回答
你在哪儿？Nǐ zài nǎr?Where are you?
我在家。Wǒ zài jiā.I am at home.
图书馆在哪儿？Túshūguǎn zài nǎr?Where is the library?
图书馆就在那儿。Túshūguǎn jiù zài nàr.The library is right over there.
💬 Sentences
我在图书馆看/读书。Wǒ zài túshūguǎn kàn/dú shū.I am reading (books) in the library. (When, where, what)
我和我爱人在饭馆吃生鱼片。Wǒ hé wǒ àirén zài fànguǎn chī shēngyúpiàn.My spouse and I are eating sashimi at a restaurant.
我在茶馆喝茶。Wǒ zài cháguǎn hē chá.I am drinking tea in a tea house.
我在酒吧喝啤酒。Wǒ zài jiǔbā hē píjiǔ.I am drinking beer in a bar.
我在家吃生鱼片。Wǒ zài jiā chī shēngyúpiàn.I am eating sashimi at home.
学生在食堂吃饭。Xuéshēng zài shítáng chī fàn.The students are eating in the cafeteria.
明天我在食堂吃午饭。Míngtiān wǒ zài shítáng chī wǔfàn.Tomorrow I will eat lunch in the cafeteria.
晚上我在家吃了生鱼片。Wǎnshang wǒ zài jiā chī le shēngyúpiàn.I ate sashimi at home in the evening. (Note the past tense marker 了)
