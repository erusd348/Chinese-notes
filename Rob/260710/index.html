📚 Grammar
是……的（强调） (structure) emphasis
不是我老婆做的鸡肉面。 bù shì wǒ lǎo pó zuò de jī ròu miàn It's not that my wife made the chicken noodles.
是我做的鸡肉面。 shì wǒ zuò de jī ròu miàn I made the chicken noodles.
给 + sb + V (structure) do sth for sb
我老婆给我做饭。 wǒ lǎo pó gěi wǒ zuò fàn My wife cooks for me.
我给我老婆付钱。 wǒ gěi wǒ lǎo pó fù qián I pay for my wife.
我给我的坏狗买吃的。 wǒ gěi wǒ de huài gǒu mǎi chī de I buy food for my bad dog.
可以给我介绍一下吗？ kě yǐ gěi wǒ jiè shào yī xià ma Could you introduce it to me?
数字 1000+ (structure) numbers above 1000
一千 yī qiān 1000
一千零一 yī qiān líng yī 1001
一千零一十 yī qiān líng yī shí 1010
一千一百 yī qiān yī bǎi 1100
我们公司有一千五百人。 wǒ men gōng sī yǒu yī qiān wǔ bǎi rén Our company has 1500 people.
1000左右。 yī qiān zuǒ yòu About 1000.
牌子 / 质量 brand / quality
你要什么牌子的手机？ nǐ yào shén me pái zi de shǒu jī What brand of phone do you want?
我想要一个华为的手机。 wǒ xiǎng yào yī ge huá wéi de shǒu jī I want a Huawei phone.
华为的手机跟苹果的手机一样贵。 huá wéi de shǒu jī gēn píng guǒ de shǒu jī yī yàng guì Huawei phones are as expensive as Apple phones.
你喜欢什么牌子的衣服？ nǐ xǐ huān shén me pái zi de yī fu What brand of clothes do you like?
我喜欢拉夫劳伦的衣服。 wǒ xǐ huān lā fū láo lún de yī fu I like Ralph Lauren clothes.
你喜欢什么牌子的车？ nǐ xǐ huān shén me pái zi de chē What brand of car do you like?
我们有一辆BMW的车。 wǒ men yǒu yī liàng BMW de chē We have a BMW.
BMW的车质量很好。 BMW de chē zhì liàng hěn hǎo BMW cars have good quality.
苹果的手机质量很好。 píng guǒ de shǒu jī zhì liàng hěn hǎo Apple phones have good quality.
你觉得油车的质量好还是电车的质量好？ nǐ jué de yóu chē de zhì liàng hǎo hái shì diàn chē de zhì liàng hǎo Do you think gas cars or electric cars have better quality?
我觉得电车的质量好。 wǒ jué de diàn chē de zhì liàng hǎo I think electric cars have better quality.
华为的手机价格很贵。 huá wéi de shǒu jī jià gé hěn guì Huawei phones are very expensive.
BYD的车价格很便宜。 BYD de chē jià gé hěn pián yi BYD cars are very cheap.
