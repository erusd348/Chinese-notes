A 在 B direction。A zài B fāngxiàng.A is in the direction of B / A is to the B side of something.
上海在北京南边。Shànghǎi zài Běijīng nánbian.Shanghai is to the south of Beijing.
你在我前边。Nǐ zài wǒ qiánbian.You are in front of me.
杯子在桌子上边。Bēizi zài zhuōzi shàngbian.The cup is on the table.
There is sth sw。(yǒu shénme zài mǒu chù)There is something somewhere.
Sw 有 sth。(mǒu chù yǒu shénme)Somewhere 有 something.
Sw 是 sth。(mǒu chù shì shénme)Somewhere is something.
桌子上有一个杯子。Zhuōzi shàng yǒu yī gè bēizi.There is a cup on the table.
我家附近有一个商店。Wǒ jiā fùjìn yǒu yī gè shāngdiàn.There is a store near my house.
我家附近还有一个饭馆。Wǒ jiā fùjìn hái yǒu yī gè fànguǎn.There is also a restaurant near my house.
我们学校里边有……Wǒmen xuéxiào lǐbian yǒu...There is... inside our school.
他还是一个孩子。Tā hái shì yī gè háizi.He is still a child.
我还没吃饭。Wǒ hái méi chīfàn.I haven't eaten yet.
我还在家。Wǒ hái zài jiā.I'm still at home.
我还在车上。Wǒ hái zài chē shàng.I'm still on the bus/car.
我最喜欢的电影是教父。Wǒ zuì xǐhuan de diànyǐng shì Jiàofù.My favorite movie is The Godfather.
