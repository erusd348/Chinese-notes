你有兄弟姐妹吗？Nǐ yǒu xiōngdì jiěmèi ma?Do you have siblings?
是的，我有两个妹妹。Shìde, wǒ yǒu liǎng ge mèimei.Yes, I have two younger sisters.
你（今天）忙吗？Nǐ (jīntiān) máng ma?Are you busy (today)?
我（今天）很忙 / 不忙。Wǒ (jīntiān) hěn máng / bù máng.I’m (very) busy / not busy (today).
