💬 Sentences
新年好/快乐！Xīnnián hǎo/kuàilè!Happy New Year!
走。Zǒu.I'm leaving. / Let's go.
你们公司同事工作努力吗？Nǐmen gōngsī tóngshì gōngzuò nǔlì ma?Are your colleagues at the company hard-working?
他们工作太努力了。Tāmen gōngzuò tài nǔlì le.They work too hard.
不太努力。Bú tài nǔlì.Not very hard-working.
教室/公司/学校/家里Jiàoshì/gōngsī/xuéxiào/jiā lǐin the classroom/company/school/home
教室里有50个学生。Jiàoshì li yǒu wǔshí gè xuésheng.There are 50 students in the classroom.
我公司（里）有1千个人。Wǒ gōngsī (li) yǒu yī qiān gè rén.There are 1,000 people in my company.
图书馆里有很多书。Túshūguǎn li yǒu hěn duō shū.There are many books in the library.
银行里有很多钱。Yínháng li yǒu hěn duō qián.There is a lot of money in the bank.
饭店里有很多吃的。Fàndiàn li yǒu hěn duō chī de.There is a lot of food in the restaurant.
教室里没有学生。Jiàoshì li méiyǒu xuésheng.There are no students in the classroom.
夏威夷没有很多人。Xiàwēiyí méiyǒu hěn duō rén.There aren't many people in Hawaii.
我家里有空调和很多风扇。Wǒ jiā li yǒu kōngtiáo hé hěn duō fēngshàn.There is an air conditioner and many fans in my house.
我在大学的时候，我们宿舍有8个人。我有7个室友。Wǒ zài dàxué de shíhòu, wǒmen sùshè yǒu bā gè rén. Wǒ yǒu qī gè shìyǒu.When I was in university, there were 8 people in our dorm. I had 7 roommates.
夏威夷离芝加哥很远。Xiàwēiyí lí Zhījiāgē hěn yuǎn.Hawaii is very far from Chicago.
中国离美国很远。Zhōngguó lí Měiguó hěn yuǎn.China is very far from the United States.
德国离美国很远。Déguó lí Měiguó hěn yuǎn.Germany is very far from the United States.
我家离办公室很近。Wǒ jiā lí bàngōngshì hěn jìn.My home is very close to the office.
中国离韩国很近。Zhōngguó lí Hánguó hěn jìn.China is very close to South Korea.
