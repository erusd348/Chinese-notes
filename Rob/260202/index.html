你在做什么呢？Nǐ zài zuò shénme ne?What are you doing?
我在做饭呢。Wǒ zài zuò fàn ne.I am cooking.
有的...，有的...yǒude..., yǒude...A pattern meaning "some..., others..."
有的狗很可爱，有的狗不可爱。Yǒude gǒu hěn kě'ài, yǒude gǒu bù kě'ài.Some dogs are very cute, some dogs are not cute.
你什么时候去了德国？Nǐ shénme shíhou qù le Déguó?When did you go to Germany?
我1989年和1990年去了德国。Wǒ yī jiǔ bā jiǔ nián hé yī jiǔ jiǔ líng nián qù le Déguó.I went to Germany in 1989 and 1990.
我有的时候早上7点上班。Wǒ yǒude shíhou zǎoshang qī diǎn shàngbān.Sometimes I go to work at 7 a.m.
我住在德国的时候，我学习德语和数学。Wǒ zhù zài Déguó de shíhou, wǒ xuéxí Déyǔ hé shùxué.When I lived in Germany, I studied German and mathematics.
我的狗常常（在）6点30分起床。Wǒ de gǒu chángcháng (zài) liù diǎn sānshí fēn qǐchuáng.My dog often gets up at 6:30.
我常(常)吃汉堡。Wǒ cháng(cháng) chī hànbǎo.I often eat hamburgers.
我常(常) 锻炼。Wǒ cháng(cháng) duànliàn.I often exercise.
我常在家吃饭。Wǒ cháng zàijiā chīfàn.I often eat at home.
我跟我爱人一起吃饭。Wǒ gēn wǒ àirén yīqǐ chīfàn.I eat together with my spouse.
我跟我的狗一起玩/去商店。Wǒ gēn wǒ de gǒu yīqǐ wán/qù shāngdiàn.I play/go to the store together with my dog.
