你们食堂的饭好吃吗？Nǐmen shítáng de fàn hǎo chī ma?Is the food in your cafeteria good?
我们食堂的饭一般。Wǒmen shítáng de fàn yìbān.The food in our cafeteria is average.
你在食堂吃什么？Nǐ zài shítáng chī shénme?What do you eat in the cafeteria?
我在食堂吃牛肉汉堡。Wǒ zài shítáng chī niúròu hànbǎo.I eat a beef burger in the cafeteria.
我要去美国留学。Wǒ yào qù Měiguó liúxué.I want to go to the U.S. to study abroad.
我要/想去德国留学。Wǒ yào/xiǎng qù Déguó liúxué.I want to study abroad in Germany.
我爱人来美国留学。Wǒ àiren lái Měiguó liúxué.My spouse came to the U.S. to study abroad.
我学数学。Wǒ xué shùxué.I study math.
我爱人也学数学。Wǒ àiren yě xué shùxué.My spouse also studies math.
我数学很不好。Wǒ shùxué hěn bù hǎo.I am not good at math. (Lit: My math is very not good.)
你大学的宿舍好吗？Nǐ dàxué de sùshè hǎo ma?Is your university dormitory good?
一般。Yìbān.It's average.
我在家和办公室办公。Wǒ zài jiā hé bàngōngshì bàngōng.I work from home and the office.
你去办公室吗？Nǐ qù bàngōngshì ma?Do you go to the office?
我两天在家办公，三天在办公室办公。Wǒ liǎng tiān zài jiā bàngōng, sān tiān zài bàngōngshì bàngōng.I work from home for two days and work in the office for three days.
你想在哪儿办公？Nǐ xiǎng zài nǎr bàngōng?Where would you like to work?
我想在家办公。Wǒ xiǎng zài jiā bàngōng.I would like to work from home.
