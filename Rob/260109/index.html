📚 Grammar
Grammar
上 + Noun (Institution)
Structure “To attend/Go to + Noun”: Used with institutions like school or work to mean “to attend” or “to go to”.
多 + Adj.
Interrogative “How + Adj.”: “多少” asks about quantity (How much/many?). “多 + Adjective” asks about degree (How + Adj.?).
挺 + Adj. + 的
Structure “Quite/Rather + Adj.”: Expresses a moderate degree, stronger than “还可以” but less than “很”. The “的” often softens the statement.
