📖 Vocabulary
穿 chuān to wear
舒服 shū fu comfortable
大小 dà xiǎo size
号 hào (shoe/clothing) size
合适 hé shì suitable; to fit
适合 shì hé to suit; to be suitable for
📚 Grammar
一双鞋 / 一只鞋 (measure words) a pair of shoes / one shoe
你有多少双鞋？ nǐ yǒu duō shǎo shuāng xié How many pairs of shoes do you have?
我有5双鞋。 wǒ yǒu wǔ shuāng xié I have five pairs of shoes.
我老婆给我买了很多双鞋。 wǒ lǎo po gěi wǒ mǎi le hěn duō shuāng xié My wife bought me many pairs of shoes.
穿 + 号：shoe size expressions for wearing + size
我在家穿UGGS的鞋。 wǒ zài jiā chuān UGGS de xié I wear UGGS shoes at home.
我在外面穿Skecher的鞋。 wǒ zài wài miàn chuān Skecher de xié I wear Skechers shoes outside.
你穿多大号的鞋？ nǐ chuān duō dà hào de xié What size shoes do you wear?
我穿9号的鞋。 wǒ chuān jiǔ hào de xié I wear size 9 shoes.
你的鞋是多大号的？ nǐ de xié shì duō dà hào de What size are your shoes?
我的鞋是9号的。 wǒ de xié shì jiǔ hào de My shoes are size 9.
合适 (adj.) suitable; fit
这个工作对我很合适。 zhè ge gōng zuò duì wǒ hěn hé shì This job is very suitable for me.
这双鞋的大小很合适。 zhè shuāng xié de dà xiǎo hěn hé shì The size of these shoes fits very well.
我和我老婆很合适。 wǒ hé wǒ lǎo po hěn hé shì My wife and I are a good match.
适合 (v.) to suit; to be suitable for
这个工作很适合你。 zhè ge gōng zuò hěn shì hé nǐ This job suits you very well.
她很适合你。 tā hěn shì hé nǐ She suits you very well.
你觉得你最适合做什么工作？ nǐ jué de nǐ zuì shì hé zuò shén me gōng zuò What job do you think suits you best?
我最适合做精算师。 wǒ zuì shì hé zuò jīng suàn shī I am best suited to be an actuary.
我现在的工作很适合我，但是我的公司不合适我。 wǒ xiàn zài de gōng zuò hěn shì hé wǒ, dàn shì wǒ de gōng sī bù hé shì wǒ My current job suits me well, but my company doesn't suit me.
