📚 Grammar
句子 Sentences
最好/好吃/漂亮/聪明/可爱zuì hǎo / hǎochī / piàoliang / cōngmíng / kě'àithe best / the most delicious / the most beautiful / the smartest / the cutest
我最爱吃生鱼片，生鱼片最好吃。Wǒ zuì ài chī shēngyúpiàn, shēngyúpiàn zuì hǎochī.I love eating sashimi the most, sashimi is the most delicious.
我最爱我爱人，我爱人最漂亮。Wǒ zuì ài wǒ àiren, wǒ àiren zuì piàoliang.I love my spouse the most, my spouse is the most beautiful.
孔子最聪明。Kǒngzǐ zuì cōngmíng.Confucius is the smartest.
这个多少钱一斤？Zhège duōshao qián yì jīn?How much is this per jin?
你最近身体好吗？Nǐ zuìjìn shēntǐ hǎo ma?How 有 your health been recently?
我身体很好。Wǒ shēntǐ hěn hǎo.I'm in good health.
A 比B adjA bǐ B adjA is more [adj.] than B.
B 没有A adjB méiyǒu A adjB is not as [adj.] as A.
生鱼片比章鱼好吃。Shēngyúpiàn bǐ zhāngyú hǎochī.Sashimi is more delicious than octopus.
我爱人比我漂亮。Wǒ àiren bǐ wǒ piàoliang.My spouse is more beautiful than me.
水比红酒好喝。Shuǐ bǐ hóngjiǔ hǎohē.Water is better to drink than red wine. (Subjective)
我比我妹妹有钱。Wǒ bǐ wǒ mèimei yǒu qián.I am richer than my younger sister.
我爱人的朋友比我有钱。Wǒ àiren de péngyou bǐ wǒ yǒu qián.My spouse's friend is richer than me.
你在学校的时候，成绩好吗？Nǐ zài xuéxiào de shíhou, chéngjì hǎo ma?When you were in school, were your grades good?
我成绩很好。Wǒ chéngjì hěn hǎo.My grades were very good.
我工作很努力。Wǒ gōngzuò hěn nǔlì.I work very hard.
