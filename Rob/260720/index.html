📖 Vocabulary
试 shì to try; to attempt
尝 cháng to taste
试吃 shì chī to sample (food); free tasting
有一点 yǒu yī diǎn a little bit; slightly
一点五 yī diǎn wǔ 1.5; one point five
挺 tǐng quite; rather (colloquial)
又 yòu again (past, already happened)
再 zài again (future, yet to happen)
📚 Grammar
试 / 尝 / 试吃 to try / to taste
我可以尝一下吗？ wǒ kě yǐ cháng yī xià ma Can I taste it?
我可以试一下吗？ wǒ kě yǐ shì yī xià ma Can I try it?
可以试吃吗？ kě yǐ shì chī ma Can I try a free sample?
有一点 + adj a little bit (adjective)
这双鞋有一点小。 zhè shuāng xié yǒu yī diǎn xiǎo These shoes are a little small.
今天有一点热。 jīn tiān yǒu yī diǎn rè It's a little hot today.
这个有一点贵。 zhè ge yǒu yī diǎn guì This is a little expensive.
A 比 B adj 很多 / 一点 comparison: A is much/a bit more adj than B
你比我高。 nǐ bǐ wǒ gāo You are taller than me.
BMW比雪佛兰贵很多。 BMW bǐ xuě fó lán guì hěn duō BMW is much more expensive than Chevrolet.
我们这儿的房子比夏威夷的房子大很多。 wǒ men zhèr de fáng zi bǐ xià wēi yí de fáng zi dà hěn duō Our houses here are much bigger than houses in Hawaii.
包子比饺子大一点。 bāo zi bǐ jiǎo zi dà yī diǎn Baozi are a bit bigger than jiaozi.
又 (past) vs 再 (future) again: past vs future
我昨天又打了匹克球。 wǒ zuó tiān yòu dǎ le pǐ kè qiú I played pickleball again yesterday.
我今天又吃了生鱼块。 wǒ jīn tiān yòu chī le shēng yú kuài I ate raw fish pieces again today.
你再试试25号半的。 nǐ zài shì shì èr shí wǔ hào bàn de Try on size 25.5 again.
大一点 / 小一点 a bit bigger / a bit smaller
这双鞋是多大号的？ zhè shuāng xié shì duō dà hào de What size are these shoes?
你有大一点的吗？ nǐ yǒu dà yī diǎn de ma Do you have a bigger size?
这双不大不小，挺合适。 zhè shuāng bù dà bù xiǎo, tǐng hé shì This pair is neither too big nor too small, quite suitable.
