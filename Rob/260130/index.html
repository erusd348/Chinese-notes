📚 Grammar
有的 + Noun
Means "some [noun]" when referring to an unspecified part of a group.
有的人喜欢吃肉，有的人不喜欢。Yǒude rén xǐhuan chī ròu, yǒude rén bù xǐhuan.Some people like to eat meat, others don't.
你什么时候来？Nǐ shénme shíhou lái?When are you coming?
💬 Sentences
一百万人的城市是一个小城市。Yī bǎi wàn rén de chéngshì shì yī gè xiǎo chéngshì.A city of one million people is a small city.
你住在哪个城市/国家？Nǐ zhù zài nǎge chéngshì/guójiā?Which city/country do you live in?
我住在印第安纳波利斯。Wǒ zhù zài Yìndì'ānnà Bōlìsī.I live in Indianapolis.
每个国家有每个国家的国歌。Měi gè guójiā yǒu měi gè guójiā de guógē.Every country 有 its own national anthem.
