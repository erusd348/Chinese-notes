📚 Grammar
又……又…… (structure) both ... and ...
又A又B both A and B
雪佛兰又便宜又好。 xuě fú lán yòu pián yi yòu hǎo The Chevrolet is both cheap and good.
生鱼块又好吃又便宜。 shēng yú kuài yòu hǎo chī yòu pián yi The sashimi is both delicious and cheap.
雪佛兰质量又好价格又便宜。 xuě fú lán zhì liàng yòu hǎo jià gé yòu pián yi The Chevrolet's quality is good and the price is cheap.
我老婆又聪明又漂亮，做饭又好吃。 wǒ lǎo pó yòu cōng ming yòu piào liang, zuò fàn yòu hǎo chī My wife is both smart and pretty, and she cooks well too.
我们的狗又小又白。 wǒ men de gǒu yòu xiǎo yòu bái Our dog is both small and white.
他又高又帅又有钱。 tā yòu gāo yòu shuài yòu yǒu qián He's both tall, handsome, and rich.
这辆车的样子又好看，价格又便宜。 zhè liàng chē de yàng zi yòu hǎo kàn, jià gé yòu pián yi This car's look is both nice and the price is cheap.
