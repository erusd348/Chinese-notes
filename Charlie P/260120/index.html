💬 Sentences
你刚起床吗？Nǐ gāng qǐchuáng ma?Did you just get up?
不是刚起床，只是没精神。Bù shì gāng qǐchuáng, zhǐ shì méi jīngshen.I didn't just get up, I just have no energy.
他是玩音乐的，他是DJ。Tā shì wán yīnyuè de, tā shì DJ.He is a musician, he is a DJ.
我觉得他们很快会长大。Wǒ juéde tāmen hěnkuài huì zhǎngdà.I think they will grow up quickly.
很多人是这样。Hěn duō rén shì zhèyàng.Many people are like this.
音乐是他们的生活。Yīnyuè shì tāmen de shēnghuó.Music is their life.
我们关系很好。Wǒmen guānxi hěn hǎo.Our relationship is very good.
我们喜欢不一样的音乐。Wǒmen xǐhuan bù yíyàng de yīnyuè.We like different music.
我们聊做音乐的事。Wǒmen liáo zuò yīnyuè de shì.We chat about making music.
我去过一些夜店。Wǒ qù guò yīxiē yèdiàn.I've been to some nightclubs.
山上的树叶都掉了。Shān shàng de shùyè dōu diào le.The leaves on the mountain have all fallen.
树上的苹果都红了。Shù shàng de píngguǒ dōu hóng le.The apples on the tree are all red now.
地上的草都黄了。Dì shàng de cǎo dōu huáng le.The grass on the ground has all turned yellow.
桌子上的菜凉了。Zhuōzi shàng de cài liáng le.The food on the table is cold now.
杯子里的水凉了。Bēizi lǐ de shuǐ liáng le.The water in the cup is cold now.
你很快会回家见亲戚的。Nǐ hěnkuài huì huí jiā jiàn qīnqi de.You will go home and see your relatives soon.
