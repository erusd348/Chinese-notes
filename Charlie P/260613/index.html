📚 Grammar
在 V (be doing) progressive action
他在穿衣服。 tā zài chuān yīfu. He is putting on clothes.
我在开门。 wǒ zài kāi mén. I am opening the door.
V着 (state/ongoing) sustained state
他穿着黑色的衣服。 tā chuānzhe hēisè de yīfu. He is wearing black clothes.
门开着。 mén kāizhe. The door is open.
电脑开着。 diànnǎo kāizhe. The computer is on.
在 place V着 V-ing at a place
他坐在椅子上。 tā zuò zài yǐzi shang. He is sitting on the chair.
他在椅子上坐着。 tā zài yǐzi shang zuòzhe. He is sitting on the chair.
他躺在床上。 tā tǎng zài chuáng shang. He is lying on the bed.
他在床上躺着。 tā zài chuáng shang tǎngzhe. He is lying on the bed.
V着 O (V + 着 + object) action while in a state
他看着电视。 tā kànzhe diànshì. He is watching TV.
他看着电视吃饭。 tā kànzhe diànshì chīfàn. He eats while watching TV.
他一边看电视一边吃饭。 tā yìbiān kàn diànshì yìbiān chīfàn. He watches TV while eating.
Place V着 O (existential) descriptive: place has object in position
书架上放着很多书。 shūjià shang fàngzhe hěn duō shū. Many books are placed on the shelf.
商店外面立着一个牌子。 shāngdiàn wàimiàn lìzhe yí ge páizi. A sign stands outside the shop.
商店里立着很多货架。 shāngdiàn lǐ lìzhe hěn duō huòjià. Many shelves stand in the shop.
Adj 地 V manner adverbial
他在床上舒服地躺着。 tā zài chuáng shang shūfu de tǎngzhe. He is comfortably lying on the bed.
他安静地看着书。 tā ānjìng de kànzhe shū. He is quietly reading.
他紧张地看足球比赛。 tā jǐnzhāng de kàn zúqiú bǐsài. He nervously watches the football match.
整齐 / 排队 neatness and lining up
排队排得很整齐。 páiduì pái de hěn zhěngqí. The line is very orderly.
他的牙很整齐。 tā de yá hěn zhěngqí. His teeth are very straight.
书架上整整齐齐摆着很多书。 shūjià shang zhěngzhěngqíqí bǎizhe hěn duō shū. Books are neatly arranged on the shelf.
各种各样 all kinds of
英国有各种各样的人。 Yīngguó yǒu gèzhǒng gèyàng de rén. Britain has all kinds of people.
商店里有各种各样的东西。 shāngdiàn lǐ yǒu gèzhǒng gèyàng de dōngxi. The shop has all kinds of things.
各个国家有各个国家的国歌。 gè ge guójiā yǒu gè ge guójiā de guógē. Each country has its own national anthem.
勿 V do not V (formal, = 不要)
