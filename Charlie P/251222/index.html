他们圣诞节工作吗？Tāmen Shèngdàn Jié gōngzuò ma?Do they work on Christmas?
他们24号关门。Tāmen èrshísì hào guānmén.They close (the shop) on the 24th.
他们的饭店开（张）了。Tāmen de fàndiàn kāi (zhāng) le.Their restaurant has opened (for business).
他住得很近。Tā zhù de hěn jìn.He lives very close.
我明天也去看他。Wǒ míngtiān yě qù kàn tā.I will go to visit him tomorrow too.
我买了一点/一些礼物。Wǒ mǎile yìdiǎn / yīxiē lǐwù.I bought a few / some gifts.
我不知道我想要什么。Wǒ bù zhīdào wǒ xiǎng yào shénme.I don't know what I want.
我喜欢给别人买东西，但是我不喜欢他们告诉我买什么。Wǒ xǐhuan gěi biérén mǎi dōngxi, dànshì wǒ bù xǐhuan tāmen gàosù wǒ mǎi shénme.I like buying things for others, but I don't like them telling me what to buy.
所以我也不想告诉他们给我买什么。Suǒyǐ wǒ yě bù xiǎng gàosù tāmen gěi wǒ mǎi shénme.So I also don't want to tell them what to buy for me.
这就是好玩的地方。Zhè jiùshì hǎowán de dìfang.This is the fun part.
Temu的东西很便宜，但是要很长时间。Temu de dōngxi hěn piányi, dànshì yào hěn cháng shíjiān.Temu's things are very cheap, but it takes a very long time (to arrive).
这些年我们很少走动。Zhèxiē nián wǒmen hěn shǎo zǒudòng.In recent years, we have rarely visited each other.
