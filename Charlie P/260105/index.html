📚 Grammar
💬 Sentences
要么... 要么...yàome... yàome...Structure "Either... or...": Used to present two alternatives, often implying limited choices.
又... 又...yòu... yòu...Structure "Both... and...": Used to state two qualities or actions simultaneously.
深圳的天气要么又热又湿，要么又热又干。Shēnzhèn de tiānqì yàome yòu rè yòu shī, yàome yòu rè yòu gān.Shenzhen's weather is either hot and humid, or hot and dry.
山上有很多树，树上有很多叶子。Shān shang yǒu hěn duō shù, shù shang yǒu hěn duō yèzi.There are many trees on the mountain, and there are many leaves on the trees.
秋天（来）了，天气凉了。Qiūtiān (lái) le, tiānqì liáng le.Autumn has arrived, the weather has turned cool.
