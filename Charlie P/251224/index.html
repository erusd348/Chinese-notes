📚 Grammar
句子 Sentences
他会给我发信息。Tā huì gěi wǒ fā xìnxī.He will send me a message.
他来了一下/一会儿。Tā láile yíxià / yíhuìr.He came for a little while.
我吃得很少。Wǒ chī de hěn shǎo.I eat very little.
我们在那儿呆到26号。Wǒmen zài nàr dāi dào èrshíliù hào.We stayed there until the 26th.
距离产生美。Jùlí chǎnshēng měi.Distance creates beauty. (Absence makes the heart grow fonder.)
我晚上有一个聚会。Wǒ wǎnshang yǒu yí gè jùhuì.I have a party/get-together tonight.
朋友们打算给他办一个生日聚会。Péngyoumen dǎsuàn gěi tā bàn yí gè shēngrì jùhuì.Friends are planning to organize/host a birthday party for him.
