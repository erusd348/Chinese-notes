💬 Sentences
要下雨了。yào xià yǔ le.It's going to rain.
我要睡觉了。wǒ yào shuì jiào le.I am going to sleep.
菜要来了。cài yào lái le.The dishes are coming.
我要结婚了。wǒ yào jié hūn le.I am going to get married.
我要去上班了。wǒ yào qù shàng bān le.I am going to work.
树上的叶子是树叶。shù shàng de yè zi shì shù yè.(Illustration) The leaves on the tree are leaves.
今天是晴天，不过很冷。jīn tiān shì qíng tiān, bú guò hěn lěng.It’s sunny today, but very cold.
天晴了。tiān qíng le.The sky has cleared up. / It's sunny now.
今天是阴天。jīn tiān shì yīn tiān.It’s overcast today.
天阴了。tiān yīn le.It has become overcast.
今天风很大/太大了。jīn tiān fēng hěn dà/tài dà le.The wind is strong/too strong today.
外面在刮大风。wài miàn zài guā dà fēng.It's blowing a strong wind outside.
外面在下大/小/毛毛雨。wài miàn zài xià dà/xiǎo/máo máo yǔ.It's raining heavily/lightly/drizzling outside.
今天下大/小雪了。jīn tiān xià dà/xiǎo xuě le.It’s snowing heavily/lightly today.
我结婚的那天，我们这儿下了一点雪。wǒ jié hūn de nà tiān, wǒ men zhèr xià le yī diǎn xuě.On the day I got married, we had a little snow here.
我身体好一点了。wǒ shēn tǐ hǎo yī diǎn le.I feel a bit better.
这个饭店有更多的shift。zhè ge fàn diàn yǒu gèng duō de shift.This restaurant has more shifts.
我不喜欢工作很晚。wǒ bù xǐ huān gōng zuò hěn wǎn.I don't like working late.
这个饭店只开3个月。zhè ge fàn diàn zhǐ kāi sān gè yuè.This restaurant is open for only 3 months.
没有什么特别的。méi yǒu shén me tè bié de.There's nothing special.
