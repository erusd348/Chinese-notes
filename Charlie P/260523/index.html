📚 Grammar
Sequence: V1 + 了 就 + V2
Once action1 completed, then action2
我吃了饭就去上班。wǒ chī le fàn jiù qù shàng bānI'll go to work after I eat.
Not yet: 还没 + V (+ 呢)
Action hasn't happened yet
我还没吃饭。wǒ hái méi chī fànI haven't eaten yet.
他还没来。tā hái méi láiHe hasn't arrived yet.
Already: 已经 + V
Action already completed
他已经走了。tā yǐ jīng zǒu leHe has already left.
State change: 要……了
About to happen
天要阴了。tiān yào yīn leIt's about to become overcast.
要下雨了。yào xià yǔ leIt's about to rain.
So + adj: 这么 + adjective
Indicating degree
这么巧。zhème qiǎoWhat a coincidence.
这么瘦。zhème shòuSo thin.
This way: 这么 + verb
Doing something this way
你不能这么做。nǐ bù néng zhème zuòYou can't do it this way.
可以这么说。kě yǐ zhème shuōYou can say it this way.
不可以这么说。bù kě yǐ zhème shuōYou can't say it this way.
都 = all / already
Expressing "all" with 都
桌子上的菜都凉了。zhuō zi shàng de cài dōu liáng leThe food on the table has gone cold.
地上的鞋都乱了。dì shàng de xié dōu luàn leThe shoes on the floor are all messy.
公交上的人都走了。gōng jiāo shàng de rén dōu zǒu leThe people on the bus have all left.
玩的时间少了。wán de shí jiān shǎo leThe time for playing has decreased.
