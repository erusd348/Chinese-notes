📚 Grammar
AABB形容词 (structure) reduplicated adjectives
干干净净的房间里放着一张床。 gàn gàn jìng jìng de fáng jiān lǐ fàng zhe yī zhāng chuáng In the spotlessly clean room, a bed is placed.
大大的电影院里坐着一排一排的人。 dà dà de diàn yǐng yuàn lǐ zuò zhe yī pái yī pái de rén In the big cinema, rows of people are sitting.
他矮矮的，胖胖的。 tā ǎi ǎi de, pàng pàng de He's short and chubby.
的 / 得 / 地 (structure) three de particles
漂亮的衣服 piào liang de yī fu beautiful clothes
开心的小朋友 kāi xīn de xiǎo péng yǒu happy children
我吃饭吃得很快。 wǒ chī fàn chī de hěn kuài I eat very fast.
他学习得非常努力。 tā xué xí de fēi cháng nǔ lì He studies extremely hard.
开心地唱歌 kāi xīn de chàng gē to sing happily
认真地学习 rèn zhēn de xué xí to study seriously
他非常努力地学习。 tā fēi cháng nǔ lì de xué xí He studies very diligently.
别/不要 V 着 (structure) don't V
别着急！ bié zháo jí Don't worry!
