📖 Vocabulary
期待 qī dài to look forward to; to expect
从来没 cóng lái méi have never (done something)
从来不 cóng lái bù never (do something, habitually)
后悔 hòu huǐ to regret
着急 zháo jí anxious; worried
排队 pái duì to queue; to line up
排 pái row (measure word)
座位 zuò wèi seat
选 xuǎn to choose; to select
选项 xuǎn xiàng option; choice
挑 tiāo to pick; picky
挑食 tiāo shí picky about food
📚 Grammar
从来没 + V + 过 have never done something before
我从来没喝过酒。 wǒ cóng lái méi hē guò jiǔ I've never drunk alcohol (before).
我从来没后悔买打折的东西。 wǒ cóng lái méi hòu huǐ mǎi dǎ zhé de dōng xi I've never regretted buying things on sale.
从来不 + V never do something (as a habit)
我从来不喝酒。 wǒ cóng lái bù hē jiǔ I never drink alcohol (as a rule).
我从来不后悔买打折的东西。 wǒ cóng lái bù hòu huǐ mǎi dǎ zhé de dōng xi I never regret buying things on sale.
着急 anxious; worried
我要迟到了，我很着急。 wǒ yào chí dào le, wǒ hěn zháo jí I'm going to be late, I'm very anxious.
如果你不着急，你可以等打折的时候买。 rú guǒ nǐ bù zháo jí, nǐ kě yǐ děng dǎ zhé de shí hou mǎi If you're not in a hurry, you can wait to buy when there's a sale.
排/座位/第几排 row / seat / which row
我们坐第几排？ wǒ men zuò dì jǐ pái Which row do we sit in?
我们的座位是几排几号？ wǒ men de zuò wèi shì jǐ pái jǐ hào What row and seat number are ours?
选/挑 to choose / picky
我们都想选最好的选项。 wǒ men dōu xiǎng xuǎn zuì hǎo de xuǎn xiàng We all want to choose the best option.
她很挑，所以她没结婚。 tā hěn tiāo, suǒ yǐ tā méi jié hūn She's very picky, that's why she's not married.
她很挑食。 tā hěn tiāo shí She's a picky eater.
