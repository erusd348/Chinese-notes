📚 Grammar
乱 + Verb 乱 (luàn) placed before a verb means to do something in a disorderly, chaotic, or indiscriminate manner.
坐 + Mode of Transport 坐 (zuò) is used for taking transportation where you sit inside (car, bus, train, plane, boat).
Location + 下 下 (xià) after a location noun means "below" or "at the base of".
快（要）……了 A structure placed before a verb or adjective to indicate that an action or state is about to happen soon.
Subject + 晚点了 Indicates that a scheduled transport (train, flight, etc.) is delayed.
我快迟到了，我要走快一点。 Wǒ kuài chídào le, wǒ yào zǒu kuài yīdiǎn. I'm almost late, I need to walk faster.
我晚了一点。 Wǒ wǎn le yīdiǎn. I am/was a little late.
不要乱吃东西！ Búyào luàn chī dōngxi! Don't eat things indiscriminately!

不要乱写乱画。 Búyào luàn xiě luàn huà. Don't scribble or draw randomly.
