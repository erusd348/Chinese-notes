📖 Vocabulary
散步 sànbù to take a walk / stroll
阿姨 āyí auntie (a respectful term for a middle-aged/elderly woman)
叔叔 shūshu uncle (a respectful term for a middle-aged/elderly man)
麻辣 málà numbing and spicy (a characteristic flavor of Sichuan cuisine)
香辣 xiānglà fragrant and spicy
稍等（一下） shāoděng (yíxià) wait a moment / hold on (a second)
稍微 shāowēi slightly / a little bit (adverb)
健康 jiànkāng healthy
油 yóu oily / greasy / (slang) slick, smooth-talking
盐 yán salt
糖 táng sugar
📚 Grammar
你最好…… You'd better... (used to give advice)
盐是咸的，糖是甜的。 Salt is salty, sugar is sweet. (的 structure to describe nature)
我们只去了一天。 Wǒmen zhǐ qùle yītiān. We only went for one day.
我女朋友还没回去。 Wǒ nǚ péngyou hái méi huíqù. My girlfriend hasn't gone back yet.
我去公园散步了。 Wǒ qù gōngyuán sànbù le. I went to the park for a walk.
你可以的！ Nǐ kěyǐ de! You can do it!
吃不到葡萄，说葡萄酸。 Chī bù dào pútáo, shuō pútáo suān. "Sour grapes" (Lit: Can't eat the grapes, so say they are sour.)
不见不散。 Bù jiàn bù sàn. Be there or be square. / I won't leave until we meet. (Lit: Won't disperse without seeing each other.)
这个面包好吃是好吃，不过太甜了。 Zhège miànbāo hǎochī shì hǎochī, bùguò tài tiánle. This bread is tasty, that's true, but it's too sweet.
苹果的手机好用是好用，不过太贵了。 Píngguǒ de shǒujī hǎoyòng shì hǎoyòng, bùguò tài guìle. Apple phones are easy to use, I admit, but they're too expensive.
这个东西便宜是便宜，不过质量不好。 Zhège dōngxī piányi shì piányi, bùguò zhìliàng bù hǎo. This thing is cheap, sure, but the quality is not good.
我的酸奶健康是健康，但是不太好吃。 Wǒ de suānnǎi jiànkāng shì jiànkāng, dànshì bù tài hǎochī. My yogurt is healthy, no doubt, but it doesn't taste very good.
他帅是帅，但是太矮了。 Tā shuài shì shuài, dànshì tài ǎile. He is handsome, I agree, but he's too short.
他很油。 Tā hěn yóu. He is very slick / smooth-talking. (or: He is very greasy/oily - depending on context)
💬 Sentences
我们是下午六点回来的。 We came back at 6 p.m. (This 是...的 structure emphasizes the time of return)
湖南菜又香又辣。 Hunan cuisine is both fragrant and spicy. (又...又... structure: both...and...)
