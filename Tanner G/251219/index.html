💬 Sentences
我回老家过圣诞节。 Wǒ huí lǎojiā guò Shèngdànjié. I went back to my hometown to celebrate Christmas.
开礼物。 Kāi lǐwù. To open gifts.
昨天我吃了酸奶，今天我又吃了酸奶，明天我还想再吃酸奶。 Zuótiān wǒ chīle suānnǎi, jīntiān wǒ yòu chīle suānnǎi, míngtiān wǒ hái xiǎng zài chī suānnǎi. I ate yogurt yesterday, I ate yogurt again today, and tomorrow I still want to eat yogurt again.
我最近去印度旅行了。 Wǒ zuìjìn qù Yìndù lǚxíngle. I traveled to India recently.
他命很苦。 Tā mìng hěn kǔ. He has had a very hard life. (Lit: His fate is very bitter.)
吃得苦中苦，方为人上人。 Chī dé kǔ zhōng kǔ, fāng wéi rén shàng rén. Only by enduring the bitterest of hardships can one rise above the common man. (Proverb about success through perseverance)
只要能吃苦，就有吃不完的苦。 Zhǐyào néng chīkǔ, jiù yǒu chī bù wán de kǔ. If you are only willing to endure hardships, there will be endless hardships for you to endure. (A modern, often cynical twist on the proverb)
酸奶健康是健康，就是不太好吃。 Suānnǎi jiànkāng shì jiànkāng, jiùshì bù tài hàochī. Yogurt is indeed healthy, it's just that it's not very tasty.
