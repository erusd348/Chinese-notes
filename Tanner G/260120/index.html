📚 Grammar
到B怎么坐车？ How do I take a bus/taxi to get to B?
先...，再/然后... (Structure) First..., then... (describing sequence of actions)
先坐1路公交，在B站换2路公交。 First take bus No. 1, then transfer to bus No. 2 at B stop.
我感冒好了。 Wǒ gǎnmào hǎo le. My cold is better/I've recovered from the cold.
我读完了我的小说。 Wǒ dú wán le wǒ de xiǎoshuō. I finished reading my novel.
我什么都没做。 Wǒ shénme dōu méi zuò. I didn't do anything at all.
她三天前回去了。 Tā sān tiān qián huí qù le. She went back three days ago.
他二十多岁。 Tā èrshí duō suì. He is in his twenties. (More than 20 years old)
这本书一百多块。 Zhè běn shū yìbǎi duō kuài. This book costs over one hundred yuan.
二十块多。 Èrshí kuài duō. A little more than twenty yuan (e.g., 20.5).
三十五块多。 Sānshíwǔ kuài duō. A little more than thirty-five yuan.
她今年五六岁。 Tā jīnnián wǔ-liù suì. She is about five or six years old this year.
二十五六岁。 Èrshí wǔ-liù suì. About twenty-five or twenty-six years old.
五六十岁。 Wǔ-liù shí suì. About fifty or sixty years old.
你家离公司有多远？ Nǐ jiā lí gōngsī yǒu duō yuǎn? How far is your home from the company?
我家离公司有1公里。 Wǒ jiā lí gōngsī yǒu yī gōnglǐ. My home is 1 kilometer from the company.
从我家到公司有1公里。 Cóng wǒ jiā dào gōngsī yǒu yī gōnglǐ. From my home to the company is 1 kilometer.
我要租房。 Wǒ yào zū fáng. I want to rent an apartment/house.
我要开车去我女朋友家。 Wǒ yào kāichē qù wǒ nǚ péngyou jiā. I want to drive to my girlfriend's house.
我们过马路的时候要往左右看。 Wǒmen guò mǎlù de shíhòu yào wǎng zuǒyòu kàn. When we cross the road, we need to look left and right.
