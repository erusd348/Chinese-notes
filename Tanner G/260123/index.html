📚 Grammar
如果...，就/会... (Structure) If..., then...
对我来说很少见。 Duì wǒ láishuō hěn shǎojiàn. It's very rare for me.
在有的国家不可以买褪黑素。 Zài yǒude guójiā bù kěyǐ mǎi tuìhēisù. In some countries, you cannot buy melatonin.
她不能早（一）点打电话，因为她有事。 Tā bù néng zǎo (yī) diǎn dǎ diànhuà, yīnwèi tā yǒu shì. She can't call earlier because she has something going on.
我小的时候骑过（马）。 Wǒ xiǎo de shíhòu qíguo (mǎ). I rode (a horse) when I was little.
我家有一个农场，农场里有几匹马，我小的时候骑过。 Wǒ jiā yǒu yī gè nóngchǎng, nóngchǎng lǐ yǒu jǐ pǐ mǎ, wǒ xiǎo de shíhòu qíguo. My family has a farm. There are several horses on the farm. I rode them when I was little.
如果我骑电动车，我会出事。 Rúguǒ wǒ qí diàndòngchē, wǒ huì chūshì. If I ride an e-bike, I will have an accident.
他编程技术不好。 Tā biānchéng jìshù bù hǎo. His programming skill is not good.
出事了！ Chū shì le! Something happened! / There's been an accident!
