📖 Vocabulary
结婚 jié hūn to get married
打折 dǎ zhé to have a discount; on sale (verb)
打九折 dǎ jiǔ zhé 10% off; pay 90%
七五折 qī wǔ zhé 25% off; pay 75%
折扣 zhé kòu discount (noun)
扣钱 kòu qián to deduct money
迟到 chí dào to be late
少 shǎo less; to be missing; to lack
便宜 pián yi cheap; to be cheaper
立省 lì shěng to instantly save
百分百 bǎi fēn bǎi one hundred percent
📚 Grammar
打折/折扣 discounts and sales
这个手机打折吗？ zhè ge shǒu jī dǎ zhé ma Is this phone on sale?
这个有折扣吗？ zhè ge yǒu zhé kòu ma Does this have a discount?
这个手机打九折。 zhè ge shǒu jī dǎ jiǔ zhé This phone is 10% off (pay 90%).
那个是七五折。 nà ge shì qī wǔ zhé That one is 25% off (pay 75%).
便宜/少了 + Num + 块 (了 change of state) price reduction with 了
这个手机便宜了500块。 zhè ge shǒu jī pián yi le wǔ bǎi kuài This phone is 500 kuai cheaper (than before).
这个手机少了500块。 zhè ge shǒu jī shǎo le wǔ bǎi kuài This phone is 500 kuai less (than before).
不X，不Y（如果不X，就不Y） If not X, then not Y (conditional)
不打折，我不买。 bù dǎ zhé, wǒ bù mǎi If it's not on sale, I won't buy it.
不打折的东西，我不会买。 bù dǎ zhé de dōng xi, wǒ bù huì mǎi If something isn't on sale, I won't buy it.
要 + verb（必须/需要） must / need to
迟到要扣钱。 chí dào yào kòu qián Being late will result in a pay deduction.
你结婚的时候要请我。 nǐ jié hūn de shí hou yào qǐng wǒ You have to invite me when you get married.
你会买打折的东西吗？ nǐ huì mǎi dǎ zhé de dōng xi ma Do you buy things when they're on sale?
不买立省百分百。 bù mǎi lì shěng bǎi fēn bǎi Not buying saves you 100%. (meme)
打折的时候，很多人会买很多不需要的东西。 dǎ zhé de shí hou, hěn duō rén huì mǎi hěn duō bù xū yào de dōng xi When there's a sale, many people buy a lot of things they don't need.
这件衣服原价一千，打七五折以后是七百五。 zhè jiàn yī fu yuán jià yī qiān, dǎ qī wǔ zhé yǐ hòu shì qī bǎi wǔ This piece of clothing was originally 1000, after 25% off it's 750.
我从来没买过打折后后悔的东西。 wǒ cóng lái méi mǎi guò dǎ zhé hòu hòu huǐ de dōng xi I've never bought something on sale that I later regretted.
这个手机的折扣比那个大。 zhè ge shǒu jī de zhé kòu bǐ nà ge dà The discount on this phone is bigger than that one.
如果不着急的话，最好等到打折再买。 rú guǒ bù zháo jí de huà, zuì hǎo děng dào dǎ zhé zài mǎi If you're not in a hurry, it's best to wait until there's a sale.
