比较好/好吃/可爱/聪明bǐjiào hǎo/hǎochī/kě'ài/cōngmíngrelatively quite good/delicious/cute/smart
A 比 B 好。A bǐ B hǎo.A is better than B.
中国菜比美国菜好吃。Zhōngguó cài bǐ Měiguó cài hǎochī.Chinese food is more delicious than American food.
茶比牛奶好喝。Chá bǐ niúnǎi hǎo hē.Tea is tastier (to drink) than milk.
Coby 比 Tom 可爱。Coby bǐ Tom kě'ài.Coby is cuter than Tom.
Tom 比 Coby 聪明。Tom bǐ Coby cōngmíng.Tom is smarter than Coby.
我爱人比他爱人漂亮。Wǒ àiren bǐ tā àiren piàoliang.My spouse is more beautiful/handsome than his/her spouse.
B 没有 A 好。B méiyǒu A hǎo.B is not as good as A.
美国菜没有中国菜好吃。Měiguó cài méiyǒu Zhōngguó cài hǎochī.American food is not as delicious as Chinese food.
牛奶没有茶好喝。Niúnǎi méiyǒu chá hǎohē.Milk is not as good (to drink) as tea.
📚 Grammar
A 比 B + Adjective
A is more [adjective] than B.
B 没有 A + Adjective
B is not as [adjective] as A.
