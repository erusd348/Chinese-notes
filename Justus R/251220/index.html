📚 Grammar
Expressing Fullness
我吃饱了。Wǒ chībǎo le.I'm full. (Lit: I have eaten to fullness.)
我很饱。Wǒ hěn bǎo.I'm very full.
Describing Existence (有/没有)
在游轮上，很多吃的不要钱。Zài yóulún shàng, hěn duō chī de bú yào qián.On the cruise ship, a lot of the food is free.
我们学校里有很多学生。Wǒmen xuéxiào lǐ yǒu hěn duō xuéshēng.There are many students in our school.
办公室里没有很多人。bàngōngshì lǐ méiyǒu hěn duō rén.There aren't many people in the office.
游轮上有很多吃的。Yóulún shàng yǒu hěn duō chī de.There is a lot of food on the cruise ship.
Asking about & Evaluating Work Ethic
你们学校同事工作努力吗？Nǐmen xuéxiào tóngshì gōngzuò nǔlì ma?Do the colleagues at your school work hard?
他们工作比较努力。Tāmen gōngzuò bǐjiào nǔlì.They work quite hard.
Expressing Extreme Degree & Contrast (太...了 / 不太)
这个火鸡太好吃了。Zhège huǒjī tài hàochī le.This turkey is extremely delicious.
他们工作太努力了。Tāmen gōngzuò tài nǔlì le.They work too hard.
Tom太聪明了，Coby不太聪明。Tom tài cōngmíng le, Coby bú tài cōngmíng.Tom is super smart, but Coby is not very smart.
Describing Distance (A 离 B + Adv. + Adj.)
我家离办公室很近。Wǒ jiā lí bàngōngshì hěn jìn.My home is very close to the office.
你家离学校远吗？Nǐ jiā lí xuéxiào yuǎn ma?Is your home far from the school?
我家离学校比较远。Wǒ jiā lí xuéxiào bǐjiào yuǎn.My home is quite far from the school.
我家离我爸妈家远极了。Wǒ jiā lí wǒ bà mā jiā yuǎn jíle.My home is extremely far from my parents' home.
Asking about Size
你的宿舍大不大？Nǐ de sùshè dà bù dà?Is your dorm room big or not? (Is your dorm big?)
