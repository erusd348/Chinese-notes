📚 Grammar
句子 Sentences
你没去你爸妈家吗？Nǐ méi qù nǐ bàmā jiā ma?Didn't you go to your parents' house?
我和我爱人还有两条狗在家。Wǒ hé wǒ àiren hái yǒu liǎng tiáo gǒu zài jiā.My spouse, our two dogs, and I are at home.
我们在家吃吃喝喝。Wǒmen zài jiā chīchīhēhē.We are at home eating and drinking (having a good time).
给 sb do sthgěi sb do sthDo sth for sb
你给你爱人买礼物了吗？Nǐ gěi nǐ àiren mǎi lǐwù le ma?Did you buy a gift for your spouse?
我没给她买礼物。Wǒ méi gěi tā mǎi lǐwù.I didn't buy a gift for her.
我爱人就是我同屋/室友。Wǒ àiren jiùshì wǒ tóngwū/shìyǒu.My spouse is exactly my roommate. (Humor/slang for "we live together")
A 离B adjA lí B adjA is (adj.) far/close from B.
我家离我办公室很近。Wǒ jiā lí wǒ bàngōngshì hěn jìn.My home is very close to my office.
多 vs. 少duō vs. shǎomany/much vs. few/little
学校里有很多人。Xuéxiào lǐ yǒu hěn duō rén.There are many people in the school.
学校里人很多。Xuéxiào lǐ rén hěn duō.The people in the school are many.
教室里有很多学生。Jiàoshì lǐ yǒu hěn duō xuésheng.There are many students in the classroom.
教室里学生很多。Jiàoshì lǐ xuésheng hěn duō.The students in the classroom are many.
图书馆里有很多书。Túshūguǎn lǐ yǒu hěn duō shū.There are many books in the library.
图书馆里书很多。Túshūguǎn lǐ shū hěn duō.The books in the library are many.
他有很多钱。Tā yǒu hěn duō qián.He 有 a lot of money.
他钱很多。Tā qián hěn duō.He 有 a lot of money. (More colloquial)
