💬 Sentences
你周末去哪儿了？Nǐ zhōumò qù nǎr le?Where did you go over the weekend?
我去沙滩露营了。Wǒ qù shātān lùyíng le.I went camping at the beach.
你一个人吗？Nǐ yī gè rén ma?Did you go alone?
我和我的朋友。Wǒ hé wǒ de péngyou.I went with my friend.
你爱人没去吗？Nǐ àirén méi qù ma?Didn’t your spouse go?
我爱人不喜欢露营。Wǒ àirén bù xǐhuan lùyíng.My spouse doesn’t like camping.
露营好玩吗？Lùyíng hǎowán ma?Is camping fun?
露营很好玩。Lùyíng hěn hǎowán.Camping is very fun.
