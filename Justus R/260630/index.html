📚 Grammar
想当 to want to be; to want to work as
我想当医生。 wǒ xiǎng dāng yī shēng I want to be a doctor.
当 to work as
她在学校当老师。 tā zài xué xiào dāng lǎo shī She works as a teacher at school.
我在学校当程序员。 wǒ zài xué xiào dāng chéng xù yuán I work as a programmer at school.
几口人 how many people (in family)
你家有几口人？ nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ kǒu rén How many people are in your family?
我家有三口人。 wǒ jiā yǒu sān kǒu rén My family has three people.
你做什么工作？ nǐ zuò shén me gōng zuò What do you do for work?
我是程序员。 wǒ shì chéng xù yuán I am a programmer.
