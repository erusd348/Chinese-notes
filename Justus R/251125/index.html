📚 Grammar
A 比 B + [Adj.]
A is more [Adj.] than B.
我学习比他努力。Wǒ xuéxí bǐ tā nǔlì.I study harder than him.
我成绩比他好。Wǒ chéngjì bǐ tā hǎo.My grades are better than his.
B 没有 A + [Adj.]
B is not as [Adj.] as A.
太 [Adj.] 了！tài [Adj.] le!So [Adj.]! / Too [Adj.]!
不太 [Adj.]bù tài [Adj.]Not very [Adj.]
Existence Sentences: Place + 有 / 没有 + 东西
[Place] 有 [Thing][Place] yǒu [Thing]There is [Thing] at/in [Place].
书店里有很多书。Shūdiàn li yǒu hěn duō shū.There are many books in the bookstore.
我学校里有很多学生。Wǒ xuéxiào li yǒu hěn duō xuéshēng.There are many students in my school.
商店里有很多可乐。Shāngdiàn li yǒu hěn duō kělè.There is a lot of cola in the store.
我家里有两只狗。Wǒ jiā li yǒu liǎng zhī gǒu.There are two dogs in my home.
[Place] 没有 [Thing][Place] méiyǒu [Thing]There is no [Thing] at/in [Place].
图书馆里没有人。Túshūguǎn li méiyǒu rén.There is no one in the library.
这个饭店没有包子。Zhège fàndiàn méiyǒu bāozi.This restaurant doesn't have baozi (steamed buns).
