这个 vs. 那个 vs. 哪个zhège vs. nàge vs. nǎgethis vs. that vs. which
这儿 vs. 那儿 vs. 哪儿zhèr vs. nàr vs. nǎrhere vs. there vs. where
哪个是Tom？Nǎge shì Tom?Which one is Tom?
那个是Tom。Nàge shì Tom.That is Tom.
图书馆在哪儿？Túshūguǎn zài nǎr?Where is the library?
图书馆在那儿。Túshūguǎn zài nàr.The library is there.
你是哪国人？Nǐ shì nǎ guó rén?What is your nationality?
你是哪儿人？Nǐ shì nǎr rén?Where are you from?
我是美国人。Wǒ shì Měiguó rén.I am American.
俄罗斯是最大的国家。Éluósī shì zuì dà de guójiā.Russia is the largest country.
你叫什么（名字）？Nǐ jiào shénme (míngzi)?What is your name?
我叫Tom。Wǒ jiào Tom.My name is Tom.
孔子，名：丘，字：仲尼。Kǒngzǐ, míng: Qiū, zì: Zhòngní.Confucius, given name: Qiu, courtesy name: Zhongni.
我妈妈叫我吃饭/睡觉。Wǒ māma jiào wǒ chīfàn/shuìjiào.My mom told me to eat/sleep.
我爱人叫我喂狗。Wǒ àirén jiào wǒ wèi gǒu.My spouse told me to feed the dog.
我的狗在叫。Wǒ de gǒu zài jiào.My dog is barking.
不要叫！Bùyào jiào!Don't shout!/Don't bark!
