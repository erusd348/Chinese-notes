Adj. 不 Adj.Adj. bù Adj.Yes-No Question Form for Adjectives
聪不聪明？Cōng bu cōngming?Is (he/she) smart or not?
好不好吃？Hǎo bu hǎo chī?Is it delicious or not? / How does it taste?
好不好？Hǎo bu hǎo?Is it good or not? / Okay?
V. 不 V.V. bù V.Yes-No Question Form for Verbs
吃不吃？Chī bu chī?Will you eat (it) or not? / Do you want to eat?
知不知道？Zhī bu zhīdào?Do (you) know or not?
V. 没 V.V. méi V.Yes-No Question Form for Past Actions
吃没吃？Chī méi chī?Have (you) eaten or not?
去没去？Qù méi qù?Did (you) go or not?
有没有水？Yǒu méiyǒu shuǐ?Is there water? / Do you have water?
有没有问题？Yǒu méiyǒu wèntí?Are there any problems? / Any questions?
V. 的V. deNominalizer "The thing that is V.-ed"
吃的 / 喝的chī de / hē dethings to eat / things to drink
我的压力很大。Wǒ de yālì hěn dà.My stress is very high. / I'm under a lot of pressure.
