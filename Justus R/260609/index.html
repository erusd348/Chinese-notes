是你做的吗？ shì nǐ zuò de ma? Was it you who made it?
不是我做的，是我买的。 bú shì wǒ zuò de, shì wǒ mǎi de. I didn't make it, I bought it.
你为什么买巧克力？ nǐ wèi shén me mǎi qiǎo kè lì? Why did you buy chocolate?
因为我女朋友喜欢吃巧克力。 yīn wèi wǒ nǚ péng you xǐ huān chī qiǎo kè lì. Because my girlfriend likes to eat chocolate.
