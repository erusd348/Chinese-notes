📖 Vocabulary
给 gěi to give; for (someone)
公司 gōng sī company
职员 zhí yuán office worker
工人 gōng rén worker; blue collar worker
员工 yuán gōng employee
记者 jì zhě journalist
名片 míng piàn business card
医院 yī yuàn hospital
医生 yī shēng doctor
📚 Grammar
给 / 做 (structure) give / do
我妈妈给我做面条。 wǒ mā ma gěi wǒ zuò miàn tiáo My mom makes noodles for me.
你做什么工作？ nǐ zuò shén me gōng zuò What work do you do?
你是做什么工作的？ nǐ shì zuò shén me gōng zuò de What kind of work do you do?
温度 / 公司 temperature / company
你那儿多少度？ nǐ nàr duō shao dù How many degrees is it over there?
我这儿39度。 wǒ zhèr sān shí jiǔ dù It's 39 degrees over here.
我们不是公司职员。 wǒ men bù shì gōng sī zhí yuán We are not office workers.
这是我的名片。 zhè shì wǒ de míng piàn This is my business card.
💬 课文对话 lesson dialogue
你父亲在哪儿工作？ nǐ fù qīn zài nǎr gōng zuò Where does your father work?
他在医院工作，他是医生。 tā zài yī yuàn gōng zuò, tā shì yī shēng He works at a hospital, he is a doctor.
你母亲呢？ nǐ mǔ qīn ne How about your mother?
她不工作。 tā bù gōng zuò She doesn't work.
你的两个哥哥做什么工作？ nǐ de liǎng ge gē ge zuò shén me gōng zuò What work do your two older brothers do?
他们都是公司职员。你做什么工作？ tā men dōu shì gōng sī zhí yuán. nǐ zuò shén me gōng zuò They are both office workers. What work do you do?
我是记者，这是我的名片。 wǒ shì jì zhě, zhè shì wǒ de míng piàn I'm a journalist, this is my business card.
