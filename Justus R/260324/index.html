📚 Grammar
💬 Sentences
在 V. = be doingzài V.be doing (action in progress)
你在做什么？Nǐ zài zuò shénme?What are you doing?
我在学习。Wǒ zài xuéxí.I am studying.
有的 (some) 时候 (time)yǒude shíhousometimes
有的人喜欢狗，有的人喜欢猫。Yǒude rén xǐhuan gǒu, yǒude rén xǐhuan māo.Some people like dogs, some people like cats.
有的狗可爱，有的狗不可爱。Yǒude gǒu kě'ài, yǒude gǒu bù kě'ài.Some dogs are cute, some dogs are not cute.
我买了几个苹果。Wǒ mǎile jǐ gè píngguǒ.I bought a few apples.
我认识了几个新朋友。Wǒ rènshìle jǐ gè xīn péngyou.I met a few new friends.
你什么时候吃早饭？Nǐ shénme shíhou chī zǎofàn?When do you eat breakfast?
我8点吃早饭。Wǒ bā diǎn chī zǎofàn.I eat breakfast at 8 o'clock.
你什么时候工作？Nǐ shénme shíhou gōngzuò?When do you work?
我上午8点到下午5点工作。Wǒ shàngwǔ bā diǎn dào xiàwǔ wǔ diǎn gōngzuò.I work from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.
……的时候 (when...)...de shíhouwhen...
我工作的时候，我很累。Wǒ gōngzuò de shíhou, wǒ hěn lèi.When I work, I am very tired.
我在学校的时候，我学习很努力。Wǒ zài xuéxiào de shíhou, wǒ xuéxí hěn nǔlì.When I was at school, I studied very hard.
我在大学的时候，我有很多朋友。Wǒ zài dàxué de shíhou, wǒ yǒu hěn duō péngyou.When I was in college/university, I had many friends.
我认识我爱人的时候，我37。Wǒ rènshì wǒ àirén de shíhou, wǒ sānshíqī.When I met my spouse, I was 37.
