📖 Vocabulary
你的麦片多少钱?Nǐ de màipiàn duōshǎo qián?How much is your cereal?
📚 Grammar
在 + V = be doing
Indicates an ongoing action (e.g., 在吃 = is eating)
我在吃早饭。Wǒ zài chī zǎofàn.I am eating breakfast.
你爱吃麦片吗？Nǐ ài chī màipiàn ma?Do you like eating cereal?
还可以。Hái kěyǐ.It's okay. / Not bad.
麦片好吃吗？Màipiàn hǎochī ma?Is the cereal tasty?
很好吃。Hěn hǎochī.It's very tasty.
你在哪儿买麦片？Nǐ zài nǎr mǎi màipiàn?Where do you buy cereal?
我在商店买麦片。Wǒ zài shāngdiàn mǎi màipiàn.I buy cereal at the store.
你今天去学校办公吗？Nǐ jīntiān qù xuéxiào bàngōng ma?Are you going to the school office today?
她今天也不去学校办公。Tā jīntiān yě bù qù xuéxiào bàngōng.She is also not going to the school office today.
你的朋友很多。Nǐ de péngyou hěn duō.You have many friends.
你有很多朋友。Nǐ yǒu hěn duō péngyou.You have a lot of friends.
你是一个好人。Nǐ shì yī gè hǎorén.You are a good person.
