📚 Grammar
在 + v.
(Structure) To be doing something (indicates ongoing action)
我在热包子。Wǒ zài rè bāozi.I am heating up the buns.
我晚上吃了鸡肉，沙拉和巧克力。Wǒ wǎnshang chī le jīròu, shālā hé qiǎokèlì.I ate chicken, salad, and chocolate in the evening.
A 离 B + 远/近
(Structure) A is far/near from B.
我家离公司很近。Wǒ jiā lí gōngsī hěn jìn.My home is close to the company.
学校离这里很远。Xuéxiào lí zhèlǐ hěn yuǎn.The school is far from here.
我的头很大。Wǒ de tóu hěn dà.My head is very big.
他的毛很多。Tā de máo hěn duō.He 有 a lot of fur/body hair.
他的毛发很茂盛。Tā de máofà hěn màoshèng.His hair is very thick.
你的头发很多。Nǐ de tóufa hěn duō.You have a lot of hair.
她的头发很少。Tā de tóufa hěn shǎo.She 有 very little hair.
他的头发很长。Tā de tóufa hěn cháng.He 有 very long hair.
我的头发很短。Wǒ de tóufa hěn duǎn.I have very short hair.
