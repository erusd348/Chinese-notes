Adj. 不 Adj.Adj. bù Adj.A-not-A Question Form (Adjective): Repeating the adjective to form a yes-no question about quality or state.
新年快乐/好！Xīnnián kuàilè/hǎo!Happy New Year!
你怎么过新年？Nǐ zěnme guò xīnnián?How do you celebrate the New Year?
我们吃了火锅，玩了桌游。Wǒmen chī le huǒguō, wán le zhuōyóu.We ate hot pot and played board games.
我们去了邻居家。Wǒmen qù le línjū jiā.We went to the neighbor's house.
他们请你们吃饭吗？Tāmen qǐng nǐmen chīfàn ma?Did they invite you to eat?

火锅好不好吃？Huǒguō hǎo bu hǎochī?Is hot pot delicious?
你吃不吃火锅？Nǐ chī bu chī huǒguō?Do you eat hot pot?
你玩不玩桌游？Nǐ wán bu wán zhuōyóu?Do you play board games?
你做不做饭？Nǐ zuò bu zuò fàn?Do you cook?
你工不工作？Nǐ gōng bu gōngzuò?Do you work?
你吃没吃？Nǐ chī méi chī?Did you eat (it)?
你玩没玩？Nǐ wán méi wán?Did you play (it)?
不太聪明。Bú tài cōngming.Not very smart.
不太好吃。Bú tài hǎochī.Not very delicious.
太聪明了。Tài cōngming le.Too smart.
太好吃了。Tài hǎochī le.Too delicious.

图书馆里有很多书。Túshūguǎn li yǒu hěn duō shū.There are many books in the library.
