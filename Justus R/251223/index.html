你们还没放假吗？Nǐmen hái méi fàngjià ma?You haven't started your holiday yet?
你们什么时候放假？Nǐmen shénme shíhou fàngjià?When do you start your holiday?
我们星期三放假。Wǒmen xīngqīsān fàngjià.We start our holiday on Wednesday.

我没有室友。Wǒ méiyǒu shìyǒu.I didn't have a roommate.
