📚 Grammar
的（领属） (structure) possessive
我卖了我的房子。 wǒ mài le wǒ de fáng zi I sold my house.
她没卖她的房子。 tā méi mài tā de fáng zi She didn't sell her house.
你们住的房子是谁的？ nǐ men zhù de fáng zi shì shuí de Whose house is the one you live in?
我们住的房子是我太太的。 wǒ men zhù de fáng zi shì wǒ tài tai de The house we live in is my wife's.
我父亲是牧师。 wǒ fù qīn shì mù shī My father is a pastor.
我母亲是老师。 wǒ mǔ qīn shì lǎo shī My mother is a teacher.
我小时候想当医生。 wǒ xiǎo shí hou xiǎng dāng yī shēng When I was young, I wanted to be a doctor.
在美国，医生很有钱。 zài měi guó, yī shēng hěn yǒu qián In the US, doctors are very rich.
我的二弟弟是男护士。 wǒ de èr dì di shì nán hù shi My second younger brother is a male nurse.
我的大弟弟是AI工程师。 wǒ de dà dì di shì AI gōng chéng shī My older younger brother is an AI engineer.
我小弟弟是焊工。 wǒ xiǎo dì di shì hàn gōng My youngest younger brother is a welder.
