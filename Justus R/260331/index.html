你在做什么？Nǐ zài zuò shénme?What are you doing?
我在学习汉语。Wǒ zài xuéxí Hànyǔ.I am studying Chinese.
我在吃饭。Wǒ zài chīfàn.I am eating.
我在工作。Wǒ zài gōngzuò.I am working.
有的人喜欢狗，有的人喜欢啤酒。Yǒude rén xǐhuan gǒu, yǒude rén xǐhuan píjiǔ.Some people like dogs, some people like beer.
有的狗很可爱，有的狗不可爱。Yǒude gǒu hěn kě'ài, yǒude gǒu bù kě'ài.Some dogs are cute, some dogs are not cute.
有的猫很听话，有的猫不听话。Yǒude māo hěn tīnghuà, yǒude māo bù tīnghuà.Some cats are obedient, some cats are not obedient.
真听话！Zhēn tīnghuà!Really obedient!
你什么时候吃早饭？Nǐ shénme shíhòu chī zǎofàn?When do you eat breakfast?
我8点吃早饭。Wǒ bā diǎn chī zǎofàn.I eat breakfast at 8 o’clock.
你什么时候工作？Nǐ shénme shíhòu gōngzuò?When do you work?
我8点到5点工作。Wǒ bā diǎn dào wǔ diǎn gōngzuò.I work from 8 to 5.
我小的时候，我喜欢冰淇淋。Wǒ xiǎo de shíhòu, wǒ xǐhuan bīngqílín.When I was little, I liked ice cream.
我在大学的时候，我学习IT。Wǒ zài dàxué de shíhòu, wǒ xuéxí IT.When I was in university, I studied IT.
我在中国的时候，我吃了很多好吃的（东西）。Wǒ zài Zhōngguó de shíhòu, wǒ chīle hěnduō hǎochī de (dōngxi).When I was in China, I ate a lot of delicious food.
我常常在家休息。Wǒ chángcháng zài jiā xiūxi.I often rest at home.
我常常早上吃麦片。Wǒ chángcháng zǎoshang chī màipiàn.I often eat cereal in the morning.
我常常8点工作。Wǒ chángcháng bā diǎn gōngzuò.I often work at 8 o’clock.
A 跟/和 B 一起 V.A gēn/hé B yīqǐ V.A do something together with B.
我跟我爱人一起吃饭。Wǒ gēn wǒ àirén yīqǐ chīfàn.I eat together with my spouse.
我跟超力一起学习汉语。Wǒ gēn Chāolì yīqǐ xuéxí Hànyǔ.I study Chinese together with Chaoli.
我跟Tom一起玩。Wǒ gēn Tom yīqǐ wán.I play together with Tom.
