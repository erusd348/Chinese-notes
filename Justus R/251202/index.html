📚 Grammar
Existential: Place + 有 / 没有 + Object
我家有两个人。Wǒ jiā yǒu liǎng gè rén.There are two people in my family.
我办公室（里）有8个人。Wǒ bàngōngshì (lǐ) yǒu bā gè rén.There are 8 people in my office.
银行里有很多钱。Yínháng lǐ yǒu hěn duō qián.There is a lot of money in the bank.
饭店里有吃的。Fàndiàn lǐ yǒu chī de.There is food in the restaurant. / The restaurant 有 food.
我学校没有食堂，也没有宿舍。Wǒ xuéxiào méiyǒu shítáng, yě méiyǒu sùshè.My school doesn't have a cafeteria, and also doesn't have a dormitory.
Distance: A 离 B + Adv. + Adj. (近/远)
我家离学校比较远。Wǒ jiā lí xuéxiào bǐjiào yuǎn.My home is quite far from school.
中国离美国非常远。Zhōngguó lí Měiguó fēicháng yuǎn.China is very far from America.
我家离我爸妈家非常远。Wǒ jiā lí wǒ bà mā jiā fēicháng yuǎn.My home is very far from my parents' home.
Other Sentences
