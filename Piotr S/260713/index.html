📖 Vocabulary
个 gè (general measure word)
杯 bēi cup; glass (measure word)
瓶 píng bottle (measure word)
碗 wǎn bowl (measure word)
水 shuǐ water
茶 chá tea
客 kè guest; customer
位 wèi (polite measure word for people)
几 jǐ how many (for small numbers, usually <10)
扫 sǎo to scan
码 mǎ code (QR code)
点单 diǎn dān to order (food)
买单 mǎi dān to pay the bill
分 fēn to divide; to split
付 fù to pay
可以 kě yǐ can; okay; may
📚 Grammar
量词：个 / 杯 / 瓶 / 碗 measure words
一个人 yī ge rén one person
一杯水 yī bēi shuǐ a glass of water
一杯咖啡 yī bēi kā fēi a cup of coffee
两杯咖啡 liǎng bēi kā fēi two cups of coffee
两杯茶 liǎng bēi chá two cups of tea
两瓶水 liǎng píng shuǐ two bottles of water
两碗饭 liǎng wǎn fàn two bowls of rice
几 vs 多少 how many (几 for <10, 多少 for any)
你们要几碗饭？ nǐ men yào jǐ wǎn fàn How many bowls of rice do you want?
我们要两碗饭。 wǒ men yào liǎng wǎn fàn We want two bowls of rice.
你们要几杯咖啡？ nǐ men yào jǐ bēi kā fēi How many cups of coffee do you want?
我们要两杯咖啡。 wǒ men yào liǎng bēi kā fēi We want two cups of coffee.
两位客人 liǎng wèi kè rén two guests
您几位？ nín jǐ wèi How many people (in your party)?
两位。 liǎng wèi Two.
可以 / 不可以 can / cannot
可以分两次付吗？ kě yǐ fēn liǎng cì fù ma Can we split the payment into two times?
可以。 kě yǐ Yes (you can).
不可以。 bù kě yǐ No (you can't).
坐这儿可以吗？ zuò zhèr kě yǐ ma Is it okay to sit here?
可以。 kě yǐ Yes, it's fine.
不可以，我想坐那儿。 bù kě yǐ, wǒ xiǎng zuò nàr No, I want to sit there.
点单 / 买单 ordering and paying
扫码点单。 sǎo mǎ diǎn dān Scan the code to order.
你好，买单！ nǐ hǎo, mǎi dān Hello, the bill please!
你扫我还是我扫你？ nǐ sǎo wǒ hái shì wǒ sǎo nǐ Do you scan me or do I scan you?
我扫你。 wǒ sǎo nǐ I scan you.
🍽️ 餐厅对话 restaurant dialogue
服务员：您几位？ fú wù yuán: nín jǐ wèi Waiter: How many people?
客人：两位。 kè rén: liǎng wèi Guest: Two.
服务员：这边请。 fú wù yuán: zhè biān qǐng Waiter: This way please.
服务员：坐这儿可以吗？ fú wù yuán: zuò zhèr kě yǐ ma Waiter: Is it okay to sit here?
客人：可以。 kè rén: kě yǐ Guest: Yes (it's okay).
客人：不可以，我想坐那儿。 kè rén: bù kě yǐ, wǒ xiǎng zuò nàr Guest: No, I want to sit there.
服务员：这是菜单，请点单。 fú wù yuán: zhè shì cài dān, qǐng diǎn dān Waiter: Here's the menu, please order.
客人：扫码点单。 kè rén: sǎo mǎ diǎn dān Guest: (We'll) scan the code to order.
服务员：您想吃什么？ fú wù yuán: nín xiǎng chī shén me Waiter: What would you like to eat?
客人：我想吃这个。 kè rén: wǒ xiǎng chī zhè ge Guest: I'd like to eat this.
服务员：您想喝什么？ fú wù yuán: nín xiǎng hē shén me Waiter: What would you like to drink?
客人：我想喝茶。 kè rén: wǒ xiǎng hē chá Guest: I'd like to drink tea.
服务员：我们没有咖啡，我们有茶。 fú wù yuán: wǒ men méi yǒu kā fēi, wǒ men yǒu chá Waiter: We don't have coffee, we have tea.
服务员：您喝茶吗？ fú wù yuán: nín hē chá ma Waiter: Do you drink tea?
客人：可以。 kè rén: kě yǐ Guest: Okay.
客人：你好，买单！ kè rén: nǐ hǎo, mǎi dān Guest: Hello, the bill please!
客人：你扫我还是我扫你？ kè rén: nǐ sǎo wǒ hái shì wǒ sǎo nǐ Guest: Do you scan me or do I scan you?
服务员：我扫你。 fú wù yuán: wǒ sǎo nǐ Waiter: I scan you.
客人：可以分两次付吗？ kè rén: kě yǐ fēn liǎng cì fù ma Guest: Can we split the payment into two times?
服务员：可以。 fú wù yuán: kě yǐ Waiter: Yes.
