📚 Grammar
也 also
我也喝了咖啡。 wǒ yě hē le kā fēi I also drank coffee.
我也喜欢吃鸡肉。 wǒ yě xǐ huān chī jī ròu I also like to eat chicken.
想 / 要 want to (desire / urgent)
我想喝水。 wǒ xiǎng hē shuǐ I want to drink water.
我要上厕所。 wǒ yào shàng cè suǒ I need to go to the bathroom.
肉 / 牛肉 / 猪肉 / 鸡肉 / 羊肉 meat types
这是什么肉？ zhè shì shén me ròu What meat is this?
我喜欢吃猪肉和牛肉。 wǒ xǐ huān chī zhū ròu hé niú ròu I like to eat pork and beef.
喜欢 to like
你喜欢吃什么肉？ nǐ xǐ huān chī shén me ròu What meat do you like to eat?
我喜欢吃羊肉。 wǒ xǐ huān chī yáng ròu I like to eat lamb.
A 在 B direction A is at B location
请问，这个酒店在哪儿？ qǐng wèn, zhè gè jiǔ diàn zài nǎr Excuse me, where is this hotel?
酒店在饭店旁边。 jiǔ diàn zài fàn diàn páng biān The hotel is next to the restaurant.
洗手间在饭店右边。 xǐ shǒu jiān zài fàn diàn yòu biān The restroom is to the right of the restaurant.
你在我前面。 nǐ zài wǒ qián miàn You are in front of me.
这儿 / 那儿 / 哪儿 here / there / where
你们去哪儿？ nǐ men qù nǎr Where are you going?
我们去这里。 wǒ men qù zhè lǐ We're going here.
就儿在那儿！ jiù zài nàr It's right there!
楼上 / 楼下 upstairs / downstairs
洗手间在哪儿？ xǐ shǒu jiān zài nǎr Where is the restroom?
洗手间在楼上。 xǐ shǒu jiān zài lóu shàng The restroom is upstairs.
洗手间在2楼。 xǐ shǒu jiān zài èr lóu The restroom is on the 2nd floor.
直走 / 右拐 go straight / turn right
直走，右拐。 zhí zǒu, yòu guǎi Go straight and turn right.
有吃的吗？ Is there food?
（你们这儿）有吃的吗？ nǐ men zhèr yǒu chī de ma Do you have food here?
有，吃的在那儿。 yǒu, chī de zài nàr Yes, the food is over there.
