📚 Grammar
A 在 B direction A is (direction of) B
超市在酒店旁边。 chāo shì zài jiǔ diàn páng biān The supermarket is next to the hotel.
地铁站在商场对面。 dì tiě zhàn zài shāng chǎng duì miàn The subway station is across from the mall.
洗手间在前面。 xǐ shǒu jiān zài qián miàn The restroom is up ahead.
餐厅在后面。 cān tīng zài hòu miàn The restaurant is in the back.
去 / 来 go / come
我要去酒店。 wǒ yào qù jiǔ diàn I want to go to the hotel.
去机场怎么走？ qù jī chǎng zěn me zǒu How do I get to the airport?
请来这儿。 qǐng lái zhèr Please come here.
往 + direction + V go / turn (direction)
往前走。 wǎng qián zǒu Go forward.
往左拐。 wǎng zuǒ guǎi Turn left.
往右拐。 wǎng yòu guǎi Turn right.
一直往前走。 yī zhí wǎng qián zǒu Keep going straight.
往这儿走。 wǎng zhèr zǒu Go this way.
这儿 / 那儿 / 哪儿 here / there / where
酒店在哪儿？ jiǔ diàn zài nǎr Where is the hotel?
酒店在那儿。 jiǔ diàn zài nàr The hotel is over there.
请问，哪儿有地铁站？ qǐng wèn, nǎr yǒu dì tiě zhàn Excuse me, where is the subway station?
我在这儿等你。 wǒ zài zhèr děng nǐ I'll wait for you here.
远 / 近 / 附近 far / near / nearby
离这儿远吗？ lí zhèr yuǎn ma Is it far from here?
走路五分钟。 zǒu lù wǔ fēn zhōng It's a five-minute walk.
附近有酒店吗？ fù jìn yǒu jiǔ diàn ma Is there a hotel nearby?
