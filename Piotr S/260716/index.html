📚 Grammar
了 / 没 le for completion / didn't
你吃早餐了吗？ nǐ chī zǎo cān le ma Did you eat breakfast?
我吃了。 wǒ chī le I ate.
我没吃。 wǒ méi chī I didn't eat.
你早上吃了什么？ nǐ zǎo shang chī le shén me What did you eat in the morning?
我吃了早餐。 wǒ chī le zǎo cān I ate breakfast.
