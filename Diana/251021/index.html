💬 Sentences
你们关系好吗？Nǐmen guānxì hǎo ma?Do you get along well?
我们关系很好。Wǒmen guānxì hěn hǎo.We get along very well.
你吃（晚）饭了吗？Nǐ chī (wǎn) fàn le ma?Have you eaten (dinner)?
我没吃饭。Wǒ méi chī fàn.I haven’t eaten.
我吃饭了。Wǒ chī fàn le.I have eaten.
有的...有的...yǒu de... yǒu de...Some... others...
面条好吃，有的面条不好吃。Miàntiáo hǎochī, yǒu de miàntiáo bù hǎochī.Some noodles are tasty, some are not.
是的。Shì de.Yes. / That’s right.
我很爱吃面包。Wǒ hěn ài chī miànbāo.I really love eating bread.
你有时候喝什么酒？Nǐ yǒu shíhou hē shénme jiǔ?What kind of alcohol do you sometimes drink?
我有时候喝红酒。Wǒ yǒu shíhou hē hóngjiǔ.I sometimes drink red wine.
我很爱喝奶茶。Wǒ hěn ài hē nǎichá.I really love drinking milk tea.
我妈妈也很爱喝奶茶。Wǒ māmā yě hěn ài hē nǎichá.My mom also loves drinking milk tea.
