📚 Grammar
很多 + N.
(Structure) a lot of + noun
马斯克有很多钱。Mǎ sī kè yǒu hěn duō qián.Musk has a lot of money.
我喝了很多水。Wǒ hē le hěn duō shuǐ.I drank a lot of water.
我吃了很多吃的。Wǒ chī le hěn duō chī de.I ate a lot of food.
我买了很多书。Wǒ mǎi le hěn duō shū.I bought a lot of books.
很少 + V.
(Structure) rarely + verb
我很少读书。Wǒ hěn shǎo dú shū.I rarely read books.
我很少吃洋葱。Wǒ hěn shǎo chī yáng cōng.I rarely eat onions.
我很少喝酒。Wǒ hěn shǎo hē jiǔ.I rarely drink alcohol.
我也是！Wǒ yě shì!(Structure) Me too!
我也是！Wǒ yě shì!Me too!
你要换多少...？Nǐ yào huàn duō shǎo...?(Structure) How much... do you want to exchange?
你要换多少美元？Nǐ yào huàn duō shǎo měi yuán?How many US dollars do you want to exchange?
你要换多少钱？Nǐ yào huàn duō shǎo qián?How much money do you want to exchange?
我要换100美元。Wǒ yào huàn yī bǎi měi yuán.I want to exchange 100 US dollars.
