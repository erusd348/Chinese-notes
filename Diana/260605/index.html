📚 Grammar
S来V. 一下O.
Subject + 来 + Verb + 一下 + Object
我认识你。wǒ rèn shi nǐI know you.
你认识他吗？nǐ rèn shi tā maDo you know him?
我很高。wǒ hěn gāoI am tall.
我不高。wǒ bù gāoI am not tall.
我很高兴。wǒ hěn gāo xìngI am very happy.
我很不高兴。wǒ hěn bù gāo xìngI am very unhappy.
我很高兴认识你。wǒ hěn gāo xìng rèn shi nǐI'm happy to meet you.
我来牙买加了。wǒ lái yá mǎi jiā leI came to Jamaica.
我来巴西了。wǒ lái bā xī leI came to Brazil.
你要去哪儿？nǐ yào qù nǎrWhere do you want to go?
你想去哪儿？nǐ xiǎng qù nǎrWhere do you want to go?
我来买！wǒ lái mǎiI'll buy it!
我来！wǒ láiI'll do it!
你来读！nǐ lái dúYou read it!
你来说！nǐ lái shuōYou say it!
看一下。kàn yī xiàTake a quick look.
听一下。tīng yī xiàListen for a moment.
说一下。shuō yī xiàSay a bit.

你来吃一下这个鱼。nǐ lái chī yī xià zhè ge yúCome try this fish.
