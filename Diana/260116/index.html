📚 Grammar
没 + v.
(Structure) did not + verb (negation of past action)
我没吃晚饭。Wǒ méi chī wǎn fàn.I didn't eat dinner.
你不饿吗？Nǐ bù è ma?Aren't you hungry?
是的，我不饿。Shì de, wǒ bù è.Yes (that's right), I'm not hungry.
一顿 + 饭
(Structure) one + meal (measure word for meals)
我吃了一顿很好的午饭。Wǒ chī le yī dùn hěn hǎo de wǔ fàn.I ate a very good lunch.
一杯水/咖啡/牛奶/可乐yī bēi shuǐ/kā fēi/niú nǎi/kě lèa glass of water/coffee/milk/cola
你今天喝了多少杯咖啡？Nǐ jīn tiān hē le duō shǎo bēi kā fēi?How many cups of coffee did you drink today?
我今天喝了3杯咖啡。Wǒ jīn tiān hē le sān bēi kā fēi.I drank 3 cups of coffee today.
我买了两个杯子。Wǒ mǎi le liǎng gè bēi zi.I bought two cups.
