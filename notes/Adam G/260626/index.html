📚 Grammar
很多 a lot of; very many
Musk 有很多钱。 musk yǒu hěn duō qián Musk has a lot of money.
中国有很多人。 zhōng guó yǒu hěn duō rén China has a lot of people.
中国的人很多。 zhōng guó de rén hěn duō China's people are many.
Musk的钱很多。 musk de qián hěn duō Musk's money is abundant.
我昨天晚上喝了很多啤酒。 wǒ zuó tiān wǎn shang hē le hěn duō pí jiǔ I drank a lot of beer last night.
我今天上午喝了很多咖啡。 wǒ jīn tiān shàng wǔ hē le hěn duō kā fēi I drank a lot of coffee this morning.
我今天早上吃了很多面条。 wǒ jīn tiān zǎo shang chī le hěn duō miàn tiáo I ate a lot of noodles this morning.
我没有很多钱。 wǒ méi yǒu hěn duō qián I don't have a lot of money.
很少 rarely; seldom
我很少喝酒。 wǒ hěn shǎo hē jiǔ I rarely drink alcohol.
我很少吃牛肉和羊肉。 wǒ hěn shǎo chī niú ròu hé yáng ròu I rarely eat beef and lamb.
我很少喝白酒。 wǒ hěn shǎo hē bái jiǔ I rarely drink baijiu.
韩国的人很少。 hán guó de rén hěn shǎo South Korea has few people.
牛肉很少。 niú ròu hěn shǎo (There is) very little beef.
多少 how many; how much
韩国有多少人？ hán guó yǒu duō shǎo rén How many people does South Korea have?
你喝了多少杯咖啡？ nǐ hē le duō shǎo bēi kā fēi How many cups of coffee did you drink?
我喝了一杯咖啡。 wǒ hē le yī bēi kā fēi I drank one cup of coffee.
你喝了多少瓶啤酒？ nǐ hē le duō shǎo píng pí jiǔ How many bottles of beer did you drink?
我喝了4瓶啤酒。 wǒ hē le sì píng pí jiǔ I drank 4 bottles of beer.
要 to want
我要吃饭。 wǒ yào chī fàn I want to eat.
你要吃什么？ nǐ yào chī shén me What do you want to eat?
我要吃汉堡。 wǒ yào chī hàn bǎo I want to eat a burger.
你要换多少钱？ nǐ yào huàn duō shǎo qián How much money do you want to exchange?
我要换200元。 wǒ yào huàn èr bǎi yuán I want to exchange 200 yuan.
想 to miss; to want
我想你！ wǒ xiǎng nǐ I miss you!
想要 to want to have
我想要你的爱。 wǒ xiǎng yào nǐ de ài I want your love.
我想要很多钱。 wǒ xiǎng yào hěn duō qián I want to have a lot of money.
你想要什么？ nǐ xiǎng yào shén me What do you want?
我想要一个漂亮的女朋友。 wǒ xiǎng yào yī ge piào liang de nǚ péng you I want a pretty girlfriend.
我想要吃好吃的。 wǒ xiǎng yào chī hǎo chī de I want to eat something delicious.
换 to exchange; to replace
我换了一个女朋友。 wǒ huàn le yī ge nǚ péng you I changed and got a new girlfriend.
💬 Sentences
我常常吃汉堡。 wǒ cháng cháng chī hàn bǎo I often eat burgers.
