📚 Grammar
💬 Sentences
A 比 B + AdjectiveA bǐ B + xíngróngcíComparative structure: "A is more [adjective] than B."
麦当劳的炸鸡比肯德基的炸鸡好吃。Màidāngláo de zhájī bǐ Kěndéjī de zhájī hǎochī.McDonald's fried chicken is more delicious than KFC's.
我的城市比新加坡大。Wǒ de chéngshì bǐ Xīnjiāpō dà.My city is bigger than Singapore.
B 没有 A + AdjectiveB méiyǒu A + xíngróngcíComparative structure (negative): "B is not as [adjective] as A."
我的银行账户里没有很多钱。Wǒ de yínháng zhànghù lǐ méiyǒu hěnduō qián.There isn't a lot of money in my bank account.
Adverb of Frequency PlacementPínlǜ fùcí de wèizhìAdverbs of frequency (e.g., 常常, 不常) are placed before the verb.
我们常常吃炸鸡。Wǒmen chángcháng chī zhájī.We often eat fried chicken.
我们不常做饭。Wǒmen bù cháng zuòfàn.We don't cook often.
Location + 里dìdiǎn + lǐExpresses "inside" or "at" a location.
学校里有很多学生。Xuéxiào lǐ yǒu hěnduō xuéshēng.There are many students at the school.
图书馆里有很多书。Túshūguǎn lǐ yǒu hěnduō shū.There are many books in the library.
银行里有很多钱。Yínháng lǐ yǒu hěnduō qián.There is a lot of money in the bank.
我的银行账户里没有很多钱。Wǒ de yínháng zhànghù lǐ méiyǒu hěnduō qián.There isn't a lot of money in my bank account.
我没有室友，我住在家里。Wǒ méiyǒu shìyǒu, wǒ zhù zài jiā lǐ.I don't have a roommate, I live at home.
A 离 B + 远/近A lí B + yuǎn/jìnStructure to express distance: "A is far from/close to B."
你家离公司远不远？Nǐ jiā lí gōngsī yuǎn bu yuǎn?Is your home far from the company?
我家离公司很远。Wǒ jiā lí gōngsī hěn yuǎn.My home is very far from the company.
马来西亚离新加坡很近。Mǎláixīyà lí Xīnjiāpō hěn jìn.Malaysia is very close to Singapore.
我家离菜店很近。Wǒ jiā lí càidiàn hěn jìn.My home is very close to the grocery store.
Verb + 不 + Verbdòngcí + bù + dòngcíAffirmative-negative question form for verbs: "Verb or not verb?"
睡不睡觉？Shuì bu shuìjiào?Are you going to sleep or not?
知不知道？Zhī bu zhīdào?Do you know or not?
Verb + 没 + Verbdòngcí + méi + dòngcíAffirmative-negative question form about past actions: "Did verb or did not verb?"
吃没吃饭？Chī méi chīfàn?Have you eaten or not?
喝没喝酒？Hē méi hējiǔ?Have you drunk alcohol or not?
你爱没爱过我？Nǐ ài méi àiguo wǒ?Did you ever love me or not?
Adjective + 不 + Adjectivexíngróngcí + bù + xíngróngcíAffirmative-negative question form for adjectives: "[Adjective] or not [adjective]?"
好不好？Hǎo bu hǎo?Good or not? (Is it okay?)
对不对？Duì bu duì?Correct or not?
可不可爱？Kě bu kě'ài?Cute or not?
听不听话？Tīng bu tīnghuà?(Are you) obedient or not? / Will (you) listen or not?
