📚 Grammar
差
adj. bad quality / v. be short of, lack
这个茶很差。zhè ge chá hěn chàThis tea is very bad.
我们差一个人。wǒ men chà yī gè rénWe are short one person.
我们差一杯可乐。wǒ men chà yī bēi kě lèWe are short one cola.
V过
have done (past experience marker)
我去过土耳其。wǒ qù guò tǔ ěr qíI have been to Turkey.
我吃过土耳其水果。wǒ chī guò tǔ ěr qí shuǐ guǒI have eaten Turkish fruit.
从...到...
from...to...
我们从4号到18号在欧洲。wǒ men cóng 4 hào dào 18 hào zài ōu zhōuWe are in Europe from 4th to 18th.
我们从7点到8点上课。wǒ men cóng 7 diǎn dào 8 diǎn shàng kèWe have class from 7 to 8.
