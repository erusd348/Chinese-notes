📚 Grammar
Place + 有 + 挺多 + Noun + 的
Pattern for stating that a place has quite a number of something.
我们学校有挺多留学生的。Wǒmen xuéxiào yǒu tǐng duō liúxuéshēng de.Our school has quite a number of international students.
Place + 的 + Noun + 挺 + Adj. + 的
Pattern for describing the noun associated with a place.
我们学校的留学生挺多的。Wǒmen xuéxiào de liúxuéshēng tǐng duō de.The international students at our school are quite numerous.
