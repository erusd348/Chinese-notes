💬 Dialogue
飞龙：你好! 你叫什么名字?Fēi Lóng: Nǐ hǎo! Nǐ jiào shénme míngzì?Feilong: Hello! What's your name?
李钟文：我叫李钟文。Lǐ Zhōngwén: Wǒ jiào Lǐ Zhōngwén.Li Zhongwen: My name is Li Zhongwen.
飞龙：你好，李钟文!我猜你一定是韩国人。Fēi Lóng: Nǐ hǎo, Lǐ Zhōngwén! Wǒ cāi nǐ yídìng shì Hánguó rén.Feilong: Hi, Li Zhongwen! I bet you're Korean.
李钟文：你真聪明! 你呢?Lǐ Zhōngwén: Nǐ zhēn cōngmíng! Nǐ ne?Li Zhongwen: You're smart! And you?
飞龙：我叫飞龙，法国人，是大学生。Fēi Lóng: Wǒ jiào Fēi Lóng, Fǎguó rén, shì dàxuéshēng.Feilong: I'm Feilong, French, a university student.
李钟文：你为什么来学汉语?Lǐ Zhōngwén: Nǐ wèishénme lái xué Hànyǔ?Li Zhongwen: Why did you come to study Chinese?
飞龙：去法国的中国人越来越多[1]，我希望以后做汉语翻译。你 呢?Fēi Lóng: Qù Fǎguó de Zhōngguó rén yuè lái yuè duō, wǒ xīwàng yǐhòu zuò Hànyǔ fānyì. Nǐ ne?Feilong: More and more Chinese go to France. I hope to become a Chinese translator. And you?
李钟文：是公司派我来学习的[2]。我要先在这儿学习半年[3]，然后在中国工作。Lǐ Zhōngwén: Shì gōngsī pài wǒ lái xuéxí de. Wǒ yào xiān zài zhèr xuéxí bàn nián, ránhòu zài Zhōngguó gōngzuò.Li Zhongwen: My company sent me to study. I'll study here for half a year first, then work in China.
💬 Dialogue 2
望月：我叫望月智子，我的发音不太好，请多多关照。Wàngyuè: Wǒ jiào Wàngyuè Zhìzǐ, wǒ de fāyīn bú tài hǎo, qǐng duōduō guānzhào.Wangyue: I'm Wangyue Zhizi. My pronunciation isn't very good, please take care of me.
爱珍：我的中文名字叫爱珍，我是从美国来的。很高兴跟你同屋。Àizhēn: Wǒ de Zhōngwén míngzì jiào Àizhēn, wǒ shì cóng Měiguó lái de. Hěn gāoxìng gēn nǐ tóng wū.Aizhen: My Chinese name is Aizhen, I'm from America. Happy to share a room with you.
望月：我也很高兴。不过我不会说英语。Wàngyuè: Wǒ yě hěn gāoxìng. Búguò wǒ bú huì shuō Yīngyǔ.Wangyue: I'm happy too. But I can't speak English.
爱珍：没关系。这样更好，咱们只能说汉语。Àizhēn: Méi guānxi. Zhèyàng gèng hǎo, zánmen zhǐ néng shuō Hànyǔ.Aizhen: It's OK. That's even better, we can only speak Chinese.
望月：希望我的发音越来越好。Wàngyuè: Xīwàng wǒ de fāyīn yuè lái yuè hǎo.Wangyue: Hope my pronunciation gets better and better.
爱珍：这个暑假，我要一边学汉语，一边学京剧[4]。Àizhēn: Zhège shǔjià, wǒ yào yìbiān xué Hànyǔ, yìbiān xué jīngjù.Aizhen: This summer break, I'll study Chinese and learn Beijing opera at the same time.
望月：你会唱京剧?我从来没听过京剧，你现在能唱一段吗?Wàngyuè: Nǐ huì chàng jīngjù? Wǒ cónglái méi tīngguò jīngjù, nǐ xiànzài néng chàng yí duàn ma?Wangyue: You can sing Beijing opera? I've never heard it before, can you sing a bit now?
爱珍：现在不行，一个月以后吧。到时候我一定唱给你听。Àizhēn: Xiànzài bùxíng, yí ge yuè yǐhòu ba. Dào shíhou wǒ yídìng chàng gěi nǐ tīng.Aizhen: Not now, maybe in a month. I'll definitely sing for you then.
📝 Notes
[1] 去法国的中国人越来越多，……
"越来越"表示程度随时间的推移而发展。例如：
天气越来越凉快。
"越来越" indicates that the degree becomes higher and higher as time passes, e.g.,
The weather is getting cooler and cooler.
[2] 是公司派我来学习的。
"是……的"强调说明做某件事的时间、地点、方式或人(团体)。被强调的必须是已经发生的事情。
"是……的" emphasizes the time, the place, the way of doing something, or the agent who does it. What is emphasized must be something that has already taken place.
[3] 我要先在这儿学习半年，然后在中国工作。
常用关联副词"先"和连词"然后"前后呼应，表示两件事情的顺序。
The adverb "先" is often used before the conjunction "然后" to indicate the sequence of two events.
[4] 我要一边学汉语，一边学京剧。
"一边……,一边……"表示在同一时间内进行两个或两个以上的动作。
Two "一边" are used in conjunction to indicate doing two or more actions at the same time.
[5] 他觉得汉语一点儿也不难。
"一……也/都+不/没……"用来强调对某种动作、行为或某种性质的否定。
"一……也/都+不/没……" is used to emphasize negation of an action, behavior, or quality.
[6] 美国学生爱珍学汉语是为了学京剧。
"为了"常用在前一分句的开始表示目的，后一分句表示为达到这一目的而采取的行动。
"为了" is often used in the first clause to indicate the aim, the second clause indicates the action taken.
