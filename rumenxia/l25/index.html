📝 Notes
Notes
口语中，当说话人突然想起别的话题，或对前边说的事情有所补充时，常用“对了”作插入语。
In spoken Chinese,when one suddenly thinks of other topics or something that has been mentioned before in the conversation needs more completion,one often inserts“对了”.
