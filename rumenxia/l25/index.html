Dialogue 1
小雨：今晚你和保罗有空儿的话，我想请你们去看电影。xiǎo yǔ： jīn wǎn nǐ hé bǎo luó yǒu kòng er de huà， wǒ xiǎng qǐng nǐ men qù kàn diàn yǐng。Xiaoyu: If you and Paul are free tonight, I'd like to invite you to a movie.
英男：我不太想去，电影里的对话太快了，我可能 听不懂。yīng nán： wǒ bù tài xiǎng qù， diàn yǐng lǐ de duì huà tài kuài le， wǒ kě néng tīng bù dǒng。Yingnan: I don't really want to go. The dialogue in the movie is too fast — I probably won't understand.
小雨：有中文字幕，大概的意思你肯定看得懂。xiǎo yǔ： yǒu zhōng wén zì mù， dà gài de yì si nǐ kěn dìng kàn de dǒng。Xiaoyu: There are Chinese subtitles. You'll definitely understand the gist.
保罗去得了去不了?bǎo luó qù dé le qù bù liǎo?Can Paul go or not?
英男：他恐怕去不了。yīng nán： tā kǒng pà qù bù liǎo。Yingnan: I'm afraid he can't go.
小雨：为什么 ?xiǎo yǔ： wèi shén me?Xiaoyu: Why?
英男：昨天他踢球的时候受伤了，现在走不了路了。yīng nán： zuó tiān tā tī qiú de shí hòu shòu shāng le， xiàn zài zǒu bù liǎo lù le。Yingnan: He hurt himself playing soccer yesterday. He can't walk now.
Dialogue 2
小雨：咱们坐这儿吧。xiǎo yǔ： zán men zuò zhè ér ba。Xiaoyu: Let's sit here.
英男：太远了，恐怕看不清楚字幕。yīng nán： tài yuǎn le， kǒng pà kàn bù qīng chǔ zì mù。Yingnan: It's too far. I'm afraid I won't be able to read the subtitles clearly.
小雨：那就坐前边吧。xiǎo yǔ： nà jiù zuò qián biān ba。Xiaoyu: Then let's sit in the front.
英男：坐这儿不错，看得清楚，也听得清楚。yīng nán： zuò zhè ér bù cuò， kàn de qīng chǔ， yě tīng dé qīng chǔ。Yingnan: This spot is good. I can see clearly and hear clearly.
小雨：可是我前边这个人太高了，我看不见。xiǎo yǔ： kě shì wǒ qián biān zhè ge rén tài gāo le， wǒ kàn bú jiàn。Xiaoyu: But the person in front of me is too tall. I can't see.
英男：咱俩换一下儿座位吧。yīng nán： zán liǎ huàn yī xià ér zuò wèi ba。Yingnan: Let's switch seats.
Dialogue 3
田中：直美她们邀请咱们明天一起去郊游，你去 得了吗 ?tián zhōng： zhí měi tā men yāo qǐng zán men míng tiān yì qǐ qù jiāo yóu， nǐ qù dé le ma?Tanaka: Naomi and the others invited us on an outing tomorrow. Can you make it?
英男：没问题，我早就想出去玩儿玩儿了。yīng nán： méi wèn tí， wǒ zǎo jiù xiǎng chū qù wán ér wán ér le。Yingnan: No problem. I've been wanting to go out and have fun for a while.
田中：我们约好早上六点半出发。tián zhōng： wǒ men yuē hǎo zǎo shàng liù diǎn bàn chū fā。Tanaka: We agreed to set off at 6:30 in the morning.
英男：这么早，怎么起得来呢?yīng nán： zhè me zǎo， zěn me qǐ dé lái ne?Yingnan: So early! How will I ever wake up in time?
田中：咱们得坐长途汽车，去晚了的话就赶不 上了。tián zhōng： zán men dé zuò cháng tú qì chē， qù wǎn le de huà jiù gǎn bù shàng le。Tanaka: We have to take a long-distance bus. If we're late, we'll miss it.
英男：那好吧。yīng nán： nà hǎo ba。Yingnan: Alright then.
对了，晚上七点我还有个约会， 七点以前回得来回不来?duì le， wǎn shàng qī diǎn wǒ hái yǒu gè yuē huì， qī diǎn yǐ qián huí dé lái huí bù lái?Oh, I also have a date at 7 PM. Can I be back before 7 or not?
田中：放心吧，耽误不了你的约会。tián zhōng： fàng xīn ba， dān wù bù liǎo nǐ de yuē huì。Tanaka: Don't worry. It won't mess up your date.
📝 Notes
对了 — 'By the Way / Oh, I Remember'
In spoken Chinese, 对了 is used when the speaker suddenly thinks of another topic or remembers something to add. It functions as an interjection.
📚 Grammar
Potential Complement (可能补语)
The complement of potential shows ability/inability to achieve a result. Insert「得」(can) or「不」(cannot) between a verb and its complement. Pattern: V + 得/不 + Result/Tendency.
看得清楚kàn de qīng chǔCan see clearly
听不懂tīng bù dǒngCan't understand (by listening)
去得了去不了？qù dé le qù bù liǎo？Can go or not?
他恐怕去不了。tā kǒng pà qù bù liǎo。I'm afraid he can't go.
怎么起得来呢？zěn me qǐ dé lái ne？How can I possibly wake up?
得 / 不 + 了 — Potential of Completion
「V+得了」means "can manage to V" and「V+不了」means "cannot manage to V." This is a subset of the potential complement using「了」(liǎo) meaning "to finish/capable."
你去得了吗？nǐ qù dé le ma？Can you make it?
他走不了路了。tā zǒu bù liǎo lù le。He can't walk.
七点以前回得来。qī diǎn yǐ qián huí dé lái。Can come back before 7.
