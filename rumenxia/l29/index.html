💬 Dialogue
Dialogue 1
莉莉：你怎么了 ?lì lì： nǐ zěn me le?Lily: What happened to you?
直美：别提了, 刚才骑车出去，被一辆三轮车撞倒了。zhí měi： bié tí le, gāng cái qí chē chū qù， bèi yī liàng sān lún chē zhuàng dào le。Naomi: Don't even mention it. I was biking and got knocked down by a tricycle.
莉莉：是吗 ?lì lì： shì ma?Lily: Really?
受伤了没有?shòu shāng le méi yǒu?Are you hurt?
直美：你看，胳膊和腿都被撞青了。zhí měi： nǐ kàn， gē bó hé tuǐ dōu bèi zhuàng qīng le。Naomi: Look, my arms and legs are all bruised.
真倒霉!zhēn dǎo méi!What bad luck!
莉莉：以后骑车的时候小心点儿。lì lì： yǐ hòu qí chē de shí hòu xiǎo xīn diǎn ér。Lily: Be more careful when you ride from now on.
直美：不是我不小心，是那个骑三轮车的骑得太快了。zhí měi： bú shì wǒ bù xiǎo xīn， shì nà ge qí sān lún chē de qí dé tài kuài le。Naomi: It's not that I wasn't careful — that guy was going way too fast.
莉莉：真不像话!lì lì： zhēn bù xiàng huà!Lily: That's outrageous!
Dialogue 2
直美：我今天真是倒霉透了 !zhí měi： wǒ jīn tiān zhēn shì dǎo méi tòu le!Naomi: I've had the worst luck today!
莉莉：还有什么倒霉事 ?lì lì： hái yǒu shén me dǎo méi shì?Lily: What else happened?
直美：上午逛商店的时候，钱包叫小偷 偷走了。zhí měi： shàng wǔ guàng shāng diàn de shí hòu， qián bāo jiào xiǎo tōu tōu zǒu le。Naomi: When I was shopping this morning, my wallet was stolen.
莉莉：丢了多少钱?lì lì： diū le duō shǎo qián?Lily: How much did you lose?
直美：五百多块。zhí měi： wǔ bǎi duō kuài。Naomi: Over 500 yuan.
Dialogue 3
莉莉：大后天考听力，你复习得怎么样了?lì lì： dà hòu tiān kǎo tīng lì， nǐ fù xí dé zěn me yàng le?Lily: The listening exam is in three days. How's your review going?
直美：咳，连电脑也让我给摔坏了，现在听不了 录音了。zhí měi： ké， lián diàn nǎo yě ràng wǒ gěi shuāi huài le， xiàn zài tīng bù liǎo lù yīn le。Naomi: Ugh, I even broke my computer. Now I can't listen to the recordings.
真急人!zhēn jí rén!So frustrating!
莉莉：你把它送到小雨那儿吧。lì lì： nǐ bǎ tā sòng dào xiǎo yǔ nà ér ba。Lily: Take it to Xiaoyu's place.
上次我的电脑就是 让小雨给修好的。shàng cì wǒ de diàn nǎo jiù shì ràng xiǎo yǔ gěi xiū hǎo de。He fixed my computer last time.
直美：好主意，我马上就去。zhí měi： hǎo zhǔ yì， wǒ mǎ shàng jiù qù。Naomi: Good idea. I'll go right away.
📝 Notes
被动句 — Passive Sentences
The passive voice in Chinese uses 被 (or 叫/让 in spoken Chinese) to introduce the agent. Pattern: Subject + 被 + Agent + Verb + Complements. Example: 钱包被小偷偷走了 (The wallet was stolen by a thief).
