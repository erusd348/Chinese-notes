💬 Dialogue
Dialogue 1
莉莉：下星期我就要回国了，我来向您告别。lì lì： xià xīng qī wǒ jiù yào huí guó le， wǒ lái xiàng nín gào bié。Lily: I'm going back to my country next week. I've come to say goodbye.
老师：行李都收拾好了吗 ?lǎo shī： xíng lǐ dōu shōu shí hǎo le ma?Teacher: Are your bags all packed?
莉莉：都收拾好了。lì lì： dōu shōu shí hǎo le。Lily: Yes, all packed.
老师：哪天的飞机?lǎo shī： nǎ tiān de fēi jī?Teacher: What day is your flight?
莉莉：下星期二上午十点一刻的。lì lì： xià xīng qī èr shàng wǔ shí diǎn yī kè de。Lily: Next Tuesday at 10:15 AM.
老师：星期二我还得上班，恐怕送不了你了。lǎo shī： xīng qī èr wǒ hái dé shàng bān， kǒng pà sòng bù liǎo nǐ le。Teacher: I have to work on Tuesday. I'm afraid I can't see you off.
莉莉：不用送了，您多保重。lì lì： bù yòng sòng le， nín duō bǎo zhòng。Lily: No need to see me off. Take care of yourself.
Dialogue 2
小叶：莉莉，今天我们给你饯行，祝你一路平安!xiǎo yè： lì lì， jīn tiān wǒ men gěi nǐ jiàn xíng， zhù nǐ yí lù píng ān!Xiaoye: Lily, today we're having a farewell dinner for you. Bon voyage!
莉莉：我也祝你们生活幸福，工作顺利 !lì lì： wǒ yě zhù nǐ men shēng huó xìng fú， gōng zuò shùn lì!Lily: I also wish you all happiness in life and success at work!
小叶：刚来中国的时候，你连一句汉语也不会说，现在说得真不错。xiǎo yè： gāng lái zhōng guó de shí hòu， nǐ lián yī jù hàn yǔ yě bú huì shuō， xiàn zài shuō dé zhēn bù cuò。Xiaoye: When you first came to China, you couldn't speak a word of Chinese. Now you speak really well.
莉莉：你们给了我很多帮助，我真不知道怎么感谢你们才好。lì lì： nǐ men gěi le wǒ hěn duō bāng zhù， wǒ zhēn bù zhī dào zěn me gǎn xiè nǐ men cái hǎo。Lily: You've all helped me so much. I don't know how to thank you.
小叶：你说到哪儿去了?我们真舍不得你走。xiǎo yè： nǐ shuō dào nǎ ér qù le? wǒ men zhēn shě bù dé nǐ zǒu。Xiaoye: Don't say that! We'll really miss you when you're gone.
莉莉：我也是。我们一定会再见面的。lì lì： wǒ yě shì。 wǒ men yí dìng huì zài jiàn miàn de。Lily: Me too. We'll definitely meet again.
小叶：来，大家举杯，为我们的友谊干杯。xiǎo yè： lái， dà jiā jǔ bēi， wèi wǒ men de yǒu yì gān bēi。Xiaoye: Come on, everyone, raise your glasses. To our friendship — cheers!
大家：干杯 !dà jiā： gān bēi!Everyone: Cheers!
Dialogue 3
小叶：咱们一起照张相吧。xiǎo yè： zán men yì qǐ zhào zhāng xiāng ba。Xiaoye: Let's take a photo together.
莉莉：好。请把照片发给我。lì lì： hǎo。 qǐng bǎ zhào piān fā gěi wǒ。Lily: OK. Please send me the photo.
小叶：该办登机手续了，护照和机票都拿出来了吧?别丢了。xiǎo yè： gāi bàn dēng jī shǒu xù le， hù zhào hé jī piào dōu ná chū lái le ba? bié diū le。Xiaoye: Time to check in. Do you have your passport and ticket ready? Don't lose them.
莉莉：那咱们就在这儿说“再见”吧。lì lì： nà zán men jiù zài zhè ér shuō “ zài jiàn ” ba。Lily: Then let's say goodbye here.
小叶：别忘了给我们来信。xiǎo yè： bié wàng le gěi wǒ men lái xìn。Xiaoye: Don't forget to write to us.
莉莉：忘不了。lì lì： wàng bù liǎo。Lily: I won't forget.
小叶：回去见到西蒙的话，请代我们向他问好。xiǎo yè： huí qù jiàn dào xī méng de huà， qǐng dài wǒ men xiàng tā wèn hǎo。Xiaoye: If you see Simon back home, say hello to him for us.
莉莉：我一定转告。lì lì： wǒ yí dìng zhuǎn gào。Lily: I'll be sure to pass it on.
朋友们：一路顺风! 再见!péng yǒu men： yī lù shùn fēng! zài jiàn!Friends: Bon voyage! Goodbye!
莉莉：再见!lì lì： zài jiàn!Lily: Goodbye!
📝 Notes
意义上的被动句 — Notional Passive
Some Chinese sentences are passive in meaning but have no passive marker like「被」. The object appears at the subject position, and the verb is active in form. Example: 作业做完了 (The homework is finished). The subject "homework" receives the action but no passive word is used.
