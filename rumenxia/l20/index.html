💬 Dialogue
Dialogue 1
保罗：时间过得真快，我们来中国已经一个多 月了。bǎo luó： shí jiān guò dé zhēn kuài， wǒ men lái zhōng guó yǐ jīng yí gè duō yuè le。Paul: Time flies. It's been over a month since we came to China.
西蒙：是啊，明天我就要回国了。xī méng： shì a， míng tiān wǒ jiù yào huí guó le。Simon: Yeah, I'm going home tomorrow.
保罗：你觉得在这儿过得怎么样?bǎo luó： nǐ jué de zài zhè ér guò dé zěn me yàng?Paul: How have you been enjoying it here?
西蒙：虽然学习很紧张，但是我过得挺愉快。xī méng： suī rán xué xí hěn jǐn zhāng， dàn shì wǒ guò dé tǐng yú kuài。Simon: Although the studies are intense, I've been having a good time.
保罗：从北京到巴黎得坐多长时间的飞机?bǎo luó： cóng běi jīng dào bā lí dé zuò duō zhǎng shí jiān de fēi jī?Paul: How long does it take to fly from Beijing to Paris?
西蒙：时间不太长，坐十几个小时就到了。xī méng： shí jiān bù tài zhǎng， zuò shí jǐ gè xiǎo shí jiù dào le。Simon: Not too long — about ten hours or so.
保罗：明天我们去送你。bǎo luó： míng tiān wǒ men qù sòng nǐ。Paul: We'll see you off tomorrow.
Dialogue 2
保罗：你怎么现在才来 ？bǎo luó： nǐ zěn me xiàn zài cái lái？Paul: Why are you only here now?
我们等了你半天了。wǒ men děng le nǐ bàn tiān le。We've been waiting for you for ages.
小雨：因为路上堵车了，所以汽车开了五十分钟才到。xiǎo yǔ： yīn wèi lù shàng dǔ chē le， suǒ yǐ qì chē kāi le wǔ shí fēn zhōng cái dào。Xiaoyu: Because of a traffic jam, the bus took 50 minutes to get here.
保罗：你应该早点儿出发。bǎo luó： nǐ yīng gāi zǎo diǎn ér chū fā。Paul: You should have left earlier.
小雨：我七点就出发了，等车等了好长时间。xiǎo yǔ： wǒ qī diǎn jiù chū fā le， děng chē děng le hǎo zhǎng shí jiān。Xiaoyu: I left at 7, but I waited for the bus for a long time.
保罗：你先休息一会儿。bǎo luó： nǐ xiān xiū xī yī huì er。Paul: Take a break for a while.
小雨：不用了，咱们快走吧。xiǎo yǔ： bù yòng le， zán men kuài zǒu ba。Xiaoyu: No need. Let's go!
Dialogue 3
司机：你们学了多长时间汉语了?sī jī： nǐ men xué le duō zhǎng shí jiān hàn yǔ le?Driver: How long have you been studying Chinese?
西蒙：我们学了一个多月了。xī méng： wǒ men xué le yí gè duō yuè le。Simon: A bit over a month.
司机：你们说得真不错。sī jī： nǐ men shuō dé zhēn bù cuò。Driver: You speak really well.
西蒙：哪里哪里，还差得远呢。xī méng： nǎ lǐ nǎ lǐ， hái chà de yuǎn ne。Simon: Not at all, we still have a long way to go.
司机：你们还准备学多长时间?sī jī： nǐ men hái zhǔn bèi xué duō zhǎng shí jiān?Driver: How much longer are you planning to study?
西蒙：我今天就回国，他们还要再学两个星期。xī méng： wǒ jīn tiān jiù huí guó， tā men hái yào zài xué liǎng gè xīng qī。Simon: I'm going back today. They'll study for two more weeks.
📝 Notes
哪里哪里 / 还差得远呢 — Polite Modesty
哪里哪里 is a polite expression used when responding to a compliment. 还差得远呢 (still a long way to go) is also used to express modesty when someone praises your ability.
📚 Grammar
Duration Complement (时量补语)
A duration complement tells how long an action lasts. It comes after the verb. If there is an object, the verb is repeated: Verb + (Object) + Verb + Duration. Questions use「多长时间」.
我们学了一个多月了。wǒ men xué le yí gè duō yuè le。We've studied for over a month.
从北京到巴黎坐十几个小时就到了。cóng běi jīng dào bā lí zuò shí jǐ gè xiǎo shí jiù dào le。It takes about ten-plus hours from Beijing to Paris.
你等了多长时间？nǐ děng le duō zhǎng shí jiān？How long did you wait?
就要……了 / 快……了 — 'About To'
Similar to「要……了」but「就要……了」can take a time phrase before it (e.g., 明天我就要回国了).「快要……了」cannot take a time phrase.
明天我就要回国了。míng tiān wǒ jiù yào huí guó le。I'm going back home tomorrow.
火车快要开了。huǒ chē kuài yào kāi le。The train is about to depart.
才 — 'Only Then / Not Until'
「才」indicates that something happened later than expected or took more time/effort than anticipated.
汽车开了五十分钟才到。qì chē kāi le wǔ shí fēn zhōng cái dào。The bus took a full 50 minutes to arrive.
我等了半天他才来。wǒ děng le bàn tiān tā cái lái。I waited for ages before he came.
