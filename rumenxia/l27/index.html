💬 Dialogue
Dialogue 1
田中：你能不能把你的自行车借给我用用 ?tián zhōng： nǐ néng bù néng bǎ nǐ de zì xíng chē jiè gěi wǒ yòng yòng?Tanaka: Could you lend me your bike?
英男：没问题。yīng nán： méi wèn tí。Yingnan: No problem.
田中：你把自行车放在哪儿了?tián zhōng： nǐ bǎ zì xíng chē fàng zài nǎ ér le?Tanaka: Where did you park your bike?
英男：放在楼下的车棚里了。yīng nán： fàng zài lóu xià de chē péng lǐ le。Yingnan: In the bike shed downstairs.
田中：我用完就马上还给你。tián zhōng： wǒ yòng wán jiù mǎ shàng huán gěi nǐ。Tanaka: I'll return it as soon as I'm done.
英男：没关系，不用着急。yīng nán： méi guān xì， bù yòng zháo jí。Yingnan: That's fine, no rush.
Dialogue 2
田中：要是方便的话，明天你去办公室时 , 帮我把这张表送到办公室。tián zhōng： yào shì fāng biàn de huà， míng tiān nǐ qù bàn gōng shì shí, bāng wǒ bǎ zhè zhāng biǎo sòng dào bàn gōng shì。Tanaka: If it's convenient, when you go to the office tomorrow, help me deliver this form.
把它交给谁?bǎ tā jiāo gěi shuí?Who should I give it to?
田中：把它交给赵老师。tián zhōng： bǎ tā jiāo gěi zhào lǎo shī。Tanaka: Give it to Teacher Zhao.
英男：要是赵老师不在，怎么办?yīng nán： yào shì zhào lǎo shī bù zài， zěn me bàn?Yingnan: What if Teacher Zhao isn't there?
田中：赵老师不在的话，你就把表放在她的桌子上。tián zhōng： zhào lǎo shī bù zài de huà， nǐ jiù bǎ biǎo fàng zài tā de zhuō zi shàng。Tanaka: If she's not there, just put the form on her desk.
英男：好，我知道了。yīng nán： hǎo， wǒ zhī dào le。Yingnan: Okay, got it.
田中：你别忘了。tián zhōng： nǐ bié wàng le。Tanaka: Don't forget.
英男：放心吧，忘不了。yīng nán： fàng xīn ba， wàng bù liǎo。Yingnan: Don't worry, I won't forget.
Dialogue 3
田中：我想请你帮个忙。tián zhōng： wǒ xiǎng qǐng nǐ bāng gè máng。Tanaka: I'd like to ask a favor.
小雨：什么事?xiǎo yǔ： shén me shì?Xiaoyu: What is it?
田中：请你帮我把这篇文章翻译成英文，好吗?tián zhōng： qǐng nǐ bāng wǒ bǎ zhè piān wén zhāng fān yì chéng yīng wén， hǎo ma?Tanaka: Could you help me translate this article into English?
小雨：我的英文水平不高，怎么翻译得了啊?xiǎo yǔ： wǒ de yīng wén shuǐ píng bù gāo， zěn me fān yì dé le a?Xiaoyu: My English isn't great. How could I possibly translate it?
田中：你不是学英语的吗 ?tián zhōng： nǐ bú shì xué yīng yǔ de ma?Tanaka: Aren't you an English major?
小雨：哪儿啊,我是学法语的。xiǎo yǔ： nǎ ér a, wǒ shì xué fǎ yǔ de。Xiaoyu: No way! I'm a French major.
田中：真的吗?tián zhōng： zhēn de ma?Tanaka: Really?
我把你当成学英语的了。wǒ bǎ nǐ dàng chéng xué yīng yǔ de le。I mistook you for an English major.
📝 Notes
……时 — 'When...'
……时 is equivalent to ……的时候 (when...). E.g., 你去办公室时 — 'When you go to the office.'
哪儿啊 — Expressing Negation
哪儿啊 is used in an answer to express negation (denying something). E.g., 哪儿啊，我是学法语的 — 'No, I'm a French major.'
📚 Grammar
把 Sentences (1) — Disposal Construction
The「把」sentence emphasizes the disposal or handling of an object. Pattern: Subject + 把 + Object + Verb + Other Element (showing result or how the object is affected).
把自行车放在楼下的车棚里。bǎ zì xíng chē fàng zài lóu xià de chē péng lǐ。Put the bike in the shed downstairs.
把这篇文章翻译成英文。bǎ zhè piān wén zhāng fān yì chéng yīng wén。Translate this article into English.
你把表放在她的桌子上。nǐ bǎ biǎo fàng zài tā de zhuō zi shàng。Put the form on her desk.
把 Sentences — Key Features
(1) The object of「把」must be definite/specific. (2) The verb cannot be bare — it must have a complement or other element. (3) Modal verbs and negators go BEFORE「把」.
你把自行车放在哪儿了？nǐ bǎ zì xíng chē fàng zài nǎ ér le？Where did you put your bike?
我把你当成学英语的了。wǒ bǎ nǐ dàng chéng xué yīng yǔ de le。I mistook you for an English major.
