Dialogue 1
小叶：刚才你去哪儿了?xiǎo yè： gāng cái nǐ qù nǎ ér le?Xiaoye: Where did you go just now?
莉莉：我跟直美逛商店去了。lì lì： wǒ gēn zhí měi guàng shāng diàn qù le。Lily: I went shopping with Naomi.
小叶：你买东西了吗?xiǎo yè： nǐ mǎi dōng xī le ma?Xiaoye: Did you buy anything?
莉莉：我没买东西，直美买了。lì lì： wǒ méi mǎi dōng xī， zhí měi mǎi le。Lily: I didn't buy anything, but Naomi did.
小叶：她买了什么东西?xiǎo yè： tā mǎi le shén me dōng xī?Xiaoye: What did she buy?
莉莉：她买了三件衬衣、两条裙子，还有一顶帽子。lì lì： tā mǎi le sān jiàn chèn yī、 liǎng tiáo qún zi， hái yǒu yī dǐng mào zi。Lily: She bought three shirts, two skirts, and a hat.
小叶：嗬，买了这么多!xiǎo yè： hē， mǎi le zhè me duō!Xiaoye: Wow, that's a lot!

📝 Notes
那还用说 — 'That Goes Without Saying'
那还用说 is a colloquial expression used as an affirmative answer. It has the same meaning as 当然了 (of course).
了 Indicating Change
In the sentence 听说小雨病了 (I heard Xiaoyu is sick), the modal particle 了 indicates that the situation has changed — he wasn't sick before, but now he is.
📚 Grammar
The Aspect Particle 了 — Completed Action
The particle「了」after a verb shows the action is finished or completed. This is different from the modal「了」which indicates a change of state. The negative is「没(有)+Verb」(omit 了).
刚才我去商店了。gāng cái wǒ qù shāng diàn le。I went to the store just now.
我买了一条裙子。wǒ mǎi le yī tiáo qún zi。I bought a skirt.
昨天你们在全聚德吃了烤鸭吗？zuó tiān nǐ men zài quán jù dé chī le kǎo yā ma？Did you eat Peking duck at Quanjude yesterday?
一……就…… — 'As Soon As'
This structure means "as soon as A happens, then B happens." The pattern is「一 + Verb₁ + 就 + Verb₂」.
一下课我就去食堂。yī xià kè wǒ jiù qù shí táng。As soon as class ends, I'll go to the cafeteria.
一回家他就看电视。yī huí jiā tā jiù kàn diàn shì。As soon as he gets home, he watches TV.
Modal Verbs 能 & 可以 — Ability and Permission
「能」and「可以」express ability or permission. 「不能」is used for both negative forms (inability or prohibition). 「不可以」is only used for prohibition.
你能帮我买瓶水吗？nǐ néng bāng wǒ mǎi píng shuǐ ma？Can you help me buy a bottle of water?
上课的时候不能说话。shàng kè de shí hòu bù néng shuō huà。You can't talk during class.
这件衣服我可以试试吗？zhè jiàn yī fú wǒ kě yǐ shì shì ma？Can I try on this piece of clothing?
