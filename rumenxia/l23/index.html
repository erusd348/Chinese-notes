Dialogue 1
小叶：喂，是留学生宿舍吗?xiǎo yè： wèi， shì liú xué shēng sù shè ma?Xiaoye: Hello, is this the international student dorm?
一位小姐：你打错了。yī wèi xiǎo jiě： nǐ dǎ cuò le。A lady: You've dialed the wrong number.
小叶：喂，我找莉莉。xiǎo yè： wèi， wǒ zhǎo lì lì。Xiaoye: Hello, I'm looking for Lily.
莉莉：我就是。lì lì： wǒ jiù shì。Lily: Speaking.
你是小叶吧 ?nǐ shì xiǎo yè ba?You're Xiaoye, right?
小叶：猜对了。xiǎo yè： cāi duì le。Xiaoye: You got it.
昨天你去哪儿了?zuó tiān nǐ qù nǎ ér le?Where did you go yesterday?
我找了你一天也没找到。wǒ zhǎo le nǐ yī tiān yě méi zhǎo dào。I looked for you all day but couldn't find you.
莉莉：我去朋友那儿了，一直待到十点才回来。lì lì： wǒ qù péng yǒu nà ér le， yì zhí dài dào shí diǎn cái huí lái。Lily: I went to a friend's place and didn't come back until 10.
你找我有什么事?nǐ zhǎo wǒ yǒu shén me shì?What did you want to see me about?
小叶：你要的书我给你准备好了，你什么时候来取?xiǎo yè： nǐ yào de shū wǒ gěi nǐ zhǔn bèi hǎo le， nǐ shén me shí hòu lái qǔ?Xiaoye: I've got the book you wanted ready. When will you come get it?
莉莉：太好了 ! 我一吃完晚饭就去。lì lì： tài hǎo le! wǒ yī chī wán wǎn fàn jiù qù。Lily: Great! I'll go as soon as I finish dinner.
Dialogue 2
英男：请问，田中在吗?yīng nán： qǐng wèn， tián zhōng zài ma?Yingnan: Excuse me, is Tanaka there?
同屋：他不在。tóng wū： tā bù zài。Roommate: He's not here.
您是哪位 ?nín shì nǎ wèi?Who's calling, please?
英男：我是李英男，麻烦您转告他一件事,行吗?yīng nán： wǒ shì lǐ yīng nán， má fán nín zhuǎn gào tā yī jiàn shì, xíng ma?Yingnan: I'm Li Yingnan. Could you please pass a message to him?
同屋：没问题, 你说吧。tóng wū： méi wèn tí, nǐ shuō ba。Roommate: No problem. Go ahead.
英男：去大同的火车票买到了，星期五晚上九点半的。yīng nán： qù dà tóng de huǒ chē piào mǎi dào le， xīng qī wǔ wǎn shàng jiǔ diǎn bàn de。Yingnan: The train ticket to Datong has been bought — Friday night at 9:30.
同屋：对不起，我没听清楚，请再说一遍，好吗?tóng wū： duì bù qǐ， wǒ méi tīng qīng chǔ， qǐng zài shuō yī biàn， hǎo ma?Roommate: Sorry, I didn't catch that. Could you repeat it?
Dialogue 3
小雨：你看见保罗了没有?xiǎo yǔ： nǐ kàn jiàn bǎo luó le méi yǒu?Xiaoyu: Have you seen Paul?
我有急事要找他。wǒ yǒu jí shì yào zhǎo tā。I need to talk to him urgently.
英男：刚才我看见他往南门那边走了。yīng nán： gāng cái wǒ kàn jiàn tā wǎng nán mén nà biān zǒu le。Yingnan: I saw him heading toward the south gate.
小雨：你知道他去哪儿了吗?xiǎo yǔ： nǐ zhī dào tā qù nǎ ér le ma?Xiaoyu: Do you know where he went?
英男：不知道。yīng nán： bù zhī dào。Yingnan: I don't know.
他没看见我。tā méi kàn jiàn wǒ。He didn't see me.
我叫他，他也没听见。wǒ jiào tā， tā yě méi tīng jiàn。I called him, but he didn't hear me.
小雨：你记住他的手机号了吗?xiǎo yǔ： nǐ jì zhù tā de shǒu jī hào le ma?Xiaoyu: Do you remember his phone number?
我得给他打个电话。wǒ dé gěi tā dǎ gè diàn huà。I need to call him.
小雨：他关机了。xiǎo yǔ： tā guān jī le。Xiaoyu: His phone is off.
英男：那你就给他发个短信。yīng nán： nà nǐ jiù gěi tā fā gè duǎn xìn。Yingnan: Then send him a text.
他一开机就看到了。tā yī kāi jī jiù kàn dào le。He'll see it as soon as he turns it on.

📚 Grammar
Complement of Result (结果补语)
A result complement tells the outcome of an action. Common result complements: 到 (reach/succeed), 见 (perceive), 完 (finish), 清楚 (clearly), 懂 (understand). Negative: 没 + Verb + Result.
你找了我一天也没找到。nǐ zhǎo le wǒ yī tiān yě méi zhǎo dào。You looked all day but didn't find me.
我没听清楚，请再说一遍。wǒ méi tīng qīng chǔ， qǐng zài shuō yī biàn。I didn't hear clearly. Please say it again.
你看见保罗了吗？nǐ kàn jiàn bǎo luó le ma？Did you see Paul?
他听见了。tā tīng jiàn le。He heard it.
Directional Complement 过来 — Turning Around
「过来」as a directional complement means turning something toward the speaker or changing direction/state.
你什么时候来取？nǐ shén me shí hòu lái qǔ？When will you come pick it up?
我一吃完晚饭就去。wǒ yī chī wán wǎn fàn jiù qù。I'll go as soon as I finish dinner.
