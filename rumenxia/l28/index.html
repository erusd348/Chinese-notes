💬 Dialogue
Xiaoyu: Why is it so cold?Dialogue 1
小雨：怎么这么冷 ?xiǎo yǔ： zěn me zhè me lěng?Xiaoyu: Why is it so cold?
快把空调关上吧。kuài bǎ kōng tiáo guān shàng ba。Turn off the AC, quick!
小雨：我可能有点儿发烧。xiǎo yǔ： wǒ kě néng yǒu diǎn ér fā shāo。Xiaoyu: I think I have a fever.
姐姐：哟，这么烫! 赶快把衣服穿上，我陪你去医院。jiě jiě： yō， zhè me tàng! gǎn kuài bǎ yī fú chuān shàng， wǒ péi nǐ qù yī yuàn。Sister: Wow, you're burning up! Put on your clothes — I'll go to the hospital with you.
小雨：我不愿意去，我最怕打针。xiǎo yǔ： wǒ bù yuàn yì qù， wǒ zuì pà dǎ zhēn。Xiaoyu: I don't want to go. I'm terrified of needles.
姐姐：那怎么行呢 ?jiě jiě： nà zěn me xíng ne?Sister: How can you not go?
Doctor: What's wrong?Dialogue 2
大夫：怎么了?dà fū： zěn me le?Doctor: What's wrong?
哪儿不舒服?nǎ ér bù shū fú?Where does it hurt?
小雨：昨天开始头疼、嗓子疼，还有点儿咳嗽。xiǎo yǔ： zuó tiān kāi shǐ tóu téng、 sǎng zi téng， hái yǒu diǎn ér ké sòu。Xiaoyu: Since yesterday, I've had a headache, sore throat, and a bit of a cough.
大夫：先量一下儿体温，把体温表放好。dà fū： xiān liàng yī xià ér tǐ wēn， bǎ tǐ wēn biǎo fàng hǎo。Doctor: First, let's take your temperature. Put the thermometer under your arm.
大夫：三十八度八。( liàng wán hòu， bǎ tǐ wēn biǎo jiāo gěi dà fū ) dà fū： sān shí bā dù bā。Doctor: 38.8 degrees.
请把嘴张开，我看看。qǐng bǎ zuǐ zhāng kāi， wǒ kàn kàn。Open your mouth please. Let me have a look.
小雨：大夫，我不想打针。xiǎo yǔ： dà fū， wǒ bù xiǎng dǎ zhēn。Xiaoyu: Doctor, I don't want an injection.
大夫：那我给你开点儿药，要按时吃。dà fū： nà wǒ gěi nǐ kāi diǎn ér yào， yào àn shí chī。Doctor: Then I'll prescribe some medicine. Make sure to take it on time.
平时要多喝点儿水。píng shí yào duō hē diǎn ér shuǐ。Drink plenty of water in general.
Sister: I advise you to smoke less. Smoking is bad for your health.Dialogue 3
姐姐：我劝你少抽点儿烟，抽烟对身体没有好处。jiě jiě： wǒ quàn nǐ shǎo chōu diǎn ér yān， chōu yān duì shēn tǐ méi yǒu hǎo chù。Sister: I advise you to smoke less. Smoking is bad for your health.
小雨：哎呀，又来了，你已经说了八百遍了。xiǎo yǔ： āi yā， yòu lái le， nǐ yǐ jīng shuō le bā bǎi biàn le。Xiaoyu: Oh, here we go again! You've said it a thousand times.
姐姐：要是你把烟戒了，我就不说了。jiě jiě： yào shì nǐ bǎ yān jiè le， wǒ jiù bù shuō le。Sister: If you quit smoking, I'll stop saying it.
今天取回来的药呢?jīn tiān qǔ huí lái de yào ne?Where's the medicine we got today?
小雨：我已经把它吃了。xiǎo yǔ： wǒ yǐ jīng bǎ tā chī le。Xiaoyu: I already took it.
姐姐：那就把电视关上，早点儿睡觉吧。jiě jiě： nà jiù bǎ diàn shì guān shàng， zǎo diǎn ér shuì jiào ba。Sister: Then turn off the TV and go to bed early.
📝 Notes
把字句 (2) — The 把 Sentence Continued
A more advanced use of the「把」sentence. When the complement indicates a change of location or state,「把」is often required. For example: 快把空调关上 (Turn off the AC quickly). The object is placed before the verb for emphasis.
