💬 Dialogue
Dialogue 1
秋天了，天气凉了，山上的树叶都红了。qiū tiān le， tiān qì liáng le， shān shàng de shù yè dōu hóng le。It's autumn. The weather is cool, and the leaves on the hills have all turned red.
西蒙 他们打算今天去香山看红叶，可是真不巧，天阴了，要下雨了。xī méng tā men dǎ suàn jīn tiān qù xiāng shān kàn hóng yè， kě shì zhēn bù qiǎo， tiān yīn le， yào xià yǔ le。Simon and his friends plan to go to Fragrant Hills today to see the red leaves, but sadly, it's cloudy and it's about to rain.
Dialogue 2
小雨现在是职员了，工作很忙，玩儿的时间少了，也不能睡懒觉了。xiǎo yǔ xiàn zài shì zhí yuán le， gōng zuò hěn máng， wán ér de shí jiān shǎo le， yě bù néng shuì lǎn jiào le。Xiaoyu is an employee now. He's busy with work, has less time to play, and can't sleep in anymore.
前几天他病了，现在已经好了。qián jǐ tiān tā bìng le， xiàn zài yǐ jīng hǎo le。He was sick a few days ago, but now he's recovered.
最近他有点儿瘦了，不过看上去更帅了。zuì jìn tā yǒu diǎn ér shòu le， bù guò kàn shàng qù gèng shuài le。He's gotten a bit thinner lately, but he looks even more handsome.
Dialogue 3
现在十一点五十了，要下课了。xiàn zài shí yì diǎn wǔ shí le， yào xià kè le。It's 11:50 now. Class is about to end.
下课以后，西蒙打算 先去食堂吃饭，然后去商店。xià kè yǐ hòu， xī méng dǎ suàn xiān qù shí táng chī fàn， rán hòu qù shāng diàn。After class, Simon plans to eat at the cafeteria first, then go to the store.
他下星期就要回国了， 他要给家里人和亲戚朋友买点儿礼物。tā xià xīng qī jiù yào huí guó le， tā yào gěi jiā lǐ rén hé qīn qī péng yǒu mǎi diǎn ér lǐ wù。He's going back home next week, so he wants to buy gifts for his family and friends.

📚 Grammar
Modal Particle 了 — Change of Situation
The modal particle「了」at the end of a sentence indicates a change of state or that a new situation has arisen. This is different from the aspect particle「了」which marks completed action.
秋天了，天气凉了。qiū tiān le， tiān qì liáng le。It's autumn — the weather has turned cool.
现在他的病已经好了。xiàn zài tā de bìng yǐ jīng hǎo le。His illness is already better now.
今年我二十一岁了。jīn nián wǒ èr shí yī suì le。I'm 21 years old this year.
要……了 — 'About To' Happen
「要……了」indicates something is about to happen or change soon. You can add「就」or「快」before「要」to add urgency:「就要……了」/「快要……了」/「快……了」.
要下课了。yào xià kè le。Class is about to end.
他就要回国了。tā jiù yào huí guó le。He is about to go back home.
快上课了。kuài shàng kè le。Class is about to start.
能 & 可以 (Part 2) — Possibility & Permission
Besides ability,「能」and「可以」also indicate possibility or permission. The negative form「不能」covers both inability and prohibition (except「不可以」for explicit prohibition).
你能吃辣的吗？nǐ néng chī là de ma？Can you eat spicy food?
病人正在休息，你们不能进去。bìng rén zhèng zài xiū xī， nǐ men bù néng jìn qù。The patient is resting. You can't go in.
