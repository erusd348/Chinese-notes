💬 Dialogue
Dialogue 1
小叶：莉莉呢?xiǎo yè： lì lì ne?Xiaoye: Where's Lily?
直美：她出去了。zhí měi： tā chū qù le。Naomi: She went out.
可能一会儿就回来。kě néng yī huì er jiù huí lái。She'll probably be back soon.
小叶：她到哪儿去了?xiǎo yè： tā dào nǎ ér qù le?Xiaoye: Where did she go?
直美：到书店去了。zhí měi： dào shū diàn qù le。Naomi: She went to the bookstore.
你进来等她吧。nǐ jìn lái děng tā ba。Come in and wait for her.
小叶：你正在学习吧?xiǎo yè： nǐ zhèng zài xué xí ba?Xiaoye: You're studying, right?
不妨碍你吗?bù fáng ài nǐ ma?Am I disturbing you?
直美：没关系，快进屋来吧。zhí měi： méi guān xì， kuài jìn wū lái ba。Naomi: It's fine. Come on in.
小叶：那打扰你了。xiǎo yè： nà dǎ rǎo nǐ le。Xiaoye: Sorry for the interruption, then.
Dialogue 2
莉莉：真抱歉，让你久等了。lì lì： zhēn bào qiàn， ràng nǐ jiǔ děng le。Lily: I'm so sorry to keep you waiting.
小叶：没什么。xiǎo yè： méi shén me。Xiaoye: It's nothing.
这是你叫我帮你买的书。zhè shì nǐ jiào wǒ bāng nǐ mǎi de shū。Here's the book you asked me to buy.
莉莉：还麻烦你给我送来，真不好意思。lì lì： hái má fán nǐ gěi wǒ sòng lái， zhēn bù hǎo yì sī。Lily: I'm so sorry you had to bring it over too.
小叶：你太客气了。xiǎo yè： nǐ tài kè qì le。Xiaoye: You're too polite.
莉莉：你看，我又买回来一些书。lì lì： nǐ kàn， wǒ yòu mǎi huí lái yī xiē shū。Lily: Look, I bought some more books.
小叶：这么多，你都准备寄回国去，是吗?xiǎo yè： zhè me duō， nǐ dōu zhǔn bèi jì huí guó qù， shì ma?Xiaoye: So many! Are you going to mail them all back home?
莉莉：是啊，我打算明天就寄回去。lì lì： shì a， wǒ dǎ suàn míng tiān jiù jì huí qù。Lily: Yes, I plan to send them back tomorrow.
小叶：挺重的吧?xiǎo yè： tǐng zhòng de ba?Xiaoye: They're pretty heavy, aren't they?
要我帮忙吗?yào wǒ bāng máng ma?Need any help?
莉莉：不用了。lì lì： bù yòng le。Lily: No, thanks.
Dialogue 3
直美：对不起，打扰你们一下儿。zhí měi： duì bù qǐ， dǎ rǎo nǐ men yī xià ér。Naomi: Sorry to interrupt you for a moment.
莉莉：别客气，你说吧。lì lì： bié kè qì， nǐ shuō ba。Lily: Don't worry about it. Go ahead.
直美：我现在得上楼去，要是田中来了，你就让他等我一下儿。zhí měi： wǒ xiàn zài dé shàng lóu qù， yào shì tián zhōng lái le， nǐ jiù ràng tā děng wǒ yī xià ér。Naomi: I need to go upstairs now. If Tanaka comes, please have him wait a moment.
莉莉：没问题。lì lì： méi wèn tí。Lily: No problem.
直美：告诉他我马上就下来。zhí měi： gào sù tā wǒ mǎ shàng jiù xià lái。Naomi: Tell him I'll be right down.
📝 Notes
呢 for Asking Location
When 呢 is placed after a noun, pronoun, or numeral-classifier compound without context, it asks about location. E.g., 莉莉呢? — 'Where's Lily?'
More examples: 手机呢? (Where's the phone?), 他呢? (Where is he?), 那两个人呢? (Where are those two people?)
让你久等了 — 'Sorry to Keep You Waiting'
让你久等了 is a common way to apologize for having kept someone waiting for a long time.
不好意思 — Expressing Embarrassment / Apology
不好意思 is used to express apology for troubling someone.
E.g., 还麻烦你给我送来，真不好意思 — 'I'm so sorry you had to bring it over too.'
……，是吗? / ……，是不是? — Asking for Confirmation
The pattern ……，是吗? or ……，是不是? is used to express an uncertain estimate and seek confirmation.
If the guess is correct, answer with 是啊 or 对. If not, use 不.
📚 Grammar
Directional Complement (趋向补语) — Simple
Simple directional complements:「来」(toward speaker),「去」(away from speaker). If the object is a place, it must go BEFORE「来/去」.
她出去了。tā chū qù le。She went out.
你进来等她吧。nǐ jìn lái děng tā ba。Come in and wait for her.
他上楼去了。tā shàng lóu qù le。He went upstairs.
Directional Complement (趋向补语) — Compound
Compound directional complements combine: 上/下/进/出/回/过/起 + 来/去. For example: 进来 (come in), 出去 (go out), 回来 (come back), 上去 (go up).
我马上就下来。wǒ mǎ shàng jiù xià lái。I'll come right down.
要是田中来了，你就让他等我一下儿。yào shì tián zhōng lái le， nǐ jiù ràng tā děng wǒ yī xià ér。If Tanaka comes, please have him wait for me.
他又买回来一些书。tā yòu mǎi huí lái yī xiē shū。He bought some more books back.
Pivotal Sentences (兼语句)
A pivotal sentence has an object that is also the subject of the next verb: Subject + Verb₁ + Object/Subject + Verb₂.
麻烦您转告他一件事。má fán nín zhuǎn gào tā yī jiàn shì。Could you please pass a message to him?
我请你吃饭。wǒ qǐng nǐ chī fàn。I invite you to eat.
让他等我一下儿。ràng tā děng wǒ yī xià ér。Tell him to wait for me.
