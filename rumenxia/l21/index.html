📝 Notes
是吗 / 真的吗 — Expressing Surprise
是吗 and 真的吗 are used to show that you didn't know something before and find it a bit surprising or hard to believe.
可……了 — Emphasis
可……了 is used to emphasize a high degree. E.g., 可好听了 — 'It's really nice to listen to.'
不简单 — 'Not Ordinary'
When 简单 means 'ordinary,' it is usually used in the negative form. 你真不简单 — 'You're amazing (lit. not ordinary).'
📚 Grammar
Aspect Particle 过 — Past Experience
The particle「过」after a verb indicates that an action has been experienced in the past. The negative is「没(有)+Verb+过」. Questions: Verb + 过 + Object + 没有?
你去过香山吗？nǐ qù guò xiāng shān ma？Have you been to Fragrant Hills?
来北京以后，我吃过两次烤鸭。lái běi jīng yǐ hòu， wǒ chī guò liǎng cì kǎo yā。I've eaten Peking duck twice since coming to Beijing.
我没听过这张光盘。wǒ méi tīng guò zhè zhāng guāng pán。I haven't listened to this CD.
是……的 — Emphasizing Details of Past Events
「是……的」emphasizes details of a past action: time, place, manner, or person. The verb + 的 structure highlights the specific detail.
我是上个月去的。wǒ shì shàng gè yuè qù de。I went LAST MONTH.
我不是坐出租车去的，是骑车去的。wǒ bú shì zuò chū zū chē qù de， shì qí chē qù de。I didn't go by TAXI — I went by bike.
第一次是在全聚德吃的。dì yī cì shì zài quán jù dé chī de。The first time I ate it was at QUANJUDE.
遍, 次, 一下儿 — Measure Words for Actions
「次」counts times an action happens (general).「遍」counts from beginning to end (through-completion).「一下儿」softens the tone of a request.
我听过好多遍。wǒ tīng guò hǎo duō biàn。I've listened to it many times (from start to finish).
你再说一遍。nǐ zài shuō yī biàn。Please say it again (from the beginning).
你来看一下儿。nǐ lái kàn yī xià ér。Come take a look.
除了……以外 — 'Besides / Except'
「除了……以外」can mean either "besides/in addition to" or "except" depending on context.
除了烤鸭以外，你还吃过哪些好吃的？chú le kǎo yā yǐ wài， nǐ hái chī guò nǎ xiē hǎo chī de？Besides Peking duck, what other tasty things have you tried?
