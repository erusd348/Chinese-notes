💬 Dialogue
Dialogue 1
英男：学习结束后，我打算去旅行。yīng nán： xué xí jié shù hòu， wǒ dǎ suàn qù lǚ xíng。Yingnan: After my studies, I plan to travel.
您说去西安好还是去大同好?nín shuō qù xī ān hǎo hái shì qù dà tóng hǎo?Which do you think is better, Xi'an or Datong?
老师：西安的名胜古迹比大同多，我建议你去西安看看。lǎo shī： xī ān de míng shèng gǔ jì bǐ dà tóng duō， wǒ jiàn yì nǐ qù xī ān kàn kàn。Teacher: Xi'an has more historical sites than Datong. I suggest you visit Xi'an.
英男：我听说西安比北京还热，大同是不是比北京凉快一点儿?yīng nán： wǒ tīng shuō xī ān bǐ běi jīng hái rè， dà tóng shì bú shì bǐ běi jīng liáng kuài yì diǎn ér?Yingnan: I heard Xi'an is even hotter than Beijing. Is Datong a bit cooler?
老师：看天气预报，今年大同也不比北京凉快。lǎo shī： kàn tiān qì yù bào， jīn nián dà tóng yě bù bǐ běi jīng liáng kuài。Teacher: According to the forecast, Datong isn't cooler than Beijing this year either.
英男：西安比大同远得多吧 ?yīng nán： xī ān bǐ dà tóng yuǎn dé duō ba?Yingnan: Xi'an is much farther than Datong, right?
老师：是啊。lǎo shī： shì a。Teacher: Yes.
不过，去西安的车比较多，比去大同更方便。bù guò， qù xī ān de chē bǐ jiào duō， bǐ qù dà tóng gèng fāng biàn。But there are more trains to Xi'an, so it's more convenient.
英男：好，就听您的。yīng nán： hǎo， jiù tīng nín de。Yingnan: Okay, I'll follow your advice.

Dialogue 3
老师：学完以后你有什么打算?lǎo shī： xué wán yǐ hòu nǐ yǒu shén me dǎ suàn?Teacher: What are your plans after you graduate?
英男：我希望在中国找个工作，可是我家里人不同意，我不知道怎么办好。yīng nán： wǒ xī wàng zài zhōng guó zhǎo gè gōng zuò， kě shì wǒ jiā lǐ rén bù tóng yì， wǒ bù zhī dào zěn me bàn hǎo。Yingnan: I hope to find a job in China, but my family doesn't agree. I don't know what to do.
老师：他们是不是觉得在国外生活没有在国内方便?lǎo shī： tā men shì bú shì jué de zài guó wài shēng huó méi yǒu zài guó nèi fāng biàn?Teacher: Do they think living abroad isn't as convenient as at home?
英男：是啊，有些方面确实没有国内方便，但是 我对在这儿工作很感兴趣。yīng nán： shì a， yǒu xiē fāng miàn què shí méi yǒu guó nèi fāng biàn， dàn shì wǒ duì zài zhè ér gōng zuò hěn gǎn xìng qù。Yingnan: Yes, in some ways it's not as convenient, but I'm very interested in working here.
老师：依我看,你还是跟家里人再好好儿商量商量吧。lǎo shī： yī wǒ kàn, nǐ hái shì gēn jiā lǐ rén zài hǎo hǎo ér shāng liáng shāng liáng ba。Teacher: In my opinion, you should talk it over properly with your family.
📝 Notes
听+Pronoun/Noun + 的 — 'Following Someone's Advice'
The pattern 听 + pronoun/noun + 的 means 'to accept someone's advice.' E.g., 就听您的 — 'I'll follow your advice.'
我看 / 依我看 — 'In My Opinion'
我看 and 依我看 mean 'in my opinion' and introduce the speaker's viewpoint.
📚 Grammar
比 — Comparison
The basic comparison pattern: A + 比 + B + Adjective. No degree adverbs (很, 太, 非常) are used before the adjective. For "even more," use「比 + B + 还/更 + Adj」.
西安比大同远得多。xī ān bǐ dà tóng yuǎn dé duō。Xi'an is much farther than Datong.
西安比北京还热。xī ān bǐ běi jīng hái rè。Xi'an is even hotter than Beijing.
他比我壮多了。tā bǐ wǒ zhuàng duō le。He's much stronger than me.
没有 / 不如 — 'Not As... As'
Use「A + 没有 + B + Adj」to say A is not as Adj as B. 「不如」means "not as good as" and is stronger.
大同没有北京热。dà tóng méi yǒu běi jīng rè。Datong is not as hot as Beijing.
A跟B一样 — 'A Is the Same As B'
Use「A + 跟 + B + 一样 + (Adj)」to express equality. Can also be used in negative form.
他跟我一样高。tā gēn wǒ yī yàng gāo。He's the same height as me.
不比 — 'Not Any More Than'
「A + 不比 + B + Adj」means A is not any Adj-er than B (emphasizing "no difference").
今年大同也不比北京凉快。jīn nián dà tóng yě bù bǐ běi jīng liáng kuài。This year Datong isn't any cooler than Beijing either.
