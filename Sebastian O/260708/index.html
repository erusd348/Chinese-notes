📚 Grammar
V 过 (structure) have done sth before ( experiential)
你去过深圳吗？ nǐ qù guò shēn zhèn ma Have you ever been to Shenzhen?
我没去过。 wǒ méi qù guò I haven't been there.
V + 了 (structure) completed action
我付了。 wǒ fù le I paid.
我订了一个房间。 wǒ dìng le yī ge fáng jiān I booked a room.
我吃饭了。 wǒ chī fàn le I have eaten.
A 比 B + Adj (structure) A is more Adj than B
我比她大。 wǒ bǐ tā dà I'm older than her.
她妈妈的饭店离我家很近。 tā mā ma de fàn diàn lí wǒ jiā hěn jìn Her mother's restaurant is very close to my home.
太 + Adj + 了 (structure) too Adj
太贵了，可以便宜一点吗？ tài guì le, kě yǐ pián yi yī diǎn ma Too expensive, can you make it a bit cheaper?
再便宜一点吧！ zài pián yi yī diǎn ba Make it a bit cheaper please!
