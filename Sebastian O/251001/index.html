📖 Vocabulary

💬 Sentences
你周末做什么了？ Nǐ zhōumò zuò shénme le? What did you do over the weekend?
我去我表弟家玩游戏了。 Wǒ qù wǒ biǎodì jiā wán yóuxì le. I went to my cousin’s house to play games.
你那儿现在几点？ Nǐ nàr xiànzài jǐ diǎn? What time is it there now?
我这儿现在（是）晚上7点40（分）。 Wǒ zhèr xiànzài (shì) wǎnshang 7 diǎn 40 fēn. It’s 7:40 PM here now.
你每天下班后做什么？ Nǐ měi tiān xiàbān hòu zuò shénme? What do you do after work every day?
有时候我回家，有时候去健身。 Yǒu shíhou wǒ huí jiā, yǒu shíhou qù jiànshēn. Sometimes I go home, sometimes I go work out.
你常常跟谁一起健身？ Nǐ chángcháng gēn shéi yīqǐ jiànshēn? Who do you often work out with?
我跟我最好的朋友一起健身。 Wǒ gēn wǒ zuì hǎo de péngyou yīqǐ jiànshēn. I work out with my best friend.
