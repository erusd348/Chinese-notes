📚 Grammar
日期表达 asking and telling dates

今天是7月16号。 jīn tiān shì qī yuè shí liù hào Today is July 16th.
今年是哪年？ jīn nián shì nǎ nián What year is it?
今年是2026年。 jīn nián shì èr líng èr liù nián This year is 2026.
你的生日是几月几号？ nǐ de shēng rì shì jǐ yuè jǐ hào What's your birthday?
星期表达 days of the week
今天星期几？ jīn tiān xīng qī jǐ What day is today?

今天星期日。 jīn tiān xīng qī tiān / rì Today is Sunday.
时间表达 telling time
现在几点？ xiàn zài jǐ diǎn What time is it now?
现在8点47分。 xiàn zài bā diǎn sì shí qī fēn It's 8:47 now.
八点零五分 bā diǎn líng wǔ fēn five past eight (8:05)
八点一刻 bā diǎn yī kè quarter past eight (8:15)
八点半 bā diǎn bàn half past eight (8:30)
九点差一刻 jiǔ diǎn chà yī kè quarter to nine (8:45)
