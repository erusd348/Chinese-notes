📚 Grammar
派对 / 楼 / 几楼 party / building / floor
我刚从我朋友的生日派对回来。 wǒ gāng cóng wǒ péng yǒu de shēng rì pài duì huí lái I just came back from my friend's birthday party.
派对在我这个楼。 pài duì zài wǒ zhè gè lóu The party is in my building.
你家在几楼？ nǐ jiā zài jǐ lóu What floor is your home on?
我家在5楼。 wǒ jiā zài wǔ lóu My home is on the 5th floor.
桌游 / 聊天 / 听 / 块 board game / chat / can / slice
在派对上，你们做什么？ zài pài duì shàng, nǐ men zuò shén me What did you do at the party?
我们玩桌游、聊天、吃东西、喝啤酒。 wǒ men wán zhuō yóu, liáo tiān, chī dōng xī, hē pí jiǔ We played board games, chatted, ate food, and drank beer.
我喝了两听啤酒，吃了五块披萨。 wǒ hē le liǎng tīng pí jiǔ, chī le wǔ kuài pī sà I drank two cans of beer and ate five slices of pizza.
我吃饱了。 wǒ chī bǎo le I'm full.
假期 / 刚 / 开始 / 迟到 holiday / just / start / late
今天是假期。 jīn tiān shì jià qī Today is a holiday.
我朋友们刚走了。 wǒ péng yǒu men gāng zǒu le My friends just left.
今天的派对5点开始。 jīn tiān de pài duì wǔ diǎn kāi shǐ Today's party starts at 5.
秘鲁人常常迟到。 bì lǔ rén cháng cháng chí dào Peruvians are often late.
楼上 / 差 / 一十 upstairs / to / ten
我9点差5分上楼。 wǒ jiǔ diǎn chà wǔ fēn shàng lóu I went upstairs at 5 to 9.
9点一十 = 9点10分 jiǔ diǎn yī shí = jiǔ diǎn shí fēn 9:10
懂 / 最后一天 understand / last day
我懂了。 wǒ dǒng le I got it.
你几月几号是最后一天在西门子工作？ nǐ jǐ yuè jǐ hào shì zuì hòu yī tiān zài xī mén zǐ gōng zuò What date is your last day working at Siemens?
8月14号是最后一天在西门子工作。 bā yuè shí sì hào shì zuì hòu yī tiān zài xī mén zǐ gōng zuò August 14th is the last day working at Siemens.
