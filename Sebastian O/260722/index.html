📖 Vocabulary
星期天/日 xīng qī tiān/rì Sunday
周天 zhōu tiān Sunday (another term)
睡觉 shuì jiào to sleep
上床 shàng chuáng to go to bed
起床 qǐ chuáng to get up, wake up
上班 shàng bān to go to work
下班 xià bān to finish work, get off work
小时 xiǎo shí hour
从 cóng from
到 dào to
休息 xiū xi to rest
出发 chū fā to set out, depart
到 dào to arrive
上飞机 shàng fēi jī to board a plane
前天 qián tiān the day before yesterday
昨天 zuó tiān yesterday
今天 jīn tiān today
明天 míng tiān tomorrow
后天 hòu tiān the day after tomorrow
📚 Grammar
星期天 / 周天 Sunday
星期天我在爸妈家看足球比赛。 xīng qī tiān wǒ zài bà mā jiā kàn zú qiú bǐ sài On Sunday I watch football matches at my parents' house.
睡觉 / 上床 / 起床 sleep / go to bed / wake up
我11点上床，12点睡觉。 wǒ shí yī diǎn shàng chuáng, shí èr diǎn shuì jiào I go to bed at 11 and sleep at 12.
你什么时候起床？ nǐ shén me shí hou qǐ chuáng When do you wake up?
我8点起床。我睡8个小时。 wǒ bā diǎn qǐ chuáng. wǒ shuì bā gè xiǎo shí I wake up at 8. I sleep 8 hours.
上班 / 下班 go to work / finish work
你几点上班？ nǐ jǐ diǎn shàng bān What time do you go to work?
我9点上班，5点下班。 wǒ jiǔ diǎn shàng bān, wǔ diǎn xià bān I go to work at 9 and finish at 5.
从...到... from...to...
你从几点到几点工作？ nǐ cóng jǐ diǎn dào jǐ diǎn gōng zuò From when to when do you work?
我从上午8点到晚上10点工作。 wǒ cóng shàng wǔ bā diǎn dào wǎn shàng shí diǎn gōng zuò I work from 8 AM to 10 PM.
我从12点到1点休息。 wǒ cóng shí èr diǎn dào yī diǎn xiū xi I rest from 12 to 1.
去中国 / 出发 / 到 going to China / depart / arrive
你什么时候去中国？ nǐ shén me shí hou qù zhōng guó When are you going to China?
我8月21号出发，23号到中国。 wǒ bā yuè èr shí yī hào chū fā, èr shí sān hào dào zhōng guó I depart on August 21st and arrive in China on the 23rd.
你21号几点上飞机？ nǐ èr shí yī hào jǐ diǎn shàng fēi jī What time do you board the plane on the 21st?
我上午9点20上飞机。 wǒ shàng wǔ jiǔ diǎn èr shí shàng fēi jī I board the plane at 9:20 AM.
前天 / 昨天 / 今天 / 明天 / 后天 days relative to today
前天我去了公司。 qián tiān wǒ qù le gōng sī The day before yesterday I went to the company.
昨天是星期天。 zuó tiān shì xīng qī tiān Yesterday was Sunday.
今天星期三。 jīn tiān xīng qī sān Today is Wednesday.
明天我要考试。 míng tiān wǒ yào kǎo shì Tomorrow I have an exam.
后天我休息。 hòu tiān wǒ xiū xi The day after tomorrow I rest.
