📚 Grammar
辞职 resigning
明天我跟我老板说我要辞职。 míng tiān wǒ gēn wǒ lǎo bǎn shuō wǒ yào cí zhí Tomorrow I'll tell my boss I want to quit.
我跟老板说后，我要工作一个月。 wǒ gēn lǎo bǎn shuō hòu, wǒ yào gōng zuò yī ge yuè After I tell my boss, I'll work for one more month.
