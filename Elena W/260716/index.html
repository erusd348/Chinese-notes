📖 Vocabulary

📚 Grammar
想 + V (structure) want to do
你想吃饭吗？ nǐ xiǎng chī fàn ma Do you want to eat?
我想吃。 wǒ xiǎng chī I want to eat.
你想吃什么？ nǐ xiǎng chī shén me What do you want to eat?
我想吃面条。 wǒ xiǎng chī miàn tiáo I want to eat noodles.
喜欢 + V (structure) like to do
你喜欢吃什么？ nǐ xǐ huan chī shén me What do you like to eat?
我喜欢吃菠萝。 wǒ xǐ huan chī bō luó I like to eat pineapple.
