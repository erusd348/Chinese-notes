📚 Grammar
了 / 没 了 = completed action marker; 没 = didn't (negates completed actions); 不 = don't (present/habitual)
💬 Sentences
走开！ zǒu kāi Go away!
你吃饭了吗？ nǐ chī fàn le ma Have you eaten?
我吃饭了。 wǒ chī fàn le I have eaten.
我没吃饭。 wǒ méi chī fàn I haven't eaten.
我不吃饭。 wǒ bù chī fàn I don't eat (rice/meals).
你吃了什么？ nǐ chī le shén me What did you eat?
我吃了牛肉面。 wǒ chī le niú ròu miàn I ate beef noodles.
我吃了鸡肉沙拉。 wǒ chī le jī ròu shā lā I ate chicken salad.
你早上吃什么？ nǐ zǎo shang chī shén me What do you eat in the morning?
我不吃早饭。 wǒ bù chī zǎo fàn I don't eat breakfast.
你中午吃什么？ nǐ zhōng wǔ chī shén me What do you eat at noon?
我中午吃猪肉。 wǒ zhōng wǔ chī zhū ròu I eat pork at noon.
你吃晚饭了吗？ nǐ chī wǎn fàn le ma Have you eaten dinner?
我没吃晚饭。 wǒ méi chī wǎn fàn I haven't eaten dinner.
你晚上吃什么？ nǐ wǎn shang chī shén me What do you eat in the evening?
我晚上吃鸡肉、冰淇淋，喝牛奶。 wǒ wǎn shang chī jī ròu, bīng qí lín, hē niú nǎi In the evening I eat chicken, ice cream, and drink milk.
