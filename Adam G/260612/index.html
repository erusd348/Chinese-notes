什么 shén me what
你爱吃泰国菜吗？ nǐ ài chī tài guó cài ma? Do you like eating Thai food?
你爱吃什么？ nǐ ài chī shén me? What do you like to eat?
我爱吃泰国菜。 wǒ ài chī tài guó cài. I like eating Thai food.
什么很好吃？ shén me hěn hǎo chī? What is delicious?
泰国菜很好吃。 tài guó cài hěn hǎo chī. Thai food is delicious.
