📚 Grammar
Number + 个 + Noun using 个 as general measure word
1个人。 yī ge rén One person.
两个人。 liǎng ge rén Two people.
一个包子。 yī ge bāo zi One steamed bun.
一个面包。 yī ge miàn bāo One loaf of bread.
一个本子。 yī ge běn zi One notebook.
本 measure word for books
一本书。 yī běn shū One book.
两本词典。 liǎng běn cí diǎn Two dictionaries.
杯 measure word for cup of drink
一杯咖啡。 yī bēi kā fēi A cup of coffee.
两杯啤酒。 liǎng bēi pí jiǔ Two glasses of beer.
两杯可乐。 liǎng bēi kě lè Two glasses of cola.
三杯水。 sān bēi shuǐ Three glasses of water.
我喝了一杯咖啡。 wǒ hē le yī bēi kā fēi I drank a cup of coffee.
我吃了一个冰淇淋。 wǒ chī le yī ge bīng qí lín I ate an ice cream.
瓶 measure word for bottles
一瓶水。 yī píng shuǐ A bottle of water.
两瓶啤酒。 liǎng píng pí jiǔ Two bottles of beer.
我喝了三瓶啤酒。 wǒ hē le sān píng pí jiǔ I drank three bottles of beer.
碗 measure word for bowls
一碗饭。 yī wǎn fàn A bowl of rice.
两碗面条。 liǎng wǎn miàn tiáo Two bowls of noodles.
我吃一碗饭。 wǒ chī yī wǎn fàn I eat one bowl of rice.
盘 measure word for plates
一盘牛肉。 yī pán niú ròu A plate of beef.
你早上吃什么？ nǐ zǎo shang chī shén me What do you eat in the morning?
我早上吃一杯酸奶、两个鸡蛋和一个面包，喝一杯牛奶咖啡。 wǒ zǎo shang chī yī bēi suān nǎi, liǎng ge jī dàn hé yī ge miàn bāo, hē yī bēi niú nǎi kā fēi I eat yogurt, two eggs, and bread, and drink a milk coffee.
他一天喝五杯咖啡。 tā yī tiān hē wǔ bēi kā fēi He drinks five cups of coffee a day.
明天见。 míng tiān jiàn See you tomorrow.
晚上见。 wǎn shang jiàn See you in the evening.
