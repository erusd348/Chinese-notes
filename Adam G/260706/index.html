📖 Vocabulary
哪儿 nǎr where
在 zài at; in; on (location marker) / (progressive marker)
去 qù to go
家 jiā home; family
办公室 bàn gōng shì office
办公 bàn gōng to do office work
室友 shì yǒu roommate
食堂 shí táng cafeteria; canteen
宿舍 sù shè dormitory
关系 guān xì relationship
东西 dōng xi thing; stuff
出去 chū qù to go out
朋友 péng you friend
📚 Grammar
S 在 + place + V (structure) do something at a place
我在家吃饭。 wǒ zài jiā chī fàn I eat at home.
我在饭店吃饭。 wǒ zài fàn diàn chī fàn I eat at a restaurant.
我在超市买东西。 wǒ zài chāo shì mǎi dōng xi I buy things at the supermarket.
我在酒吧喝啤酒。 wǒ zài jiǔ bā hē pí jiǔ I drink beer at the bar.
我在书店读书。 wǒ zài shū diàn dú shū I read at the bookstore.
我在公园散步。 wǒ zài gōng yuán sàn bù I take a walk in the park.
我在健身房健身。 wǒ zài jiàn shēn fáng jiàn shēn I work out at the gym.
我在办公室办公。 wǒ zài bàn gōng shì bàn gōng I do office work in the office.
我今天在家工作。 wǒ jīn tiān zài jiā gōng zuò I work from home today.
S 在 V (structure) be doing something (progressive)
你在做什么？ nǐ zài zuò shén me What are you doing?
我在吃饭。 wǒ zài chī fàn I am eating.
我在健身。 wǒ zài jiàn shēn I am working out.
我在散步。 wǒ zài sàn bù I am taking a walk.
去哪儿 where to go
你们去哪儿了？ nǐ men qù nǎr le Where did you guys go?
我和我的朋友们去upstate了。 wǒ hé wǒ de péng you men qù upstate le I went upstate with my friends.
我今天不出去。 wǒ jīn tiān bù chū qù I'm not going out today.
我今天也没出去。 wǒ jīn tiān yě méi chū qù I didn't go out today either.
在宿舍，我有两个室友。 zài sù shè, wǒ yǒu liǎng ge shì yǒu In the dorm, I have two roommates.
你和你室友的关系好吗？ nǐ hé nǐ shì yǒu de guān xì hǎo ma Is your relationship with your roommate good?
我们关系很好。我们今天也是好朋友。 wǒ men guān xì hěn hǎo. wǒ men jīn tiān yě shì hǎo péng you We have a good relationship. We are still good friends today.
我没有室友。 wǒ méi yǒu shì yǒu I don't have a roommate.
我女朋友是我室友。 wǒ nǚ péng you shì wǒ shì yǒu My girlfriend is my roommate.
