Time + Place + Action Time-Place-Action word order

你周末吃冰淇淋了吗？ nǐ zhōumò chī bīngqílín le ma? Did you eat ice cream over the weekend?
是的，我吃冰淇淋了。 shìde, wǒ chī bīngqílín le. Yes, I ate ice cream.
你喜欢吃什么面？ nǐ xǐhuān chī shénme miàn? What kind of noodles do you like?
我喜欢吃鸡肉面。 wǒ xǐhuān chī jīròumiàn. I like eating chicken noodle soup.
你早上喜欢喝什么？ nǐ zǎoshang xǐhuān hē shénme? What do you like to drink in the morning?
我早上喜欢喝咖啡和水。 wǒ zǎoshang xǐhuān hē kāfēi hé shuǐ. I like to drink coffee and water in the morning.
你喜欢喝什么咖啡？ nǐ xǐhuān hē shénme kāfēi? What kind of coffee do you like?
我喜欢喝黑咖啡和卡布奇诺。 wǒ xǐhuān hē hēi kāfēi hé kǎbùqínuò. I like black coffee and cappuccino.
你爱喝什么酒？ nǐ ài hē shénme jiǔ? What kind of alcohol do you love to drink?
我爱喝啤酒。 wǒ ài hē píjiǔ. I love drinking beer.
我不爱喝啤酒。 wǒ bú ài hē píjiǔ. I don't love drinking beer.
我不喝酒。 wǒ bù hē jiǔ. I don't drink alcohol.
酒不好喝。 jiǔ bù hǎohē. Alcohol doesn't taste good.
