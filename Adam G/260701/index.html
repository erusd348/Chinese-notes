📚 Grammar
去 + Place + V go to a place to do something
我今天要去超市买菜。 wǒ jīn tiān yào qù chāo shì mǎi cài I'm going to the supermarket to buy groceries today.
我去书店买书。 wǒ qù shū diàn mǎi shū I go to the bookstore to buy books.
我去咖啡店喝咖啡。 wǒ qù kā fēi diàn hē kā fēi I go to the cafe to drink coffee.
我去饭店吃饭。 wǒ qù fàn diàn chī fàn I go to the restaurant to eat.
我去酒店睡觉。 wǒ qù jiǔ diàn shuì jiào I go to the hotel to sleep.
我今天要去健身房健身。 wǒ jīn tiān yào qù jiàn shēn fáng jiàn shēn I'm going to the gym to work out today.
我今晚要去酒吧喝啤酒。 wǒ jīn wǎn yào qù jiǔ bā hē pí jiǔ I'm going to the bar to drink beer tonight.
我去公园散步。 wǒ qù gōng yuán sàn bù I go to the park for a stroll.
