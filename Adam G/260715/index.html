📖 Vocabulary
走 zǒu to walk, to leave
做 zuò to make, to cook, to do
来 lái to come
介绍 jiè shào to introduce
高兴 gāo xìng happy
高 gāo tall, high
难过 nán guò sad
认识 rèn shi to know, to recognize
见 jiàn to meet
📚 Grammar
走 walk / leave to walk / to leave
我去公园走了一下。 wǒ qù gōng yuán zǒu le yī xià I walked in the park for a bit.
我们走吧！ wǒ men zǒu ba Let's go!
我们去公园吧！ wǒ men qù gōng yuán ba Let's go to the park!
他们昨天走了。 tā men zuó tiān zǒu le They left yesterday.
V 一下 V a little bit
我想一下。 wǒ xiǎng yī xià Let me think for a moment.
你看一下这个。 nǐ kàn yī xià zhè ge Take a look at this.
请在这儿坐一下。 qǐng zài zhèr zuò yī xià Please sit here for a moment.
听一下。 tīng yī xià Listen for a moment.
请你说一下。 qǐng nǐ shuō yī xià Please say it.
写一下这个字。 xiě yī xià zhè ge zì Write this character.
来 + V come and V
我爸妈来看我了。 wǒ bà mā lái kàn wǒ le My parents came to see me.
你来一下。 nǐ lái yī xià Come here for a moment.
你来说一下！ nǐ lái shuō yī xià Come and say it!
你来读一下！ nǐ lái dú yī xià Come and read it!
做 / 来 to do / to come
我来做饭。 wǒ lái zuò fàn I'll cook.
我来买。 wǒ lái mǎi I'll buy (it).
我来！ wǒ lái I'll do it!
介绍 to introduce
我来介绍一下。 wǒ lái jiè shào yī xià Let me introduce.
高 / 高兴 tall / happy
我很高。 wǒ hěn gāo I am tall.
你多高？ nǐ duō gāo How tall are you?
我178厘米。 wǒ yī bǎi qī shí bā lí mǐ I'm 178 cm.
难过 sad
他很难过。 tā hěn nán guò He is very sad.
为什么你很难过？ wèi shén me nǐ hěn nán guò Why are you sad?
认识 / 见 to know / to meet
我不认识他。 wǒ bù rèn shi tā I don't know him.
我很高兴认识你。 wǒ hěn gāo xìng rèn shi nǐ Nice to meet you.
我很高兴见到你。 wǒ hěn gāo xìng jiàn dào nǐ Nice to meet you (face to face).
我去咖啡店见我朋友。 wǒ qù kā fēi diàn jiàn wǒ péng yǒu I went to the café to meet my friend.
我在咖啡店见到了我朋友。 wǒ zài kā fēi diàn jiàn dào le wǒ péng yǒu I met my friend at the café.
