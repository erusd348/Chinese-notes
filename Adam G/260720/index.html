📚 Grammar
留学生 / 学生 student / international student
他是留学生。 tā shì liú xué shēng He is an international student.
我们班有十个学生。 wǒ men bān yǒu shí gè xué shēng Our class has ten students.
上班 / 下班 / 上课 / 下课 work / class schedules
我早上八点上班。 wǒ zǎo shang bā diǎn shàng bān I go to work at 8 AM.
他下午五点下班。 tā xià wǔ wǔ diǎn xià bān He finishes work at 5 PM.
我们早上九点上课。 wǒ men zǎo shang jiǔ diǎn shàng kè We have class at 9 AM.
你们中午下课。 nǐ men zhōng wǔ xià kè You finish class at noon.
学（习）+ 语言 to study a language
我在学中文。 wǒ zài xué zhōng wén I am learning Chinese.
德国人说德语。 dé guó rén shuō dé yǔ Germans speak German.
法国人说法语。 fǎ guó rén shuō fǎ yǔ French people speak French.
韩国人说韩语。 hán guó rén shuō hán yǔ Koreans speak Korean.
因为 because
我在学中文，因为我想和女朋友的爸妈说中文。 wǒ zài xué zhōng wén, yīn wèi wǒ xiǎng hé nǚ péng yǒu de bà mā shuō zhōng wén I'm learning Chinese because I want to speak Chinese with my girlfriend's parents.
因为他生病了，所以没来上课。 yīn wèi tā shēng bìng le, suǒ yǐ méi lái shàng kè Because he's sick, he didn't come to class.
📖 课文：自我介绍 text: self-introduction
我叫亚当，我是美国人，我今年34。 wǒ jiào yà dāng, wǒ shì měi guó rén, wǒ jīn nián sān shí sì My name is Adam, I'm American, I'm 34.
这是我女朋友，她叫王婷，她是台湾人，她今年30。 zhè shì wǒ nǚ péng yǒu, tā jiào wáng tíng, tā shì tái wān rén, tā jīn nián sān shí This is my girlfriend, her name is Wang Ting, she's from Taiwan, she's 30.
我喜欢听电子乐、打篮球、滑雪和吃好吃的。 wǒ xǐ huān tīng diàn zǐ yuè, dǎ lán qiú, huá xuě hé chī hǎo chī de I like listening to electronic music, playing basketball, skiing, and eating good food.
我在学中文，因为我想和我女朋友的爸妈说中文。 wǒ zài xué zhōng wén, yīn wèi wǒ xiǎng hé wǒ nǚ péng yǒu de bà mā shuō zhōng wén I'm learning Chinese because I want to speak Chinese with my girlfriend's parents.
