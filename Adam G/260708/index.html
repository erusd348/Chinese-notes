📚 Grammar
S 在 V (structure) be doing (progressive)
你在做什么？ nǐ zài zuò shén me What are you doing?
我在看电视。 wǒ zài kàn diàn shì I am watching TV.
地址 (structure) address with 号楼室
你家在哪儿？ nǐ jiā zài nǎr Where is your home?
我家在1号楼2楼209室。 wǒ jiā zài yī hào lóu èr lóu èr líng jiǔ shì My home is in Building 1, 2nd floor, Room 209.
我家在2楼11室。 wǒ jiā zài èr lóu shí yī shì My home is on the 2nd floor, Room 11.
去 + place + V (structure) go to a place to do sth
我去银行换钱。 wǒ qù yín háng huàn qián I go to the bank to exchange money.
他要去医院看医生。 tā yào qù yī yuàn kàn yī shēng He wants to go to the hospital to see a doctor.
我爸妈家有一个院子。 wǒ bà mā jiā yǒu yī ge yuàn zi My parents' house has a yard.
你的宿舍有多少人？ nǐ de sù shè yǒu duō shǎo rén How many people are in your dorm?
我的宿舍有3个人。 wǒ de sù shè yǒu sān ge rén My dorm has 3 people.
你有多少个室友？ nǐ yǒu duō shǎo ge shì yǒu How many roommates do you have?
我有两个室友。 wǒ yǒu liǎng ge shì yǒu I have two roommates.
你们关系好吗？ nǐ men guān xì hǎo ma Do you have a good relationship?
我们关系很好。 wǒ men guān xì hěn hǎo Our relationship is very good.
你吃我豆腐。 nǐ chī wǒ dòu fu You're eating my tofu (teasing/flirting).
