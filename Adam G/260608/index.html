&quot;S also...&quot; pattern
你好吗？nǐ hǎo ma?How are you?
我很好/帅/漂亮。wǒ hěn hǎo / shuài / piào liangI'm good/handsome/beautiful.
你女朋友漂亮吗？nǐ nǚ péng you piào liang ma?Is your girlfriend beautiful?
我女朋友很漂亮。wǒ nǚ péng you hěn piào liangMy girlfriend is very beautiful.
我爱你。我不爱你。wǒ ài nǐ. wǒ bú ài nǐ.I love you. I don't love you.
你爸爸妈妈不爱你。nǐ bà ba mā ma bú ài nǐ.Your parents don't love you.
他是好人。tā shì hǎo rén.He is a good person.
