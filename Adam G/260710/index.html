📚 Grammar
在 + place + V (structure) doing sth at a place
你在哪儿？ nǐ zài nǎr Where are you?
我在图书馆看书。 wǒ zài tú shū guǎn kàn shū I'm reading at the library.
我在邮局发邮件。 wǒ zài yóu jú fā yóu jiàn I'm sending mail at the post office.
在哪儿 / 在哪里 asking location
请问，邮局在哪儿？ qǐng wèn, yóu jú zài nǎr Excuse me, where is the post office?
就在那儿。 jiù zài nàr It's right over there.
你知道邮局在哪儿吗？ nǐ zhī dào yóu jú zài nǎr ma Do you know where the post office is?
我知道邮局在哪儿。 wǒ zhī dào yóu jú zài nǎr I know where the post office is.
我不知道。 wǒ bù zhī dào I don't know.
你明白吗？ nǐ míng bai ma Do you understand?
我不明白。 wǒ bù míng bai I don't understand.
我没去图书馆。 wǒ méi qù tú shū guǎn I didn't go to the library.
我很少去图书馆。 wǒ hěn shǎo qù tú shū guǎn I rarely go to the library.
纽约有很多图书馆。 niǔ yuē yǒu hěn duō tú shū guǎn New York has many libraries.
在中国，我们很少去邮局。 zài zhōng guó, wǒ men hěn shǎo qù yóu jú In China, we rarely go to the post office.
我和我女朋友12月去台湾。 wǒ hé wǒ nǚ péng you shí èr yuè qù tái wān My girlfriend and I are going to Taiwan in December.
台湾也有故宫。 tái wān yě yǒu gù gōng Taiwan also has a Forbidden Palace.
