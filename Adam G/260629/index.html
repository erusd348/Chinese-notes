📚 Grammar
想 vs 要 want — 想 = softer/less urgent, 要 = more urgent/definite
我晚上想吃香蕉。 wǒ wǎn shang xiǎng chī xiāng jiāo I want to eat bananas tonight (just thinking about it).
我晚上要吃香蕉。 wǒ wǎn shang yào chī xiāng jiāo I want to eat bananas tonight (I've decided).
我很想你。 wǒ hěn xiǎng nǐ I miss you.
想要 want to have (sth concretely)
我想要一个女朋友。 wǒ xiǎng yào yī ge nǚ péng you I want to have a girlfriend.
我想要很多钱。 wǒ xiǎng yào hěn duō qián I want to have a lot of money.
我想要一个房子。 wǒ xiǎng yào yī ge fáng zi I want to have a house.
今天天气好吗？ jīn tiān tiān qì hǎo ma How is the weather today?
今天天气很好。 jīn tiān tiān qì hěn hǎo The weather is good today.
今天天气很热。 jīn tiān tiān qì hěn rè The weather is hot today.
