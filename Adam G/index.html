Adjective sentences: S + 很 + adjNo "to be" in Chinese
我很好。wǒ hěn hǎoI am fine.
我很帅。wǒ hěn shuàiI am handsome.
你很漂亮。nǐ hěn piào liangYou are pretty.
他很帅。tā hěn shuàiHe is handsome.
Negative: 不 + adjNot + adjective
他不帅。tā bù shuàiHe is not handsome.
