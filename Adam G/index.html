📗 第2课 你好吗 How Are You
生词 Vocabulary
吗ma(question particle)
好hǎogood / fine
很hěnvery
呢ne(question particle for follow-up)
也yěalso / too
都dōuall / both
忙mángbusy
累lèitired
渴kěthirsty
饿èhungry
爸爸bà badad
妈妈mā mamom
他们tā menthey / them (male)
课文 Dialogue
A: 你好吗？A: nǐ hǎo ma?A: How are you?
B: 我很好。你呢？B: wǒ hěn hǎo. nǐ ne?B: I'm fine. And you?
A: 我也很好。A: wǒ yě hěn hǎo.A: I'm fine too.
B: 你爸爸、妈妈都好？B: nǐ bà ba, mā ma dōu hǎo?B: Your dad and mom are both well?
A: 他们也都很好。A: tā men yě dōu hěn hǎo.A: They're also both well.
A: 你忙吗？A: nǐ máng ma?A: Are you busy?
B: 我不忙。B: wǒ bù máng.B: I'm not busy.

Adjective sentences: S + 很 + adjNo "to be" in Chinese
我很好。wǒ hěn hǎoI am fine.
我很帅。wǒ hěn shuàiI am handsome.
你很漂亮。nǐ hěn piào liangYou are pretty.
他很帅。tā hěn shuàiHe is handsome.
Negative: 不 + adjNot + adjective
他不帅。tā bù shuàiHe is not handsome.
