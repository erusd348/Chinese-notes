📚 Grammar
数字：百 / 千 numbers: hundred / thousand
三百零五。 sān bǎi líng wǔ 305.
三百五十。 sān bǎi wǔ shí 350.
我们学校有1千8百人。 wǒ men xué xiào yǒu yī qiān bā bǎi rén Our school has 1800 people.
我们班有24人。 wǒ men bān yǒu èr shí sì rén Our class has 24 people.
多 / 少 / 很多 / 很少 many / few / a lot / seldom
我喝了很多水。 wǒ hē le hěn duō shuǐ I drank a lot of water.
他有很多钱。 tā yǒu hěn duō qián He has a lot of money.
我很少吃披萨。 wǒ hěn shǎo chī pī sà I seldom eat pizza.
我很少喝酒和咖啡。 wǒ hěn shǎo hē jiǔ hé kā fēi I seldom drink alcohol and coffee.
他的钱很少。 tā de qián hěn shǎo He has very little money.
多大 / 岁 how old / years old
你多大了？我17岁了。 nǐ duō dà le? wǒ shí qī suì le How old are you? I'm 17 years old.
你们家有多少人？ How many people in your family?
我们家有4口人。 wǒ men jiā yǒu sì kǒu rén Our family has 4 people.
妈妈、爸爸、弟弟和我。 mā ma, bà ba, dì di hé wǒ Mom, Dad, brother and me.
杯子 / 多少钱 cups / how much
你有多少个杯子？我有两个杯子。 nǐ yǒu duō shǎo gè bēi zi? wǒ yǒu liǎng gè bēi zi How many cups do you have? I have two cups.
一杯咖啡多少钱？一杯咖啡4欧元。 yī bēi kā fēi duō shǎo qián? yī bēi kā fēi sì ōu yuán How much is a cup of coffee? A cup of coffee is 4 euros.
一瓶水多少钱？一瓶水1欧元。 yī píng shuǐ duō shǎo qián? yī píng shuǐ yī ōu yuán How much is a bottle of water? A bottle of water is 1 euro.
一杯奶茶多少钱？一杯奶茶18块。 yī bēi nǎi chá duō shǎo qián? yī bēi nǎi chá shí bā kuài How much is a cup of milk tea? A cup of milk tea is 18 yuan.
块 / 毛 / 分 yuan / dime / cent
9块9毛9。 jiǔ kuài jiǔ máo jiǔ 9.99 yuan
9块9。 jiǔ kuài jiǔ 9.9 yuan
