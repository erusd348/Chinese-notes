自我介绍 self-introduction
你好，我叫亚瑟，我今年17岁，我来自俄罗斯。 nǐ hǎo, wǒ jiào yà sè, wǒ jīn nián shí qī suì, wǒ lái zì é luó sī Hello, my name is Arthur. I am 17 years old. I come from Russia.
我家有4口人，爸爸、妈妈、弟弟和我。我是高中生。我喜欢打篮球、游泳和跑步。我喜欢吃汉堡和包子。我喜欢喝水和茶。 wǒ jiā yǒu sì kǒu rén, bà ba, mā ma, dì di hé wǒ. wǒ shì gāo zhōng shēng. wǒ xǐ huān dǎ lán qiú, yóu yǒng hé pǎo bù. wǒ xǐ huān chī hàn bǎo hé bāo zi. wǒ xǐ huān hē shuǐ hé chá. There are 4 people in my family: dad, mom, younger brother, and me. I'm a high school student. I like playing basketball, swimming, and running. I like eating hamburgers and buns. I like drinking water and tea.
