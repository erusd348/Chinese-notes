📖 Vocabulary
不客气 bù kè qì you're welcome; don't mention it
关系 guān xi relationship
对不起 duì bu qǐ I'm sorry; to let down
没关系 méi guān xi it doesn't matter; no problem
也 yě also; too
📚 Grammar
原谅、道歉和问候 apologies, thanks, and greetings
你不用谢我。 nǐ bù yòng xiè wǒ You don't need to thank me.
不客气。 bù kè qì You're welcome.
我对不起你。 wǒ duì bu qǐ nǐ I let you down.
没关系。 méi guān xi It's okay.
关系 relationships
我们没关系。 wǒ men méi guān xi We have no relationship. / It doesn't concern us.
你们关系好吗？ nǐ men guān xi hǎo ma Is your relationship good?
我们关系很好。你们呢？ wǒ men guān xi hěn hǎo. nǐ men ne Our relationship is good. How about you?
我们关系不好。 wǒ men guān xi bù hǎo Our relationship is not good.
他们关系不好。 tā men guān xi bù hǎo Their relationship is not good.
S adv adj (structure) subject + adverb + adjective
我很好。 wǒ hěn hǎo I'm very good.
我不好。 wǒ bù hǎo I'm not good.
Statement + 吗？ (structure) yes/no question
你爸爸妈妈关系好吗？ nǐ bà ba mā ma guān xi hǎo ma Are your parents' relationship good?
你是蒋真吗？ nǐ shì jiǎng zhēn ma Are you Jiang Zhen?
你是美国人吗？ nǐ shì měi guó rén ma Are you American?
你好吗？ nǐ hǎo ma How are you?
Pronoun + 呢 (structure) how about pronoun?
你呢？ nǐ ne How about you?
你爸爸呢？ nǐ bà ba ne How about your dad?
你妈妈呢？ nǐ mā ma ne How about your mom?
S + 也 + predicate (structure) also
我也很好。 wǒ yě hěn hǎo I'm also very good.
我爸爸也很好。 wǒ bà ba yě hěn hǎo My dad is also very good.
我也是美国人。 wǒ yě shì měi guó rén I'm also American.
我爸爸也是美国人。 wǒ bà ba yě shì měi guó rén My dad is also American.
我妈妈也是美国人。 wǒ mā ma yě shì měi guó rén My mom is also American.
我也对不起你。 wǒ yě duì bu qǐ nǐ I also let you down.
我爸爸妈妈关系也很好。 wǒ bà ba mā ma guān xi yě hěn hǎo My parents' relationship is also good.
