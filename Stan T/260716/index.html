📚 Grammar
V + 不起 can't afford to; can't bear to
买不起 mǎi bu qǐ can't afford to buy
看不起 kàn bu qǐ to look down on
想不起 xiǎng bu qǐ can't remember
对不起 / 没关系 sorry / it's okay
他对我说对不起。 tā duì wǒ shuō duì bu qǐ He said sorry to me.
没关系。 méi guān xi It's okay.
关系 relationship; connection
我们关系很好。 wǒ men guān xi hěn hǎo We have a good relationship.
这两件事没有关系。 zhè liǎng jiàn shì méi yǒu guān xi These two things have nothing to do with each other.
你们什么关系？我们是朋友关系。 nǐ men shén me guān xi? wǒ men shì péng you guān xi What's your relationship? We are friends.
我在政府里有关系。 wǒ zài zhèng fǔ lǐ yǒu guān xi I have connections in the government.
在中国做事，要有关系。 zài zhōng guó zuò shì, yào yǒu guān xi To get things done in China, you need connections.
这个人老气横秋。 zhè ge rén lǎo qì héng qiū This person is arrogantly old-fashioned.
这件衣服很老气。 zhè jiàn yī fu hěn lǎo qì This piece of clothing is very old-fashioned.
这里死气沉沉。 zhè lǐ sǐ qì chén chén It's lifeless here.
这里充满生气。 zhè lǐ chōng mǎn shēng qì This place is full of vitality.
你看不起我。 nǐ kàn bu qǐ wǒ You look down on me.
我想不起来了。 wǒ xiǎng bu qǐ lái le I can't remember.
