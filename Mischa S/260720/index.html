📚 Grammar
高 tall
我不高。 wǒ bù gāo I'm not tall.
我们都是高人。 wǒ men dōu shì gāo rén We are all tall people.
没（有） to not have
我没吃饭。 wǒ méi chī fàn I haven't eaten.
我不吃饭。 wǒ bù chī fàn I don't eat.
关系 / 都得 relationship / both + all
它没有关系。 tā méi yǒu guān xì It doesn't matter.
你们关系好吗？ nǐ men guān xì hǎo ma Do you have a good relationship?
我们关系很好。 wǒ men guān xì hěn hǎo We have a good relationship.
你爸爸妈妈关系好吗？ nǐ bà ba mā ma guān xì hǎo ma Do your parents have a good relationship?
他们关系很好。你爸爸妈妈呢？ tā men guān xì hěn hǎo. nǐ bà ba mā ma ne They have a good relationship. How about your parents?
他们关系也很好。 tā men guān xì yě hěn hǎo Their relationship is also good.
我们都不高。 wǒ men dōu bù gāo We are all not tall.
我们都是大人。 wǒ men dōu shì dà rén We are all adults.
我们都不是小人。 wǒ men dōu bù shì xiǎo rén We are not petty people.
也 / 都 also / both
你好吗？我很好。你呢？ nǐ hǎo ma? wǒ hěn hǎo. nǐ ne How are you? I'm fine. How about you?
我也很好。 wǒ yě hěn hǎo I'm also fine.
你爸爸妈妈都好吗？ nǐ bà ba mā ma dōu hǎo ma Are both your parents fine?
他们也都很好。 tā men yě dōu hěn hǎo They're also both fine.
再 / 又 again (future / past)
再见。 zài jiàn Goodbye.
是的 / 不是 yes / no
是的。 shì de Yes.
不是。 bù shì No.
