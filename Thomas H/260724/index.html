📚 Grammar
语法 grammar
韩语的语法很难。 hán yǔ de yǔ fǎ hěn nán Korean grammar is very difficult.
难 difficult
这个考试太难了，我考不过。 zhè gè kǎo shì tài nán le, wǒ kǎo bù guò This exam is too difficult, I can't pass it.
至少 at least
这个工作至少要有三年的经验。 zhè gè gōng zuò zhì shǎo yào yǒu sān nián de jīng yàn This job requires at least three years of experience.
至少/小 at least / small
他今年至少不小了。 tā jīn nián zhì shǎo bù xiǎo le He's not young anymore.
身体 body
你的身体很健康。 nǐ de shēn tǐ hěn jiàn kāng Your body is very healthy.
生日 birthday
明天是我的生日，我们一起吃饭吧。 míng tiān shì wǒ de shēng rì, wǒ men yī qǐ chī fàn ba Tomorrow is my birthday, let's eat together.
在 + location + direction at/in/on plus location/direction
在我们的课上，她坐在我旁边。 zài wǒ men de kè shàng, tā zuò zài wǒ páng biān In our class, she sits next to me.
做饭 to cook
她每天在家里做饭。 tā měi tiān zài jiā lǐ zuò fàn She cooks at home every day.
岳母 / 丈母娘 mother-in-law
我的岳母对我很好。 wǒ de yuè mǔ duì wǒ hěn hǎo My mother-in-law is very good to me.
电子 / 电子产品 electronic / electronic products
我喜欢听电子乐。 wǒ xǐ huān tīng diàn zǐ yuè I like to listen to electronic music.
这个电子产品太贵了，我买不起。 zhè gè diàn zǐ chǎn pǐn tài guì le, wǒ mǎi bù qǐ This electronic product is too expensive, I can't afford it.
游戏机 game console
我弟弟买了一个新游戏机。 wǒ dì di mǎi le yī gè xīn yóu xì jī My younger brother bought a new game console.
电车 / 电脑 / 电视 / 电影 electric car / computer / TV / movie
我喜欢看电影。 wǒ xǐ huān kàn diàn yǐng I like watching movies.
滚 get lost
他说了很不好听的话，我让他滚。 tā shuō le hěn bù hǎo tīng de huà, wǒ ràng tā gǔn He said something very offensive, I told him to get lost.
📖 课文：自我介绍 self-introduction
我叫黄俊吾，我今年29岁。 wǒ jiào huáng jùn wú, wǒ jīn nián èr shí jiǔ suì My name is Huang Junwu, I'm 29 years old.
我在美国出生长大。 wǒ zài měi guó chū shēng zhǎng dà I was born and raised in the US.
我喜欢踢足球，打高尔夫、听摇滚和电子乐。 wǒ xǐ huān tī zú qiú, dǎ gāo ěr fū, tīng yáo gǔn hé diàn zǐ yuè I like playing football, golf, listening to rock and electronic music.
我喜欢吃小笼包、饺子、馄饨、韩国菜和日本菜。 wǒ xǐ huān chī xiǎo lóng bāo, jiǎo zi, hún tun, hán guó cài hé rì běn cài I like eating xiaolongbao, dumplings, wontons, Korean food and Japanese food.
我以前是软件工程师，现在我准备开一家软件公司。 wǒ yǐ qián shì ruǎn jiàn gōng chéng shī, xiàn zài wǒ zhǔn bèi kāi yī jiā ruǎn jiàn gōng sī I used to be a software engineer, now I'm preparing to start a software company.
我结婚6年了，我老婆28岁。 wǒ jié hūn liù nián le, wǒ lǎo pó èr shí bā suì I've been married for 6 years, my wife is 28.
