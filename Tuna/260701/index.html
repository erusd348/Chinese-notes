📚 Grammar
Statement + 吗 making yes/no questions
你好吗？ nǐ hǎo ma How are you?
她好吗？ tā hǎo ma Is she well?
S + Adv + Adj subject + adverb + adjective
我很好。 wǒ hěn hǎo I am very well.
她很好。 tā hěn hǎo She is very well.
她不好。 tā bù hǎo She is not well.
S + 是 + N subject + to be + noun
你是Tuna吗？ nǐ shì Tuna ma Are you Tuna?
我是Tuna。 wǒ shì Tuna I am Tuna.
Pronoun + 呢 what about...?
你呢？ nǐ ne What about you?
