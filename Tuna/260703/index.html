📚 Grammar
了 past tense marker / completion
你吃饭了吗？ nǐ chī fàn le ma? Have you eaten?
我吃饭了。 wǒ chī fàn le. I have eaten.
我没吃饭。 wǒ méi chī fàn. I haven't eaten.
我不吃饭。 wǒ bù chī fàn. I don't eat.
你早上吃什么？ nǐ zǎoshang chī shénme? What do you eat in the morning?
我早上吃包子。 wǒ zǎoshang chī bāozi. I eat steamed buns in the morning.
你吃了什么？ nǐ chī le shénme? What did you eat?
我吃了鱼和水果。 wǒ chī le yú hé shuǐguǒ. I ate fish and fruit.
