📚 Grammar
喜欢 + V (structure) like to do
你喜欢喝什么？ nǐ xǐ huān hē shén me What do you like to drink?
我喜欢喝红茶和咖啡。 wǒ xǐ huān hē hóng chá hé kā fēi I like to drink black tea and coffee.
你喜欢喝奶茶吗？ nǐ xǐ huān hē nǎi chá ma Do you like milk tea?
是的，我喜欢。 shì de, wǒ xǐ huān Yes, I like it.
要 / 想 (structure) want
你要吃什么？ nǐ yào chī shén me What do you want to eat?
我要吃一碗面条和一条鱼。 wǒ yào chī yī wǎn miàn tiáo hé yī tiáo yú I want to eat a bowl of noodles and a fish.
你想买什么？ nǐ xiǎng mǎi shén me What do you want to buy?
我想买这个。 wǒ xiǎng mǎi zhè ge I want to buy this one.
你要喝什么？ nǐ yào hē shén me What do you want to drink?
我要一杯水。 wǒ yào yī bēi shuǐ I want a glass of water.
