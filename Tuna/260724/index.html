📚 Grammar
饿 / 不饿 / 汤 hungry / not hungry / soup
我饿了。 wǒ è le I'm hungry.
我不饿。 wǒ bù è I'm not hungry.
我很饿。 wǒ hěn è I'm very hungry.
你今天怎么样？ nǐ jīn tiān zěn me yàng How are you today?
还可以。 hái kě yǐ Still okay.
出去玩 / 做什么 go out / do what
你明天做什么？ nǐ míng tiān zuò shén me What are you doing tomorrow?
我明天和朋友出去玩。 wǒ míng tiān hé péng yǒu chū qù wán I'm going out with a friend tomorrow.
你后天做什么？ nǐ hòu tiān zuò shén me What are you doing the day after tomorrow?
我后天在家看书、玩游戏。 wǒ hòu tiān zài jiā kàn shū, wán yóu xì The day after tomorrow I'll read books and play games at home.
你昨天做什么了？ nǐ zuó tiān zuò shén me le What did you do yesterday?
我昨天去商店买衣服了。 wǒ zuó tiān qù shāng diàn mǎi yī fu le Yesterday I went to the store to buy clothes.
你前天做什么了？ nǐ qián tiān zuò shén me le What did you do the day before yesterday?
我前天和我女朋友去美术馆了。 wǒ qián tiān hé wǒ nǚ péng yǒu qù měi shù guǎn le The day before yesterday I went to the art museum with my girlfriend.
我下个星期去中国。 wǒ xià gè xīng qī qù zhōng guó I'm going to China next week.
A 和 B 见面 / 等 / 上课 / 下课 meet / wait / class
我们在哪儿见面？ wǒ men zài nǎr jiàn miàn Where shall we meet?
我们什么时候见面？ wǒ men shén me shí hòu jiàn miàn When shall we meet?
我们上午12点在学校食堂见面。 wǒ men shàng wǔ shí èr diǎn zài xué xiào shí táng jiàn miàn We'll meet at the school canteen at noon.
我11点在学校食堂等你。 wǒ shí yī diǎn zài xué xiào shí táng děng nǐ I'll wait for you at the school canteen at 11.
