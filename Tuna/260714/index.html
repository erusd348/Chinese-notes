📚 Grammar
买单 paying the bill
买单！ mǎi dān The bill please!
两块五（毛）。 liǎng kuài wǔ (máo) Two kuai and five mao.
去 / 留学 / 学校 go / study abroad / school
你要去哪儿？ nǐ yào qù nǎr Where are you going?
我要去复旦大学。 wǒ yào qù fù dàn dà xué I'm going to Fudan University.
你要去哪儿留学？ nǐ yào qù nǎr liú xué Where do you want to study abroad?
我要去中国留学。 wǒ yào qù zhōng guó liú xué I want to study abroad in China.
我是留学生，我学神经科学。 wǒ shì liú xué shēng, wǒ xué shén jīng kē xué I'm an international student, I study neuroscience.
图书馆 library
你去图书馆吗？ nǐ qù tú shū guǎn ma Are you going to the library?
我去图书馆看书。 wǒ qù tú shū guǎn kàn shū I go to the library to read books.
我去图书馆学习。 wǒ qù tú shū guǎn xué xí I go to the library to study.
