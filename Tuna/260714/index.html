📚 Grammar
在 + 地方 to be at a place
你在哪儿？ nǐ zài nǎr Where are you?
我在学校。 wǒ zài xué xiào I'm at school.
在 + verb (正在进行) to be doing (present continuous)
你在做什么？ nǐ zài zuò shén me What are you doing?
我在吃饭。 wǒ zài chī fàn I'm eating.
我在食堂吃饭。 wǒ zài shí táng chī fàn I'm eating at the canteen.
我在图书馆看书。 wǒ zài tú shū guǎn kàn shū I'm reading at the library.
我在宿舍休息。 wǒ zài sù shè xiū xi I'm resting in the dormitory.
请问 + 地方在哪儿 asking where a place is
请问，食堂在哪儿？ qǐng wèn, shí táng zài nǎr Excuse me, where is the canteen?
食堂在那儿。 shí táng zài nàr The canteen is over there.
请问，洗手间在哪儿？ qǐng wèn, xǐ shǒu jiān zài nǎr Excuse me, where is the washroom?
洗手间在二楼。 xǐ shǒu jiān zài èr lóu The washroom is on the second floor.
