📚 Grammar
命座 constellations
你的桑多涅有几命？ nǐ de sāng duō niè yǒu jǐ mìng How many constellations does your Sandrone have?
我的桑多涅有一命。 wǒ de sāng duō niè yǒu yī mìng My Sandrone has C1.
你有满命角色吗？ nǐ yǒu mǎn mìng jué sè ma Do you have any C6 characters?
你的哪个角色命座最多？ nǐ de nǎ ge jué sè mìng zuò zuì duō Which character has the most constellations?
刻晴。 kè qíng Keqing.
抽卡 / 月卡 pulling / battle pass
你抽卡花钱了吗？ nǐ chōu kǎ huā qián le ma Did you spend money on pulls?
我有时候花钱。 wǒ yǒu shí hou huā qián I spend sometimes.
我买过一次大月卡。 wǒ mǎi guo yī cì dà yuè kǎ I bought the battle pass once.
加强 / 理性 / 为了 buff / rational / for
最近很多角色都加强了。 zuì jìn hěn duō jué sè dōu jiā qiáng le Recently many characters got buffed.
我想要七七，因为她最近很厉害。 wǒ xiǎng yào qī qī, yīn wèi tā zuì jìn hěn lì hài I want Qiqi because she's very strong lately.
有时候武器也很重要。 yǒu shí hou wǔ qì yě hěn zhòng yào Sometimes weapons are also important.
为了拿到想要的角色，抽卡要理性。 wèi le ná dào xiǎng yào de jué sè, chōu kǎ yào lǐ xìng To get the character you want, you need to be rational about pulling.
