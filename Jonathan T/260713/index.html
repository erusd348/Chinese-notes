📚 Grammar
很少 rarely
我很少喝咖啡。 wǒ hěn shǎo hē kā fēi I rarely drink coffee.
上班 / 下班 work routine
你今天几点上班？ nǐ jīn tiān jǐ diǎn shàng bān What time do you go to work today?
我每天10点上班，6点下班。 wǒ měi tiān shí diǎn shàng bān, liù diǎn xià bān I go to work at 10 and get off at 6 every day.
我不用很早起床。 wǒ bù yòng hěn zǎo qǐ chuáng I don't need to wake up very early.
吃饭 eating
你中午和晚上在哪儿吃饭？ nǐ zhōng wǔ hé wǎn shang zài nǎr chī fàn Where do you eat lunch and dinner?
我中午在公司吃饭，晚上在家吃饭。 wǒ zhōng wǔ zài gōng sī chī fàn, wǎn shang zài jiā chī fàn I eat lunch at the company and dinner at home.
你会做饭吗？ nǐ huì zuò fàn ma Can you cook?
我会做饭。 wǒ huì zuò fàn I can cook.
我们公司没有餐厅。 wǒ men gōng sī méi yǒu cān tīng Our company doesn't have a cafeteria.
A 离 B adj distance from A to B
你家离公司很近吗？ nǐ jiā lí gōng sī hěn jìn ma Is your home very close to the company?
我家离公司很近。 wǒ jiā lí gōng sī hěn jìn My home is very close to the company.
从我家到公司只要20分钟。 cóng wǒ jiā dào gōng sī zhǐ yào èr shí fēn zhōng From my home to the company only takes 20 minutes.
从牛津到伦敦开车要一个半小时。 cóng niú jīn dào lún dūn kāi chē yào yī ge bàn xiǎo shí Driving from Oxford to London takes an hour and a half.
伦敦的中心到外边也要一个半小时。 lún dūn de zhōng xīn dào wài bian yě yào yī ge bàn xiǎo shí From the center of London to the outside also takes an hour and a half.
其他 other
我和我的弟弟聊关于做饭的事。 wǒ hé wǒ de dì di liáo guān yú zuò fàn de shì My brother and I chat about cooking.
我有的时候中午回家。 wǒ yǒu de shí hou zhōng wǔ huí jiā Sometimes I go home at noon.
我每两个星期回家一次。 wǒ měi liǎng ge xīng qī huí jiā yī cì I go home once every two weeks.
有的人喜欢咖啡，有的人喜欢茶。 yǒu de rén xǐ huan kā fēi, yǒu de rén xǐ huan chá Some people like coffee, some people like tea.
