📚 Grammar
又 + adj + 又 + adj both...and... (manner/degree)
香港的房子又小又贵。 xiāng gǎng de fáng zi yòu xiǎo yòu guì Houses in Hong Kong are both small and expensive.
这个菜又好看又好吃。 zhè ge cài yòu hǎo kàn yòu hǎo chī This dish is both beautiful and delicious.
