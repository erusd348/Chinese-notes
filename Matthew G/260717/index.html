📚 Grammar
但是 but; however
我们公司没有食堂，但是有免费的吃的。 wǒ men gōng sī méi yǒu shí táng, dàn shì yǒu miǎn fèi de chī de Our company doesn't have a canteen, but has free food.
我天天去韩国酒店的健身房健身。 wǒ tiān tiān qù hán guó jiǔ diàn de jiàn shēn fáng jiàn shēn I go to the Korean hotel's gym every day to work out.
我和我的同事昨天晚上在酒吧喝了很多啤酒。 wǒ hé wǒ de tóng shì zuó tiān wǎn shàng zài jiǔ bā hē le hěn duō pí jiǔ My colleague and I drank a lot of beer at the bar last night.
