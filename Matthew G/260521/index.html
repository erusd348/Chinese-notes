很多 + noun vs 很有 + adj
很 + measure vs 很 + verb

他很有钱。tā hěn yǒu qiánHe is very rich.
我喝很多咖啡。wǒ hē hěn duō kā fēiI drink a lot of coffee.
noun + 很少 vs 很少 + verb
Little vs rarely

我很少喝酒。wǒ hěn shǎo hē jiǔI rarely drink alcohol.
