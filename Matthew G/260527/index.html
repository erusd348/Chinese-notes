📚 Grammar
Want: 想 vs 要
Soft vs strong desire
我想要很多钱。wǒ xiǎng yào hěn duō qiánI want a lot of money.

Pricing questions
How much is it?
一杯咖啡多少钱？yī bēi kā fēi duō shǎo qiánHow much is a cup of coffee?
一杯咖啡10块。yī bēi kā fēi shí kuàiA cup of coffee is 10 yuan.
一个包子2块。yī gè bāo zi liǎng kuàiA baozi is 2 yuan.
一个本子3块。yī gè běn zi sān kuàiA notebook is 3 yuan.
很多 + noun vs 有钱
A lot of vs rich
我喝了很多咖啡。wǒ hē le hěn duō kā fēiI drank a lot of coffee.
中国有很多人。zhōng guó yǒu hěn duō rénChina has a lot of people.
他很有钱。tā hěn yǒu qiánHe is rich.
很少 + verb
Rarely do something
我很少喝茶。wǒ hěn shǎo hē cháI rarely drink tea.
Asking about quantity
多少 + measure word
你有多少个好朋友？nǐ yǒu duō shǎo gè hǎo péng youHow many good friends do you have?
我有5个好朋友。wǒ yǒu wǔ gè hǎo péng youI have 5 good friends.
你有多少个女朋友？nǐ yǒu duō shǎo gè nǚ péng youHow many girlfriends do you have?
我有1个女朋友。wǒ yǒu yī gè nǚ péng youI have 1 girlfriend.
我一天喝了10杯咖啡。wǒ yī tiān hē le shí bēi kā fēiI drank 10 cups of coffee in one day.
