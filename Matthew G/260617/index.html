在 place V do V at a place

我中午在饭店吃饭。 wǒ zhōngwǔ zài fàndiàn chīfàn. I eat lunch at the restaurant.

我不爱去酒吧。 wǒ bú ài qù jiǔbā. I don't like going to bars.
