📖 Vocabulary
怎么说 zěn me shuō how to say
睡 shuì to sleep
觉 jiào sleep (noun)
睡一觉 shuì yī jiào to take a nap; to get some sleep
医院 yī yuàn hospital
看医生 kàn yī shēng to see a doctor
苹果 píng guǒ apple
苹果手机 píng guǒ shǒu jī iPhone
企鹅 qǐ é penguin
不用 bù yòng don't need to
疾病 jí bìng sickness; disease
远离 yuǎn lí to stay away from; far from
📚 Grammar
V + 得 + adj V + 得 + adjective (describing the action)
我吃得很好。 wǒ chī de hěn hǎo I eat well.
你睡得好吗？ nǐ shuì de hǎo ma Do you sleep well?
我睡得不好。 wǒ shuì de bù hǎo I don't sleep well.
我没睡好。 wǒ méi shuì hǎo I didn't sleep well.
什么时候 / 昨天晚上 when / last night
我昨天晚上10点睡觉。 wǒ zuó tiān wǎn shang shí diǎn shuì jiào I went to sleep at 10 last night.
医院 / 看医生 / 苹果 hospital / see a doctor / apple
他去医院看医生。 tā qù yī yuàn kàn yī shēng He went to the hospital to see a doctor.
天天吃一个苹果，不用看医生。 tiān tiān chī yī gè píng guǒ, bù yòng kàn yī shēng Eat an apple every day, no need to see a doctor.
苹果手机 / 企鹅 iPhone / penguin
你用苹果手机吗？ nǐ yòng píng guǒ shǒu jī ma Do you use an iPhone?
我有苹果手机，但是我要换三星手机。 wǒ yǒu píng guǒ shǒu jī, dàn shì wǒ yào huàn sān xīng shǒu jī I have an iPhone, but I want to switch to a Samsung.
💬 Sentences
怎么说……？ how do you say...?
