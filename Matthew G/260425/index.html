我吃米饭了。Wǒ chī mǐfàn le.I ate rice.
我不吃米饭。Wǒ bù chī mǐfàn.I don’t eat rice.
你不吃米饭吗？Nǐ bù chī mǐfàn ma?You don’t eat rice?
我没吃米饭。Wǒ méi chī mǐfàn.I didn’t eat rice.
你没吃米饭吗？Nǐ méi chī mǐfàn ma?You didn’t eat rice?
你吃晚饭了吗？Nǐ chī wǎnfàn le ma?Did you eat dinner?
我吃晚饭了。Wǒ chī wǎnfàn le.I ate dinner.
我没吃早饭。Wǒ méi chī zǎofàn.I didn’t eat breakfast.
你早上吃什么？Nǐ zǎoshang chī shénme?What do you eat in the morning?
我早上吃燕麦。Wǒ zǎoshang chī yànmài.I eat oats in the morning.
牛肉好吃吗？Niúròu hǎochī ma?Is beef tasty?
牛肉很好吃。Niúròu hěn hǎochī.Beef is very tasty.
你爱吃什么饺子？Nǐ ài chī shénme jiǎozi?What kind of dumplings do you like to eat?
我爱吃牛肉饺子。Wǒ ài chī niúròu jiǎozi.I like to eat beef dumplings.
我爱吃猪肉饺子。Wǒ ài chī zhūròu jiǎozi.I like to eat pork dumplings.

我没吃饭。Wǒ méi chī fàn.I haven’t eaten.
