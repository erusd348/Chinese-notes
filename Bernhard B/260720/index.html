📚 Grammar
周末 / 周日 weekend / Sunday
我周末做运动了。 wǒ zhōu mò zuò yùn dòng le I exercised on the weekend.
周日我在家休息。 zhōu rì wǒ zài jiā xiū xi On Sunday I rest at home.
运动 / 骑自行车 exercise / to ride a bike
你做了什么运动？ nǐ zuò le shén me yùn dòng What exercise did you do?
我骑自行车了。 wǒ qí zì xíng chē le I rode a bike.
你跟谁一起骑自行车？ nǐ gēn shuí yì qǐ qí zì xíng chē Who did you ride a bike with?
我跟爱人一起骑自行车。 wǒ gēn ài rén yì qǐ qí zì xíng chē I rode a bike with my spouse.
买东西 / 买了几件 to go shopping / how many items bought
我们去商场买东西了。 wǒ men qù shāng chǎng mǎi dōng xi le We went to the mall to buy things.
你们买了什么？ nǐ men mǎi le shén me What did you buy?
我们买了衣服和吃的。 wǒ men mǎi le yī fu hé chī de We bought clothes and food.
你们买了几件衣服？ nǐ men mǎi le jǐ jiàn yī fu How many pieces of clothing did you buy?
我们买了两件衣服，一件是我的，一件是我爱人的。 wǒ men mǎi le liǎng jiàn yī fu, yí jiàn shì wǒ de, yí jiàn shì wǒ ài rén de We bought two pieces of clothing, one is mine, one is my spouse's.
也 / 和 also / and (listing)
我和爱人还有孩子们都住在家里。 wǒ hé ài rén hái yǒu hái zi men dōu zhù zài jiā lǐ My spouse, children and I all live at home.
我们买了意大利面、蔬菜、面包和水。 wǒ men mǎi le yì dà lì miàn, shū cài, miàn bāo hé shuǐ We bought pasta, vegetables, bread and water.
📖 课文：周末生活 text: weekend life
我周末做运动了。 wǒ zhōu mò zuò yùn dòng le I exercised on the weekend.
我跟爱人一起骑自行车。 wǒ gēn ài rén yì qǐ qí zì xíng chē I rode a bike with my spouse.
我们去商场买东西了。 wǒ men qù shāng chǎng mǎi dōng xi le We went to the mall to buy things.
我们买了衣服和吃的。 wǒ men mǎi le yī fu hé chī de We bought clothes and food.
我们买了两件衣服。 wǒ men mǎi le liǎng jiàn yī fu We bought two pieces of clothing.
我们还买了意大利面、蔬菜、面包和水。 wǒ men hái mǎi le yì dà lì miàn, shū cài, miàn bāo hé shuǐ We also bought pasta, vegetables, bread and water.
我的孩子很大了。 wǒ de hái zi hěn dà le My children are very grown up.
我大女儿30岁，是商场经理。 wǒ dà nǚ ér sān shí suì, shì shāng chǎng jīng lǐ My eldest daughter is 30, she's a mall manager.
大儿子28岁，是医院后勤经理。 dà ér zi èr shí bā suì, shì yī yuàn hòu qín jīng lǐ My eldest son is 28, he's a hospital logistics manager.
小儿子20岁，是学生。 xiǎo ér zi èr shí suì, shì xué shēng My youngest son is 20, he's a student.
我和爱人还有孩子们都住在家里。 wǒ hé ài rén hái yǒu hái zi men dōu zhù zài jiā lǐ My spouse, children and I all live at home.
我有一个很幸福的家庭。 wǒ yǒu yí gè hěn xìng fú de jiā tíng I have a very happy family.
