早上 / 中午 / 吃 / 喝 morning / noon / eat / drink

你早上吃什么？ nǐ zǎo shang chī shén me What do you eat in the morning?
早上我吃两个面包和蜂蜜。 zǎo shang wǒ chī liǎng gè miàn bāo hé fēng mì In the morning I eat two breads and honey.
你中午吃什么？ nǐ zhōng wǔ chī shén me What do you eat at noon?

我每天喝5杯水。 wǒ měi tiān hē wǔ bēi shuǐ I drink 5 cups of water every day.
