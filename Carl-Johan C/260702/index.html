📚 Grammar
缘分 fate; destiny; serendipitous bond
我们能认识真是缘分。 wǒ men néng rèn shi zhēn shì yuán fèn It's really fate that we got to meet.
兄弟姐妹 siblings
你有兄弟姐妹吗？ nǐ yǒu xiōng dì jiě mèi ma Do you have any siblings?
我没有兄弟姐妹，我是独生子。 wǒ méi yǒu xiōng dì jiě mèi, wǒ shì dú shēng zǐ I don't have siblings, I'm an only child.
发展 development; to develop
新加坡的经济发展很快。 xīn jiā pō de jīng jì fā zhǎn hěn kuài Singapore's economy is developing very fast.
这几年中国的科技发展得很好。 zhè jǐ nián zhōng guó de kē jì fā zhǎn de hěn hǎo China's technology has developed very well in recent years.
觉得 to think; to feel
你觉得新加坡怎么样？ nǐ jué de xīn jiā pō zěn me yàng What do you think of Singapore?
我觉得中文很有意思。 wǒ jué de zhōng wén hěn yǒu yì si I think Chinese is very interesting.
孤单 lonely
一个人在国外有时候会觉得孤单。 yī ge rén zài guó wài yǒu shí hou huì jué de gū dān Being alone abroad sometimes feels lonely.
你有朋友陪你就不会孤单了。 nǐ yǒu péng you péi nǐ jiù bù huì gū dān le If you have friends with you, you won't feel lonely.
抱怨 to complain
她常常抱怨工作太累。 tā cháng cháng bào yuàn gōng zuò tài lèi She often complains that work is too tiring.
我常听新加坡人抱怨物价很贵。 wǒ cháng tīng xīn jiā pō rén bào yuàn wù jià hěn guì I often hear Singaporeans complain that prices are expensive.
物价 prices
香港的物价很高。 xiāng gǎng de wù jià hěn gāo Hong Kong's prices are very high.
新加坡的物价也很贵。 xīn jiā pō de wù jià yě hěn guì Singapore's prices are also expensive.
严格 strict
我们公司的规定很严格。 wǒ men gōng sī de guī dìng hěn yán gé Our company's rules are very strict.
我的老师很严格但是很好。 wǒ de lǎo shī hěn yán gé dàn shì hěn hǎo My teacher is strict but very good.
规定 rules; regulations
学校有很多规定。 xué xiào yǒu hěn duō guī dìng The school has many rules.
这是公司的规定。 zhè shì gōng sī de guī dìng This is a company regulation.
后来 afterwards; later
后来你去了哪里？ hòu lái nǐ qù le nǎ lǐ Where did you go afterwards?
然后 then; after that
我先吃饭，然后去上课。 wǒ xiān chī fàn, rán hòu qù shàng kè I'll eat first, then go to class.
她们以前住在香港，然后来到了新加坡。 tā men yǐ qián zhù zài xiāng gǎng, rán hòu lái dào le xīn jiā pō They used to live in Hong Kong, then came to Singapore.
两年后，我也来到了这儿。 liǎng nián hòu, wǒ yě lái dào le zhèr Two years later, I also came here.
新加坡天气太热了，但是生活很舒服、很方便、很干净、很安全。 xīn jiā pō tiān qì tài rè le, dàn shì shēng huó hěn shū fu, hěn fāng biàn, hěn gān jìng, hěn ān quán Singapore's weather is too hot, but life is very comfortable, convenient, clean, and safe.
