📚 Grammar
先……然后…… first… then…
我先坐蓝线坐1站，到city hall，换红线坐1站，就到了。 wǒ xiān zuò lán xiàn zuò yī zhàn, dào city hall, huàn hóng xiàn zuò yī zhàn, jiù dào le First I take the blue line for 1 stop to City Hall, then transfer to the red line for 1 stop and I'm there.
我先洗澡然后睡觉。 wǒ xiān xǐ zǎo rán hòu shuì jiào I take a shower first then sleep.
打扫 / 收拾 to clean / to tidy up
我在打扫房间。 wǒ zài dǎ sǎo fáng jiān I'm cleaning the room.
请你收拾一下桌子。 qǐng nǐ shōu shi yī xià zhuō zi Please tidy up the table.
转车 / 换 / 蓝线 / 红线 transfer / change / subway lines
在这里换车。 zài zhè lǐ huàn chē Transfer here.
蓝线和红线在city hall换乘。 lán xiàn hé hóng xiàn zài city hall huàn chéng The blue line and red line connect at City Hall.
概念 / 一线城市 concept / first-tier city
一线城市的概念是什么？ yī xiàn chéng shì de gài niàn shì shén me What is the concept of a first-tier city?
长沙有一千多万人。 cháng shā yǒu yī qiān duō wàn rén Changsha has over 10 million people.
长沙是新一线城市。 cháng shā shì xīn yī xiàn chéng shì Changsha is a new first-tier city.
凉快 / 夏天 cool / summer
今天很凉快。 jīn tiān hěn liáng kuai Today is very cool.
瑞典的夏天很凉快。 ruì diǎn de xià tiān hěn liáng kuai Summers in Sweden are very cool.
小龙虾 / 炖 / 放凉 / 剥 crayfish / stew / cool down / peel
瑞典人用啤酒和小茴香炖小龙虾，放凉后就着白酒和啤酒一起吃。 ruì diǎn rén yòng pí jiǔ hé xiǎo huí xiāng dùn xiǎo lóng xiā, fàng liáng hòu jiù zhe bái jiǔ hé pí jiǔ yì qǐ chī Swedes stew crayfish with beer and dill, let it cool, then eat it with liquor and beer.
你一定要试一下！ nǐ yí dìng yào shì yī xià You must try it!
我很少吃小龙虾，因为很麻烦。 wǒ hěn shǎo chī xiǎo lóng xiā, yīn wèi hěn má fan I rarely eat crayfish because it's too much trouble.
我不想剥虾。 wǒ bù xiǎng bāo xiā I don't want to peel the shrimp.
剥橙子和柚子。 bāo chéng zi hé yòu zi Peel oranges and pomelos.
