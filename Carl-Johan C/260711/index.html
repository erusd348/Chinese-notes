📚 Grammar
变懒了 / 费时间 / 浪费 / 划不来 laziness & waste
我最近变懒了，什么都不想做。 wǒ zuì jìn biàn lǎn le, shén me dōu bù xiǎng zuò I've become lazy recently, I don't want to do anything.
做这件事很费时间。 zuò zhè jiàn shì hěn fèi shí jiān Doing this is very time-consuming.
打游戏浪费太多时间了。 dǎ yóu xì làng fèi tài duō shí jiān le Playing games wastes too much time.
买这个划不来。 mǎi zhè ge huà bù lái Buying this is not worth it.
金融工作 finance careers
他在金融中心工作，是一个银行分析师。 tā zài jīn róng zhōng xīn gōng zuò, shì yī ge yín háng fēn xī shī He works in a financial center, he's a bank analyst.
这个工作需要定性的分析，也需要定量的数据。 zhè ge gōng zuò xū yào dìng xìng de fēn xī, yě xū yào dìng liàng de shù jù This job requires qualitative analysis and also quantitative data.
风险评估是精算师的工作。 fēng xiǎn píng gū shì jīng suàn shī de gōng zuò Risk assessment is an actuary's job.
他在数学方面很厉害。 tā zài shù xué fāng miàn hěn lì hai He's very strong in math.
游戏 gaming
Split fiction 是我们玩的第一个游戏。 Split fiction shì wǒ men wán de dì yī ge yóu xì Split Fiction is the first game we played.
你觉得好玩吗？ nǐ jué de hǎo wán ma Do you think it's fun?
这是双人游戏，需要配合。 zhè shì shuāng rén yóu xì, xū yào pèi hé This is a two-player game, it requires cooperation.
游戏的剧情和技术都很不错。 yóu xì de jù qíng hé jì shù dōu hěn bù cuò The game's plot and technology are both quite good.
我偶尔打游戏，不经常玩。 wǒ ǒu ěr dǎ yóu xì, bù jīng cháng wán I play games occasionally, not often.
