📚 Grammar
肉 / 鸡肉 / 牛肉 / 猪肉 meat types
我喜欢吃牛肉。 wǒ xǐ huān chī niú ròu I like to eat beef.
你中午吃了什么？我中午吃了牛肉。 nǐ zhōng wǔ chī le shén me? wǒ zhōng wǔ chī le niú ròu What did you eat for lunch? I ate beef for lunch.
你晚上吃了什么？我晚上吃了鸡肉。 nǐ wǎn shàng chī le shén me? wǒ wǎn shàng chī le jī ròu What did you eat for dinner? I ate chicken for dinner.
好吃 tasty
鸡心很好吃。 jī xīn hěn hǎo chī Chicken heart is very tasty.
煎饼好吃吗？ jiān bǐng hǎo chī ma Are the pancakes tasty?
喝 / 好喝 to drink / tasty (drinks)
我喝茶和水。 wǒ hē chá hé shuǐ I drink tea and water.
你喜欢喝什么？我喜欢喝啤酒。 nǐ xǐ huān hē shén me? wǒ xǐ huān hē pí jiǔ What do you like to drink? I like to drink beer.
酒不好喝。 jiǔ bù hǎo hē Alcohol doesn't taste good.
你早上喝什么？我早上喝咖啡或者茶。 nǐ zǎo shang hē shén me? wǒ zǎo shang hē kā fēi huò zhě chá What do you drink in the morning? I drink coffee or tea.
有 / 没有 have / don't have
我没有吃晚饭。 wǒ méi yǒu chī wǎn fàn I haven't eaten dinner.
我没有喝咖啡。 wǒ méi yǒu hē kā fēi I didn't drink coffee.
饿 / 渴 hungry / thirsty
我很饿。你饿吗？ wǒ hěn è. nǐ è ma I'm very hungry. Are you hungry?
我饿了。你饿了吗？ wǒ è le. nǐ è le ma I'm hungry. Are you hungry yet?
我不饿。 wǒ bú è I'm not hungry.
我很渴。 wǒ hěn kě I'm very thirsty.
你渴吗？ nǐ kě ma Are you thirsty?
