📚 Grammar
怎么 / 怎么样 how / how is
怎么说？ zěn me shuō How do you say it?
你说什么？ nǐ shuō shén me What did you say?
你今天怎么样？ nǐ jīn tiān zěn me yàng How are you today?
晚饭 dinner
你晚上吃了什么？ nǐ wǎn shang chī le shén me What did you eat in the evening?
你晚饭吃了什么？ nǐ wǎn fàn chī le shén me What did you eat for dinner?
对不起 / 关系 sorry / relationship
对不起！ duì bu qǐ Sorry!
我对不起你！ wǒ duì bu qǐ nǐ I let you down!
没关系。 méi guān xi It doesn't matter.
我们的关系很好。 wǒ men de guān xi hěn hǎo Our relationship is very good.
你和她的关系好吗？ nǐ hé tā de guān xi hǎo ma Is your relationship with her good?
V+不起 (structure) can't afford to / look down on
看不起 kàn bu qǐ to look down on
我看不起你！ wǒ kàn bu qǐ nǐ I look down on you!
买不起 mǎi bu qǐ can't afford
我买不起房子。 wǒ mǎi bu qǐ fáng zi I can't afford a house.
