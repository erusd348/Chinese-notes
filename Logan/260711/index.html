📚 Grammar
Time + place + action (structure) sequence
你中午吃了什么？ nǐ zhōng wǔ chī le shén me What did you eat at noon?
我中午在家吃了馄饨。 wǒ zhōng wǔ zài jiā chī le hún tun I ate wontons at home at noon.
你晚上吃什么？ nǐ wǎn shang chī shén me What do you eat in the evening?
我晚上吃北京烤鸭。 wǒ wǎn shang chī běi jīng kǎo yā I eat Peking duck in the evening.

💬 Sentences
你吃……了吗？ (structure) Have you eaten ...?
你吃饭了吗？ nǐ chī fàn le ma Have you eaten?
是的，我吃了。 shì de, wǒ chī le Yes, I ate.
我没吃。 wǒ méi chī I didn't eat.
你吃午饭了吗？ nǐ chī wǔ fàn le ma Have you eaten lunch?
我吃午饭了。 wǒ chī wǔ fàn le I ate lunch.
