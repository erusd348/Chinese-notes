📚 Grammar
次数 / 每星期 frequency / per week
我一个星期去五次健身房。 wǒ yí gè xīng qī qù wǔ cì jiàn shēn fáng I go to the gym five times a week.
聚会 party
上个周末我去了两个朋友的生日聚会。 shàng gè zhōu mò wǒ qù le liǎng gè péng yǒu de shēng rì jù huì Last weekend I went to two friends' birthday parties.
在生日聚会上，我们吃饭聊天。 zài shēng rì jù huì shàng, wǒ men chī fàn liáo tiān At the birthday party, we ate and chatted.
我们吃吃喝喝，然后聊天。 wǒ men chī chī hē hē, rán hòu liáo tiān We ate and drank, then chatted.
在……上 during (an event)
在课上，我们学中文。 zài kè shàng, wǒ men xué zhōng wén In class, we study Chinese.
熬夜 / 午休 / 摸鱼 staying up / napping / slacking
我昨天熬夜了。 wǒ zuó tiān áo yè le I stayed up late yesterday.
你吃午饭后休息吗？我常常吃午饭后休息。 nǐ chī wǔ fàn hòu xiū xi ma? wǒ cháng cháng chī wǔ fàn hòu xiū xi Do you rest after lunch? I often rest after lunch.
他上班的时候摸鱼。 tā shàng bān de shí hou mō yú He slacks off at work.
会 + V can (know how to)
我会踢足球。 wǒ huì tī zú qiú I can play soccer.
我会打排球。 wǒ huì dǎ pái qiú I can play volleyball.
他很笨。 tā hěn bèn He is very clumsy.
她很矮。 tā hěn ǎi She is very short.
V + 过 (experience) have done before
三年前，我教过别人做HR的工作。 sān nián qián, wǒ jiāo guò bié rén zuò HR de gōng zuò Three years ago, I taught someone to do HR work.
S V + 了 + duration action duration
我睡了8个小时。 wǒ shuì le bā gè xiǎo shí I slept for 8 hours.
我睡觉睡了8个小时。 wǒ shuì jiào shuì le bā gè xiǎo shí I slept for 8 hours (emphasis).
