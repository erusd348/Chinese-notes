📚 Grammar
换 + obj to change/exchange something
换电池 huàn diàn chí to change the battery
换钱 huàn qián to exchange money
我换手机了。 wǒ huàn shǒu jī le I changed my phone.
我换了一个手机。 wǒ huàn le yī ge shǒu jī I changed (to) a new phone.
我换车了。 wǒ huàn chē le I changed my car.
我换工作了。 wǒ huàn gōng zuò le I changed jobs.
我换对象了。 wǒ huàn duì xiàng le I changed partners.
回 + place to return to a place
我每年回意大利4次。 wǒ měi nián huí yì dà lì sì cì I return to Italy 4 times a year.
我住在意大利。 wǒ zhù zài yì dà lì I live in Italy.
每年12月我在意大利住一个月。 měi nián shí èr yuè wǒ zài yì dà lì zhù yī ge yuè Every December I stay in Italy for a month.
我妈妈生日的时候，我回去。 wǒ mā ma shēng rì de shí hou, wǒ huí qù When it's my mom's birthday, I go back.
一模一样 vs 很像 exactly the same vs very similar
你们两个人很像。 nǐ men liǎng ge rén hěn xiàng You two are very similar.
我们两个人性格不像。 wǒ men liǎng ge rén xìng gé bù xiàng Our personalities aren't similar.
你有你们两个人的照片吗？ nǐ yǒu nǐ men liǎng ge rén de zhào piàn ma Do you have a photo of you two?
负责 to be responsible for
你负责赚钱养家，我负责貌美如花。 nǐ fù zé zhuàn qián yǎng jiā, wǒ fù zé mào měi rú huā You are responsible for making money and supporting the family, I am responsible for being beautiful.
我对这个错负责。 wǒ duì zhè ge cuò fù zé I take responsibility for this mistake.
我的错。 wǒ de cuò My fault.
我会对你负责。 wǒ huì duì nǐ fù zé I will take responsibility for you.
作为HR我负责设计和开发公司的培训项目。 zuò wéi HR wǒ fù zé shè jì hé kāi fā gōng sī de péi xùn xiàng mù As an HR, I am responsible for designing and developing the company's training programs.
