📚 Grammar
有的……有的…… some... some...
有的人很好，有的人不好。 yǒu de rén hěn hǎo, yǒu de rén bù hǎo Some people are good, some people are not.
有的东西很贵，有的东西很便宜。 yǒu de dōng xi hěn guì, yǒu de dōng xi hěn pián yi Some things are expensive, some things are cheap.
这里 / 那里 here / there
我在我爸妈这里。 wǒ zài wǒ bà mā zhè lǐ I'm at my parents' place.
我去我爸妈那里。 wǒ qù wǒ bà mā nà lǐ I'm going to my parents' place.
A 离 B 远 / 近 A is far/near B
你爸妈那儿离我这儿很远。 nǐ bà mā nàr lí wǒ zhèr hěn yuǎn Your parents' place is very far from mine.
年龄 age
你几岁？ nǐ jǐ suì How old are you? (to children)
你多大了？ nǐ duō dà le How old are you?
您多大年纪？ nín duō dà nián jì How old are you? (polite)
我三十三岁。 wǒ sān shí sān suì I'm 33 years old.
我们一样大。 wǒ men yī yàng dà We're the same age.
你比我大五个月。 nǐ bǐ wǒ dà wǔ ge yuè You're five months older than me.
日期 / 时间 date and time
今天几月几号？ jīn tiān jǐ yuè jǐ hào What's the date today?
今天七月十号。 jīn tiān qī yuè shí hào Today is July 10th.
今天星期几？ jīn tiān xīng qī jǐ What day of the week is today?
今天星期五。 jīn tiān xīng qī wǔ Today is Friday.
你的生日是什么时候？ nǐ de shēng rì shì shén me shí hou When is your birthday?
我的生日是1992年10月14号。 wǒ de shēng rì shì yī jiǔ jiǔ èr nián shí yuè shí sì hào My birthday is October 14, 1992.
时间表达 telling time
一点一刻 yī diǎn yī kè 1:15
一点半 yī diǎn bàn 1:30
一点零五分 yī diǎn líng wǔ fēn 1:05
一点差五分 yī diǎn chà wǔ fēn 12:55
A 比 B adj (structure) comparison
你的中文比我的好很多。 nǐ de zhōng wén bǐ wǒ de hǎo hěn duō Your Chinese is much better than mine.
中国的人比荷兰的人多很多。 zhōng guó de rén bǐ hé lán de rén duō hěn duō Chinese people are much more numerous than Dutch people.
意大利的咖啡比荷兰的咖啡好很多。 yì dà lì de kā fēi bǐ hé lán de kā fēi hǎo hěn duō Italian coffee is much better than Dutch coffee.
B 没有 A adj (structure) B is not as adj as A
我的中文没有你的好。 wǒ de zhōng wén méi yǒu nǐ de hǎo My Chinese is not as good as yours.
荷兰的人没有中国的人多。 hé lán de rén méi yǒu zhōng guó de rén duō Dutch people are not as numerous as Chinese people.
我没有你大。 wǒ méi yǒu nǐ dà I'm not as old as you.
在办公室工作没有在家工作好。 zài bàn gōng shì gōng zuò méi yǒu zài jiā gōng zuò hǎo Working at the office is not as good as working from home.
更 / 差 more / to lack
荷兰的工作更多。 hé lán de gōng zuò gèng duō The Netherlands has more jobs.
意大利更漂亮。 yì dà lì gèng piào liang Italy is even more beautiful.
我差一块。 wǒ chà yī kuài I'm short one kuai.
我差一个对象。 wǒ chà yī ge duì xiàng I'm lacking a partner.
我们差一个人。 wǒ men chà yī ge rén We're one person short.
