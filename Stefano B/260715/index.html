📚 Grammar
习惯 habit / to get used to
我习惯了不好的天气。 wǒ xí guàn le bù hǎo de tiān qì I'm used to the bad weather.
我习惯了。 wǒ xí guàn le I'm used to it.
这是我的习惯。 zhè shì wǒ de xí guàn This is my habit.
比较 comparison review
我的胡子比你的多。 wǒ de hú zi bǐ nǐ de duō My beard is more than yours.
我的头发比你的长。 wǒ de tóu fa bǐ nǐ de cháng My hair is longer than yours.
你的眼睛没有我的大。 nǐ de yǎn jīng méi yǒu wǒ de dà Your eyes are not as big as mine.
你比我起床更早。 nǐ bǐ wǒ qǐ chuáng gèng zǎo You get up even earlier than me.
洗漱 / 早饭 wash up / breakfast
起床后我先洗漱然后吃早饭。 qǐ chuáng hòu wǒ xiān xǐ shù rán hòu chī zǎo fàn After getting up I wash up first, then eat breakfast.
早上我习惯喝牛奶吃鸡蛋。 zǎo shang wǒ xí guàn hē niú nǎi chī jī dàn In the morning I'm used to drinking milk and eating eggs.
鸡蛋和牛奶有很多蛋白质，它们也很好吃。 jī dàn hé niú nǎi yǒu hěn duō dàn bái zhì, tā men yě hěn hǎo chī Eggs and milk have a lot of protein, and they taste good too.
锻炼 / 工作 exercise / work
早饭后我去健身房锻炼。 zǎo fàn hòu wǒ qù jiàn shēn fáng duàn liàn After breakfast I go to the gym to work out.
健身很累，但是我喜欢它给我的感觉。 jiàn shēn hěn lèi, dàn shì wǒ xǐ huan tā gěi wǒ de gǎn jué Working out is tiring, but I like how it makes me feel.
我工作的时候很忙。 wǒ gōng zuò de shí hou hěn máng I'm very busy when I work.
📖 课文：我的日常 text: my daily routine
我每天六点起床。起床后我先洗漱然后吃早饭。早上我习惯喝牛奶吃鸡蛋，因为鸡蛋和牛奶有很多蛋白质，它们也很好吃。早饭后我去健身房锻炼。健身很累，但是我喜欢它给我的感觉。健身后，我开始工作。我工作的时候很忙。 wǒ měi tiān liù diǎn qǐ chuáng. qǐ chuáng hòu wǒ xiān xǐ shù rán hòu chī zǎo fàn. zǎo shang wǒ xí guàn hē niú nǎi chī jī dàn, yīn wèi jī dàn hé niú nǎi yǒu hěn duō dàn bái zhì, tā men yě hěn hǎo chī. zǎo fàn hòu wǒ qù jiàn shēn fáng duàn liàn. jiàn shēn hěn lèi, dàn shì wǒ xǐ huan tā gěi wǒ de gǎn jué. jiàn shēn hòu, wǒ kāi shǐ gōng zuò. wǒ gōng zuò de shí hou hěn máng. I get up at 6 every day. After getting up I wash up first, then eat breakfast. In the morning I'm used to drinking milk and eating eggs, because eggs and milk have a lot of protein, and they taste good too. After breakfast I go to the gym to work out. Working out is tiring, but I like how it makes me feel. After working out, I start working. I'm very busy when I work.
