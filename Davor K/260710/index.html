📚 Grammar
Pronoun + 们 (structure) plural pronouns
你们 nǐ men you (plural)
我们 wǒ men we
他们 tā men they (men or mixed)
她们 tā men they (women only)
它们 tā men they (animals)
S + Adv + Adj (structure) subject + adverb + adjective
马其顿很好。 mǎ qí dùn hěn hǎo Macedonia is very good.
悉尼很好。 xī ní hěn hǎo Sydney is very good.
悉尼不好。 xī ní bù hǎo Sydney is not good.
墨尔本一般。 mò ěr běn yī bān Melbourne is so-so.
吗 — yes/no question (structure) adding 吗 makes a statement into a yes/no question
你好吗？ nǐ hǎo ma How are you?
你是中国人吗？ nǐ shì zhōng guó rén ma Are you Chinese?
马其顿好吗？ mǎ qí dùn hǎo ma Is Macedonia good?
呢 — how about (structure) pronoun + 呢 = how about X?
我很好。你呢？ wǒ hěn hǎo. nǐ ne I'm fine. How about you?
我也很好。 wǒ yě hěn hǎo I'm also fine.
