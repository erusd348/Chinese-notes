前天晚上我喝了一大杯奶茶，我一晚上没睡着。Qiántiān wǎnshang wǒ hēle yī dà bēi nǎichá, wǒ yī wǎnshang méi shuìzháo.The night before last, I drank a large cup of milk tea and couldn’t sleep all night.
奶茶/咖啡很提神。Nǎichá/kāfēi hěn tíshén.Milk tea/coffee is very energizing.
奶茶没有咖啡提神。Nǎichá méiyǒu kāfēi tíshén.Milk tea isn’t as energizing as coffee.
过了1点就不能喝咖啡了。Guòle yī diǎn jiù bù néng hē kāfēi le.I can’t drink coffee after 1 o’clock.
从去年开始，在Netflix有很多广告。Cóng qùnián kāishǐ, zài Netflix yǒu hěnduō guǎnggào.Since last year, there have been many ads on Netflix.
