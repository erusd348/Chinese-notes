📚 Grammar
从来不/没 + 动词
(Structure) Never + verb (indicating a habitual or experiential negation)
我从来没去过北京。Wǒ cónglái méi qù guò Běijīng.I have never been to Beijing.
你最好练习中文。Nǐ zuì hǎo liànxí Zhōngwén.You had better practice Chinese.
你最好早点睡觉。Nǐ zuì hǎo zǎo diǎn shuìjiào.You had better go to sleep early.
