📚 Grammar
自然 natural; nature
他说话很不自然。 tā shuō huà hěn bù zì rán He speaks very unnaturally.
我们要保护大自然。 wǒ men yào bǎo hù dà zì rán We should protect nature.
性格 personality
她性格很好。 tā xìng gé hěn hǎo She has a good personality.
她很有性格。 tā hěn yǒu xìng gé She has a strong character.
我们性格很合得来。 wǒ men xìng gé hěn hé de lái Our personalities get along well.
我们性格不合。 wǒ men xìng gé bù hé Our personalities don't match.
性格互补的关系很重要。 xìng gé hù bǔ de guān xi hěn zhòng yào A complementary relationship in personality is important.
互相 mutually; each other
我们互相理解。 wǒ men hù xiāng lǐ jiě We understand each other.
我们互相帮助。 wǒ men hù xiāng bāng zhù We help each other.
我们可以互相学习。 wǒ men kě yǐ hù xiāng xué xí We can learn from each other.
几乎 almost
他几乎快死了。 tā jī hū kuài sǐ le He almost died.
英国有名的大学我几乎都去了。 yīng guó yǒu míng de dà xué wǒ jī hū dōu qù le I've been to almost all the famous universities in the UK.
