阿伦又高又帅。Ā lún yòu gāo yòu shuài.Alan is both tall and handsome.

他又烦人又懒。Tā yòu fánrén yòu lǎn.He is both annoying and lazy.
他又有钱，做饭又好吃。Tā yòu yǒuqián, zuò fàn yòu hǎochī.He is both rich and cooks well. (Lit.: ...and his cooking is delicious.)
他又聪明，工作又努力。Tā yòu cōngmíng, gōngzuò yòu nǔlì.He is both smart and works hard.
我的女儿又可爱，成绩又好。Wǒ de nǚ'ér yòu kě'ài, chéngjī yòu hǎo.My daughter is both cute and has good grades.
新加坡又干净又漂亮。Xīnjiāpō yòu gānjìng yòu piàoliang.Singapore is both clean and beautiful.
印度又大又热闹。Yìndù yòu dà yòu rènao.India is both big and lively.
这个东西价钱又贵，质量又差。Zhège dōngxi jiàqián yòu guì, zhìliàng yòu chà.This thing is both expensive and of poor quality.
在新加坡没有什么是便宜的。Zài Xīnjiāpō méiyǒu shénme shì piányi de.In Singapore, nothing is cheap.
小米的手机价钱又便宜，质量又好。Xiǎomǐ de shǒujī jiàqián yòu piányi, zhìliàng yòu hǎo.Xiaomi's phones are both cheap and good quality.
我觉得我的母语的电影很好看。Wǒ juéde wǒ de mǔyǔ de diànyǐng hěn hǎokàn.I think movies in my native language are very good.
