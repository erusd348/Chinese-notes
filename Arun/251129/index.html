📚 Grammar
About the Past
他以前在新加坡学习。Tā yǐqián zài Xīnjiāpō xuéxí.He studied in Singapore before.
Asking and Telling Sizes
你的鞋子是多大码的？Nǐ de xiézi shì duōdà mǎ de?What size are your shoes?
我的鞋子是42码（的）。Wǒ de xiézi shì sìshí'èr mǎ (de).My shoes are size 42.
你穿多大码的（鞋子）？Nǐ chuān duōdà mǎ de (xiézi)?What size (shoes) do you wear?
我穿42码的鞋子。Wǒ chuān sìshí'èr mǎ de xiézi.I wear size 42 shoes.
Making Requests
这儿有人吗？Zhèr yǒu rén ma?Is this seat taken? (Lit: Is there someone here?)
我可以试一下/试吃吗？Wǒ kěyǐ shì yīxià / shìchī ma?Can I try it / have a taste?
有(一)点儿 + Adj.
我有(一)点儿饿。Wǒ yǒu (yì)diǎnr è.I'm a little hungry.
今天有一点儿热。Jīntiān yǒu yìdiǎnr rè.It's a little hot today.
一点儿 + Noun
我买了一点儿水果。Wǒ mǎile yìdiǎnr shuǐguǒ.I bought a few fruits.
多吃/喝一点儿。Duō chī / hē yìdiǎnr.Eat/Drink a little more.
多喝一点儿热水。Duō hē yìdiǎnr rèshuǐ.Drink a little more hot water.
Making Comparisons (A 比 B Adj. 一点儿/很多)
Arun比我高一点儿/很多。Arun bǐ wǒ gāo yìdiǎnr / hěnduō.Arun is a little / much taller than me.
我比他矮一点儿。Wǒ bǐ tā ǎi yìdiǎnr.I am a little shorter than him.
苹果比橙子便宜一点儿。Píngguǒ bǐ chéngzi piányi yìdiǎnr.Apples are a little cheaper than oranges.
橙子比苹果好吃一点儿。Chéngzi bǐ píngguǒ hǎochī yìdiǎnr.Oranges are a little more delicious than apples.
Using 适合 (shìhé)
这件衣服很适合你。Zhè jiàn yīfu hěn shìhé nǐ.This piece of clothing suits you very well.
她很适合你。Tā hěn shìhé nǐ.She is very suitable for you. / You two are a good match.
包子适合早上吃。Bāozi shìhé zǎoshang chī.Baozi are suitable for eating in the morning.
