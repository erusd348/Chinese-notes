我们在中国见过这个水果的树。Wǒmen zài Zhōngguó jiànguò zhège shuǐguǒ de shù.We’ve seen the tree of this fruit in China.
1斤 = 10两
1 jin = 10 liang (traditional Chinese weight unit)
你们摘过菜吗？Nǐmen zhāi guò cài ma?Have you ever picked vegetables?
我爸妈种了很多菜。Wǒ bàmā zhòngle hěnduō cài.My parents grow a lot of vegetables.
这是今天早上摘的。Zhè shì jīntiān zǎoshang zhāi de.This was picked this morning.
这个衣服是在新加坡买的。Zhège yīfu shì zài Xīnjiāpō mǎi de.This clothing was bought in Singapore.
这个衣服是我买的。Zhège yīfu shì wǒ mǎi de.This clothing was bought by me.
这个衣服是去年买的。Zhège yīfu shì qùnián mǎi de.This clothing was bought last year.
我女儿是2020年生的。Wǒ nǚ’ér shì èr líng èr líng nián shēng de.My daughter was born in 2020.
我们是2016年认识的。Wǒmen shì èr líng yī liù nián rènshi de.We met in 2016.
你们是怎么认识的？Nǐmen shì zěnme rènshi de?How did you meet?
我们是爸妈介绍（认识）的。Wǒmen shì bàmā jièshào (rènshi) de.We were introduced by our parents.
