我们一样大。Wǒmen yīyàng dà.We are the same age.
我孩子和你孩子一样大。Wǒ háizi hé nǐ háizi yīyàng dà.My child is the same age as yours.

我孩子是2020年10月22号生的。Wǒ háizi shì 2020 nián 10 yuè 22 hào shēng de.My child was born on October 22, 2020.
我孩子今年5岁了。Wǒ háizi jīnnián 5 suì le.My child is 5 years old this year.
别人BiérénOther people / Others

Adj + 的 = Noun
Adjective + 的 = Noun (e.g., "好吃的" = "delicious food")
A和B 一样 + Adj
"A and B are the same [adjective]"
