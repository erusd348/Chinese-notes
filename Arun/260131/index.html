📚 Grammar
带 + Person + 去 + Verb (Object)
Means "to take someone to do something".
带 + Person + 去 + Place
Means "to take someone to a place".
Subject + 用 + Tool + Verb
Pattern for stating the tool or means used to perform an action.
Noun + 很好用/常用
Describes that a noun is very useful or commonly used.
Subject + 打算 + Verb (Phrase)
Expresses one's plan or intention to do something.
Subject + 有什么打算？
A question asking about someone's plans.
别/不要 + 乱 + Verb
A command or advice against doing something carelessly, randomly, or improperly.
去 + Place + 旅行
Pattern for "to travel to a place".
