A 比 B + AdjectiveA bǐ B + adj.A is more [adj.] than B.
火鸡 比 鸡 好吃。Huǒjī bǐ jī hǎochī.Turkey is more delicious than chicken.
B 没有 A + AdjectiveB méiyǒu A + adj.B is not as [adj.] as A.
鸡 没有 火鸡 好吃。Jī méiyǒu huǒjī hǎochī.Chicken is not as delicious as turkey.

Location + 里[Location] + lǐin/at [location]

学校里有很多学生。Xuéxiào li yǒu hěn duō xuéshēng.There are many students in the school.
我家里有一个猫。Wǒ jiā li yǒu yí gè māo.There is a cat in my home.
晚上办公室里没有人。Wǎnshang bàngōngshì li méiyǒu rén.There is no one in the office at night.
