📚 Grammar
S + 跟 + Person + 一起 + V together with someone
周末我跟我朋友一起做烧烤。 zhōu mò wǒ gēn wǒ péng you yī qǐ zuò shāo kǎo On the weekend, I do BBQ together with my friends.
Age: Number + 岁 stating age
王小朋今年五岁。 wáng xiǎo péng jīn nián wǔ suì Wang Xiaopeng is five years old this year.
Family: 有 + Number + 口人 family size
他家有四口人：爸爸、妈妈、弟弟和他。 tā jiā yǒu sì kǒu rén: bà ba, mā ma, dì di hé tā His family has four people: dad, mom, little brother, and him.
他爸爸今年三十五岁，在公司工作，是职员。 tā bà ba jīn nián sān shí wǔ suì, zài gōng sī gōng zuò, shì zhí yuán His dad is 35, works at a company, is an employee.
他妈妈今年三十二岁，是老师。 tā mā ma jīn nián sān shí èr suì, shì lǎo shī His mom is 32, is a teacher.
他弟弟今年也五岁，他们是双胞胎。 tā dì di jīn nián yě wǔ suì, tā men shì shuāng bāo tāi His little brother is also five, they are twins.
喜欢 + V like to do sth
王小朋喜欢看动画片，喜欢吃冰淇淋，喜欢喝可口可乐。 wáng xiǎo péng xǐ huān kàn dòng huà piàn, xǐ huān chī bīng qí lín, xǐ huān hē kě kǒu kě lè Wang Xiaopeng likes watching cartoons, eating ice cream, and drinking Coke.
王小朋每天和弟弟王小友一起去幼儿园。 wáng xiǎo péng měi tiān hé dì di wáng xiǎo yǒu yī qǐ qù yòu ér yuán Wang Xiaopeng goes to kindergarten together with his little brother Wang Xiaoyou every day.
