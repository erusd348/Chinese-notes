你家在多少楼？Nǐ jiā zài duōshǎo lóu?Which floor is your home on?
我家在1楼。Wǒ jiā zài yī lóu.My home is on the 1st floor.
我家在15号楼44楼。Wǒ jiā zài shíwǔ hào lóu sìshísì lóu.My home is in Building 15, Floor 44.
