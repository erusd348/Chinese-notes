你身体好吗？Nǐ shēntǐ hǎo ma?How is your health? (Are you well?)
你身体怎么样？Nǐ shēntǐ zěnmeyàng?How is your health?
我身体很好。Wǒ shēntǐ hěn hǎo.I am very healthy. / I feel very well.
我身体比较好。Wǒ shēntǐ bǐjiào hǎo.My health is relatively good.
我身材比较好。Wǒ shēncái bǐjiào hǎo.I have a relatively good figure.
最近我身材不好。我吃太多了。Zuìjìn wǒ shēncái bù hǎo. Wǒ chī tài duō le.My figure hasn't been good lately. I've been eating too much.
披萨比米饭好吃。Pīsà bǐ mǐfàn hǎo chī.Pizza is more delicious than rice.
咖啡比可乐好喝。Kāfēi bǐ kělè hǎo hē.Coffee tastes better than cola.
我爱人比我好看。Wǒ àiren bǐ wǒ hǎokàn.My spouse is better-looking than me.
我身体比他好。Wǒ shēntǐ bǐ tā hǎo.I am healthier than him.
我身材比他好。Wǒ shēncái bǐ tā hǎo.I have a better figure than him.
我成绩比他好。Wǒ chéngjī bǐ tā hǎo.My grades are better than his.
他身材没有我好。Tā shēncái méiyǒu wǒ hǎo.His figure is not as good as mine.
米饭没有披萨好吃。Mǐfàn méiyǒu pīsà hǎo chī.Rice is not as delicious as pizza.
可乐没有咖啡好喝。Kělè méiyǒu kāfēi hǎo hē.Cola is not as good as coffee.
我的猫没有我聪明。Wǒ de māo méiyǒu wǒ cōngmíng.My cat is not as smart as me.
他成绩没有我好。Tā chéngjī méiyǒu wǒ hǎo.His grades are not as good as mine.
