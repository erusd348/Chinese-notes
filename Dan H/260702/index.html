📚 Grammar
从……到…… (structure) from ... to ...
从8点到12点 cóng bā diǎn dào shí èr diǎn from 8 to 12
从我家到咖啡店 cóng wǒ jiā dào kā fēi diàn from my home to the cafe
你从几点到几点工作？ nǐ cóng jǐ diǎn dào jǐ diǎn gōng zuò From what time to what time do you work?
我从10点到6点工作。 wǒ cóng shí diǎn dào liù diǎn gōng zuò I work from 10 to 6.
你从几岁到几岁住在波士顿？ nǐ cóng jǐ suì dào jǐ suì zhù zài bō shì dùn From what age to what age did you live in Boston?
我从出生到18岁住在波士顿。 wǒ cóng chū shēng dào shí bā suì zhù zài bō shì dùn I lived in Boston from birth to 18.
差 + N (structure) short of / lacking
我差钱。 wǒ chà qián I'm short on money.
我差觉。 wǒ chà jiào I lack sleep.
我们去看足球比赛了。 wǒ men qù kàn zú qiú bǐ sài le We went to watch a soccer match.
我喜欢西班牙队。 wǒ xǐ huān xī bān yá duì I like the Spanish team.
你今天6点起床了。 wǒ jīn tiān liù diǎn qǐ chuáng le I got up at 6 today.
你白天睡觉了吗？ nǐ bái tiān shuì jiào le ma Did you sleep during the day?
我没有睡觉。 wǒ méi yǒu shuì jiào I didn't sleep.
你睡多长时间？ nǐ shuì duō cháng shí jiān How long did you sleep?
我睡1个小时。 wǒ shuì yī ge xiǎo shí I slept for one hour.
