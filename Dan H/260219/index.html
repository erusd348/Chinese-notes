Subject adv adj (Chinese)
Subject is adj (English)

什么时候

起床
get up
你每天什么时候起床？Nǐ měitiān shénme shíhou qǐchuáng?What time do you get up every day?
我每天7点起床。Wǒ měitiān qī diǎn qǐchuáng.I get up at 7 o’clock every day.
你每天什么时候工作？Nǐ měitiān shénme shíhou gōngzuò?What time do you work every day?
我每天9点工作。Wǒ měitiān jiǔ diǎn gōngzuò.I work at 9 o’clock every day.
你每天什么时候睡觉？Nǐ měitiān shénme shíhou shuìjiào?What time do you go to sleep every day?
我每天11点睡觉。Wǒ měitiān shíyī diǎn shuìjiào.I go to sleep at 11 o’clock every day.
