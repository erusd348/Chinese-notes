💬 Sentences
你家多大？nǐ jiā duō dà?How big is your home?
你（今年）多大(了)？nǐ (jīnnián) duō dà (le)?How old are you (this year)?
我（今年）34岁（了）。wǒ (jīnnián) sānshísì suì (le).I am 34 years old (this year).
你多高？nǐ duō gāo?How tall are you?
我1米82。wǒ yī mǐ bāshí'èr.I am 1.82 meters tall.
您多大年纪？nín duō dà niánjì?How old are you? (formal/for elderly)
我80。wǒ bāshí.I am 80.
他年纪很大。tā niánjì hěn dà.He is very old.
他年纪很小。tā niánjì hěn xiǎo.He is very young.
你几岁？nǐ jǐ suì?How old are you? (for children)
我5岁。wǒ wǔ suì.I am 5 years old.
这个楼有多少年了？zhège lóu yǒu duōshǎo nián le?How many years has this building been here?
你的车有多少年了？nǐ de chē yǒu duōshǎo nián le?How many years has your car been used?
你跟你老婆在一起多少年了？nǐ gēn nǐ lǎopo zài yīqǐ duōshǎo nián le?How many years have you been with your wife?
我跟我老婆在一起4年了。wǒ gēn wǒ lǎopo zài yīqǐ sì nián le.I have been with my wife for 4 years.
我们在一起了。wǒmen zài yīqǐ le.We are together (in a relationship).
我们不在一起了。wǒmen bù zài yīqǐ le.We are not together anymore (broken up).
