📖 Vocabulary
咱们 zán men we (informal; including listener)
好啊 hǎo a okay! (enthusiastic)
好的 hǎo de okay
好吧 hǎo ba fine; alright
出发 chū fā to set out; to depart
出 chū out; to exit
出去 chū qù to go out
出来 chū lái to come out
分钟 fēn zhōng minute
📚 Grammar
咱们 we / let's
这个周末你们出去玩吗？ zhè ge zhōu mò nǐ men chū qù wán ma Are you going out to play this weekend?
咱们什么时候出发？ zán men shén me shí hou chū fā When shall we set out?
咱们10点出发吧。 zán men shí diǎn chū fā ba Let's set out at 10.
出发 / 到 set out / arrive
我9点出发去工作。 wǒ jiǔ diǎn chū fā qù gōng zuò I set out to go to work at 9.
你什么时候到公司？ nǐ shén me shí hou dào gōng sī When do you arrive at the company?
我9点45到公司。 wǒ jiǔ diǎn sì shí wǔ dào gōng sī I arrive at the company at 9:45.
我从星期一到星期五工作。 wǒ cóng xīng qī yī dào xīng qī wǔ gōng zuò I work from Monday to Friday.
从……到…… from...to...
从我家到公司要45分钟。 cóng wǒ jiā dào gōng sī yào sì shí wǔ fēn zhōng It takes 45 minutes from my home to the company.
