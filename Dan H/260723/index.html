📖 Vocabulary
新工作 xīn gōng zuò new job
面试 miàn shì interview
面试官 miàn shì guān interviewer
见面 jiàn miàn to meet
门口 mén kǒu entrance; doorway
等 děng to wait
📚 Grammar
面试 interview
明天我有一个新工作的面试。 míng tiān wǒ yǒu yī gè xīn gōng zuò de miàn shì Tomorrow I have a job interview.
面试官 interviewer
面试官问了我很多问题。 miàn shì guān wèn le wǒ hěn duō wèn tí The interviewer asked me many questions.
A 跟 B 见面 A meets with B
明天我要和面试官见面。 míng tiān wǒ yào hé miàn shì guān jiàn miàn Tomorrow I'm going to meet with the interviewer.
我们8点半在商店见面吧。 wǒ men bā diǎn bàn zài shāng diàn jiàn miàn ba Let's meet at the store at 8:30.
我没和他见面。 wǒ méi hé tā jiàn miàn I didn't meet with him.
你想和她见面吗？ nǐ xiǎng hé tā jiàn miàn ma Do you want to meet with her?
门口 entrance
学校门口 xué xiào mén kǒu school entrance
家门口 jiā mén kǒu home entrance
公司门口 gōng sī mén kǒu company entrance
我们在电影院门口见面吧！ wǒ men zài diàn yǐng yuàn mén kǒu jiàn miàn ba Let's meet at the movie theater entrance!
我八点一刻在电影院门口等你。 wǒ bā diǎn yī kè zài diàn yǐng yuàn mén kǒu děng nǐ I'll wait for you at the movie theater entrance at 8:15.
等 to wait
等一下 děng yī xià wait a moment
等我一下。 děng wǒ yī xià Wait for me a moment.
📖 课文：去颐和园 dialogue: going to the Summer Palace
我们星期天去颐和园，怎么样？ wǒ men xīng qī tiān qù yí hé yuán, zěn me yàng How about we go to the Summer Palace on Sunday?
好啊，我们什么时候出发？ hǎo a, wǒ men shén me shí hòu chū fā Sure, when do we set out?
上午9点，行吗？ shàng wǔ jiǔ diǎn, xíng ma 9 AM, is that okay?
好的，我们什么时候见面？ hǎo de, wǒ men shén me shí hòu jiàn miàn Okay, when do we meet?
9点差5分，我在学校门口等你。 jiǔ diǎn chà wǔ fēn, wǒ zài xué xiào mén kǒu děng nǐ I'll wait for you at the school entrance at 5 to 9.
