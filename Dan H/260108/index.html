📚 Grammar
💬 Sentences
哪 + Noun + 人？nǎ + Noun + rén?Structure “Which + Noun + person?”: Used to ask about someone's origin. 国 = country, 儿/里 = place.
多少钱? / 几块钱?duōshao qián? / jǐ kuài qián?Phrases “How much money?”: “多少钱” is general. “几块钱” implies a small amount (a few dollars).
S + 教 + O1 + O2S + jiāo + O1 + O2Structure “Teach someone something”: S teaches O1 (person) O2 (thing/skill).
你是哪国人？Nǐ shì nǎ guó rén?Which country are you from?
我是美国人。Wǒ shì Měiguó rén.I am American.
你是哪儿人？Nǐ shì nǎr rén?Where are you from?
我是湖北人。Wǒ shì Húběi rén.I am from Hubei province.
他姓孔。Tā xìng Kǒng.His surname is Kong.
我小名是Dan。Wǒ xiǎomíng shì Dan.My childhood name is Dan.
你叫什么（名字）？Nǐ jiào shénme (míngzi)?What is your name?
我叫Daniel。你可以叫我Dan。Wǒ jiào Daniel. Nǐ kěyǐ jiào wǒ Dan.My name is Daniel. You can call me Dan.
你姓什么？Nǐ xìng shénme?What is your surname?
您贵姓？Nín guìxìng?(Polite) What is your surname?
我姓袁。Wǒ xìng Yuán.My surname is Yuan.
纽约的房子很贵。Niǔyuē de fángzi hěn guì.Houses in New York are very expensive.
奥迪很贵。Àodí hěn guì.Audis are expensive.
我有几个朋友。Wǒ yǒu jǐ gè péngyou.I have a few friends.
我买了几本书。Wǒ mǎile jǐ běn shū.I bought a few books.
你吃了几个包子？Nǐ chīle jǐ gè bāozi?How many steamed buns did you eat?
我吃了2个包子。Wǒ chīle liǎng gè bāozi.I ate two steamed buns.
这个多少钱？Zhège duōshao qián?How much is this?
这个几块钱？Zhège jǐ kuài qián?How many dollars is this? (implies it's cheap)
这个2块钱。Zhège liǎng kuài qián.This is two dollars.
您几位？Nín jǐ wèi?How many in your party? (polite)
两位。Liǎng wèi.Two people. (polite)
