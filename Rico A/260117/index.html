📚 Grammar
A 比 B + adjective
(Structure) A is more + adjective + than B.
牛奶比可乐好喝。Niúnǎi bǐ kělè hǎo hē.Milk is tastier than cola.
鸡米花比炸鸡好吃。Jīmǐhuā bǐ zhájī hǎo chī.Popcorn chicken is more delicious than fried chicken.
我比Rico好看。Wǒ bǐ Rico hǎokàn.I am better-looking than Rico.
我比Arina颜值高。Wǒ bǐ Arina yánzhí gāo.I have better looks than Arina.
我比他成绩好。Wǒ bǐ tā chéngjì hǎo.My grades are better than his.
B 没有 A + adj
(Structure) B is not as + adjective + as A.
可乐没有牛奶好喝。Kělè méiyǒu niúnǎi hǎo hē.Cola is not as tasty as milk.
someone/something + 很 + adj
(Structure) Someone/Something is very + adjective.
他颜值很高。Tā yánzhí hěn gāo.He is very good-looking.
我很吃惊。Wǒ hěn chījīng.I am very surprised.
我成绩比他成绩好。Wǒ chéngjì bǐ tā chéngjì hǎo.My grades are better than his grades.
我们要努力学习/工作。Wǒmen yào nǔlì xuéxí/gōngzuò.We must study/work hard.
他学习/工作很努力。Tā xuéxí/gōngzuò hěn nǔlì.He studies/works very hard.
常（常） + 动词
(Structure) Often + verb
我们常（常）吃炸鸡。Wǒmen cháng (cháng) chī zhájī.We often eat fried chicken.
他常常非常烦人。Tā chángcháng fēicháng fánrén.He is often very annoying.
我常常买衣服。Wǒ chángcháng mǎi yīfu.I often buy clothes.
我女儿常常睡觉。Wǒ nǚ'ér chángcháng shuìjiào.My daughter often sleeps.
我常常10点睡觉。Wǒ chángcháng shí diǎn shuìjiào.I often go to bed at 10 o'clock.
你说谎！Nǐ shuōhuǎng!(Structure) You're lying!
你说谎！Nǐ shuōhuǎng!You're lying!
