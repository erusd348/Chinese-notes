📚 Grammar
句子 Sentences
这个炸鸡是在超市买的。Zhège zhájī shì zài chāoshì mǎi de.This fried chicken was bought at the supermarket.
你应该多吃一点。Nǐ yīnggāi duō chī yìdiǎn.You should eat a bit more.
下个星期是圣诞节。Xià gè xīngqī shì Shèngdàn Jié.Next week is Christmas.
你们放假吗？Nǐmen fàngjià ma?Do you have time off (for the holiday)?
我们星期四放假。Wǒmen xīngqīsì fàngjià.We have time off on Thursday.
你们放几天假？Nǐmen fàng jǐ tiān jià?How many days off do you have?
1天半。Yì tiān bàn.One and a half days.
我带（我）宝贝去工作。Wǒ dài (wǒ) bǎobèi qù gōngzuò.I bring my baby to work.
我想在家休息。Wǒ xiǎng zài jiā xiūxi.I want to rest at home.
我家人在一起过圣诞节。Wǒ jiārén zài yìqǐ guò Shèngdàn Jié.My family spends Christmas together.
我宝贝哭了。Wǒ bǎobèi kū le.My baby cried.
太惨了！Tài cǎn le!How tragic!/That's so miserable!
我会说马来语，但是我说得不好。Wǒ huì shuō Mǎláiyǔ, dànshì wǒ shuō de bù hǎo.I can speak Malay, but I don't speak it well.
我教我宝贝看书/走路/说话/翻身。Wǒ jiāo wǒ bǎobèi kàn shū / zǒu lù / shuō huà / fān shēn.I teach my baby to read books / walk / talk / turn over.
你家有几口人？/ 你家有几个人？Nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ kǒu rén? / Nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ gè rén?How many people are in your family?
我办公室来了一个新人。Wǒ bàngōngshì láile yí gè xīnrén.A new person has come to my office.
我们班来了一个新同学。Wǒmen bān láile yí gè xīn tóngxué.A new classmate has come to our class.
