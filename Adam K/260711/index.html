📚 Grammar
Time + V + 了 (structure) past action with time
我早上买了鸡蛋三明治。 wǒ zǎo shang mǎi le jī dàn sān míng zhì I bought an egg sandwich this morning.
我早上喝了咖啡。 wǒ zǎo shang hē le kā fēi I drank coffee this morning.
我早上没喝咖啡。 wǒ zǎo shang méi hē kā fēi I didn't drink coffee this morning.
想 + V (structure) want to do
你想吃什么？ nǐ xiǎng chī shén me What do you want to eat?
我想吃牛肉。 wǒ xiǎng chī niú ròu I want to eat beef.
你想喝什么？ nǐ xiǎng hē shén me What do you want to drink?
我想喝雪碧。 wǒ xiǎng hē xuě bì I want to drink Sprite.
你想买什么？ nǐ xiǎng mǎi shén me What do you want to buy?
我想买书包。 wǒ xiǎng mǎi shū bāo I want to buy a schoolbag.
