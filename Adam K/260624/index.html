📚 Grammar
爱 + V to love doing sth
饺子好吃吗？ jiǎo zi hǎo chī ma Are the dumplings delicious?
饺子很好吃。 jiǎo zi hěn hǎo chī The dumplings are very delicious.
你爱吃饺子吗？ nǐ ài chī jiǎo zi ma Do you love eating dumplings?
我爱吃饺子。 wǒ ài chī jiǎo zi I love eating dumplings.
你爱吃什么？ nǐ ài chī shén me What do you love to eat?
你爱吃什么饺子？ nǐ ài chī shén me jiǎo zi What kind of dumplings do you love to eat?
我爱吃猪肉饺子。 wǒ ài chī zhū ròu jiǎo zi I love eating pork dumplings.
我不爱吃饺子。 wǒ bù ài chī jiǎo zi I don't love eating dumplings.
你早上吃了什么？ nǐ zǎo shang chī le shén me What did you eat this morning?
我吃了饺子。 wǒ chī le jiǎo zi I ate dumplings.
