📚 Grammar
过程 / 角色 / 扎根 process; role; taking root
在孩子的教育过程中，父母扮演了很重要的角色。 zài hái zi de jiào yù guò chéng zhōng, fù mǔ bàn yǎn le hěn zhòng yào de jué sè In the process of a child's education, parents play a very important role.
帮助孩子们扎根，扎得更深。 bāng zhù hái zi men zhā gēn, zhā de gèng shēn Help children take root, and take root deeper.
日本·秽多·制度 Japanese outcaste system
江户时代的锁国政策下，幕府建立了一套严格的体制。 jiāng hù shí dài de suǒ guó zhèng cè xià, mù fǔ jiàn lì le yī tào yán gé de tǐ zhì Under the Edo period's isolation policy, the shogunate established a strict system.
秽多从事丧葬、行刑和肮脏的处理工作。 huì duō cóng shì sāng zàng, xíng xíng hé āng zāng de chǔ lǐ gōng zuò The Eta performed funeral, execution, and dirty handling work.
废除歧视制度后，他们的生活也没有太大改变。 fèi chú qí shì zhì dù hòu, tā men de shēng huó yě méi yǒu tài dà gǎi biàn After the discriminatory system was abolished, their lives didn't change much either.
他们不能继承其他职业，更不用说和贵族通婚。 tā men bù néng jì chéng qí tā zhí yè, gèng bù yòng shuō hé guì zú tōng hūn They couldn't inherit other professions, let alone marry nobility.
很多人只能靠种地和养家糊口。 hěn duō rén zhǐ néng kào zhòng dì hé yǎng jiā hú kǒu Many could only rely on farming to support their families.
打仗 / 特权 / 任命 war; privilege; appointment
打仗的时候需要盔甲和武器。 dǎ zhàng de shí hou xū yào kuī jiǎ hé wǔ qì During wartime you need armor and weapons.
幕府任命头目管理不同的群体。 mù fǔ rèn mìng tóu mù guǎn lǐ bù tóng de qún tǐ The shogunate appointed leaders to manage different groups.
贵族享有特权，而秽多没有。 guì zú xiǎng yǒu tè quán, ér huì duō méi yǒu Nobility enjoyed privileges, while the Eta did not.
