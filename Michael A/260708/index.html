📚 Grammar
僧人 / 和尚 Buddhist monk
僧人是出家修行的佛教男性。 sēng rén shì chū jiā xiū xíng de fó jiào nán xìng A monk is a Buddhist man who has left home to practice.
和尚是我们对僧人更常用的说法。 hé shang shì wǒ men duì sēng rén gèng cháng yòng de shuō fǎ "Heshang" is a more common way to say monk.
出家 to become a monk/nun
出家就是离开家庭，去寺庙过修行生活。 chū jiā jiù shì lí kāi jiā tíng, qù sì miào guò xiū xíng shēng huó Leaving home means to leave one's family and live a monastic life in a temple.
他三十岁出家当了和尚。 tā sān shí suì chū jiā dāng le hé shang He became a monk at age 30.
戒律 monastic precepts
戒律是僧人必须遵守的规则，比如不杀生、不喝酒。 jiè lǜ shì sēng rén bì xū zūn shǒu de guī zé, bǐ rú bù shā shēng, bù hē jiǔ Precepts are the rules monks must follow, such as not killing and not drinking.
僧人有严格的戒律，不能从事生产。 sēng rén yǒu yán gé de jiè lǜ, bù néng cóng shì shēng chǎn Monks have strict precepts and cannot engage in productive labor.
保持内心的平静 maintain inner peace
修行的人要学会保持内心的平静。 xiū xíng de rén yào xué huì bǎo chí nèi xīn de píng jìng Those who practice must learn to maintain inner peace.
我的理解很浅，还在学习。 wǒ de lǐ jiě hěn qiǎn, hái zài xué xí My understanding is shallow; I am still learning.
佛陀的故事 the story of the Buddha
佛陀本来是王子，但他看到了生老病死，决定出家探索真理。 fó tuó běn lái shì wáng zǐ, dàn tā kàn dào le shēng lǎo bìng sǐ, jué dìng chū jiā tàn suǒ zhēn lǐ The Buddha was originally a prince, but after seeing birth, aging, sickness, and death, he decided to become a monk and seek the truth.
他放弃了王位，离开了侍从和宫殿。 tā fàng qì le wáng wèi, lí kāi le shì cóng hé gōng diàn He gave up the throne and left his attendants and palace.
佛陀的觉悟是摆脱了欲望和痛苦。 fó tuó de jué wù shì bǎi tuō le yù wàng hé tòng kǔ The Buddha's enlightenment was breaking free from desire and suffering.
四谛 / 八苦 Four Noble Truths / Eight Sufferings
佛教说人生有八苦：生老病死、爱别离、怨憎会、求不得、五蕴炽盛。 fó jiào shuō rén shēng yǒu bā kǔ: shēng lǎo bìng sǐ, ài bié lí, yuàn zēng huì, qiú bù dé, wǔ yùn chì shèng Buddhism says life has eight sufferings: birth, aging, sickness, death, separation from loved ones, meeting the disliked, unattainable desires, and the blazing of the five aggregates.
五蕴包括色、受、想、行、识。 wǔ yùn bāo kuò sè, shòu, xiǎng, xíng, shí The five aggregates include form, sensation, perception, mental formations, and consciousness.
三苦 three types of suffering
苦苦是最直接的痛苦，比如呻吟和身体的疼。 kǔ kǔ shì zuì zhí jiē de tòng kǔ, bǐ rú shēn yín hé shēn tǐ de téng Suffering of suffering is the most direct pain, such as groaning and physical pain.
坏苦是快乐消失时的痛苦。 huài kǔ shì kuài lè xiāo shī shí de tòng kǔ The suffering of change is the pain when happiness fades.
行苦是一切事物变化无常带来的苦。 xíng kǔ shì yī qiē shì wù biàn huà wú cháng dài lái de kǔ The suffering of conditioned existence is the pain from everything being impermanent.
无欲则刚的意思是，没有欲望就内心强大。 wú yù zé gāng de yì si shì, méi yǒu yù wàng jiù nèi xīn qiáng dà "Wuyu zhe gang" means that without desire, one's heart becomes strong.
