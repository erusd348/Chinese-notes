📚 Grammar
年假 / 终身职位 / 合同工 annual leave; tenure; contract work
年假是员工的基本权利。 nián jià shì yuán gōng de jī běn quán lì Annual leave is a basic right of employees.
终身职位给了教授很大的安全感。 zhōng shēn zhí wèi gěi le jiào shòu hěn dà de ān quán gǎn Tenure gives professors a great sense of security.
合同工没有终身职位的保障。 hé tong gōng méi yǒu zhōng shēn zhí wèi de bǎo zhàng Contract workers don't have the security of tenure.
炒鱿鱼 / 劳动力 / 互相竞争 firing; labor force; competition
他被公司炒鱿鱼了。 tā bèi gōng sī chǎo yóu yú le He was fired by the company.
劳动力市场上大家都在互相竞争。 láo dòng lì shì chǎng shang dà jiā dōu zài hù xiāng jìng zhēng Everyone is competing with each other in the labor market.
工会 / 声誉 / 供应链 labor union; reputation; supply chain
工会可以保护工人的权利。 gōng huì kě yǐ bǎo hù gōng rén de quán lì Labor unions can protect workers' rights.
公司的声誉取决于供应链的管理。 gōng sī de shēng yù qǔ jué yú gōng yìng liàn de guǎn lǐ A company's reputation depends on supply chain management.
百分之八十的员工都加入了工会。 bǎi fēn zhī bā shí de yuán gōng dōu jiā rù le gōng huì 80% of the employees have joined the labor union.
