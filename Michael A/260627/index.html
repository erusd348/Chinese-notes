📚 Grammar
褪黑素 melatonin
医生建议我吃褪黑素来改善睡眠。 yī shēng jiàn yì wǒ chī tuì hēi sù lái gǎi shàn shuì mián The doctor recommended I take melatonin to improve my sleep.
实验 experiment
科学家做了一个有趣的实验。 kē xué jiā zuò le yī ge yǒu qù de shí yàn Scientists conducted an interesting experiment.
效果 effect; result
这种药的效果很好。 zhè zhǒng yào de xiào guǒ hěn hǎo This medicine has a very good effect.
中药 traditional Chinese medicine
中药不伤身。 zhōng yào bù shāng shēn Chinese medicine doesn't harm the body.
西药 Western medicine
西药见效快。 xī yào jiàn xiào kuài Western medicine takes effect quickly.
概念 concept
这个概念很难解释。 zhè ge gài niàn hěn nán jiě shì This concept is hard to explain.
不流行 not popular
这种风格现在不流行了。 zhè zhǒng fēng gé xiàn zài bù liú xíng le This style is no longer popular now.
选择 choice; to choose
每个人都有自己的选择。 měi ge rén dōu yǒu zì jǐ de xuǎn zé Everyone has their own choice.
相对来说 relatively speaking
相对来说，这里的物价比较低。 xiāng duì lái shuō, zhè lǐ de wù jià bǐ jiào dī Relatively speaking, the prices here are lower.
岳父 father-in-law (wife's father)
我岳父很喜欢喝茶。 wǒ yuè fù hěn xǐ huān hē chá My father-in-law really likes drinking tea.
岳母 mother-in-law (wife's mother)
我岳母做的菜很好吃。 wǒ yuè mǔ zuò de cài hěn hǎo chī The food my mother-in-law cooks is delicious.
老丈人 father-in-law (colloquial)
他老丈人对女婿很好。 tā lǎo zhàng ren duì nǚ xu hěn hǎo His father-in-law treats his son-in-law well.
丈母娘 mother-in-law (colloquial)
丈母娘很喜欢他。 zhàng mǔ niáng hěn xǐ huān tā His mother-in-law likes him very much.
公公 husband's father
我公公今年六十岁了。 wǒ gōng gong jīn nián liù shí suì le My father-in-law is 60 years old this year.
婆婆 husband's mother
我婆婆对我们很好。 wǒ pó po duì wǒ men hěn hǎo My mother-in-law treats us very well.
距离 distance
距离产生美。 jù lí chǎn shēng měi Distance creates beauty (absence makes the heart grow fonder).
行李 luggage
我的行李还没到。 wǒ de xíng li hái méi dào My luggage hasn't arrived yet.
短期旅行 short trip
我计划了一次短期旅行。 wǒ jì huà le yī cì duǎn qī lǚ xíng I planned a short trip.
马克思主义 Marxism
马克思主义对现代社会影响很大。 mǎ kè sī zhǔ yì duì xiàn dài shè huì yǐng xiǎng hěn dà Marxism has a great influence on modern society.
信仰 belief; faith
每个人都有不同的信仰。 měi ge rén dōu yǒu bù tóng de xìn yǎng Everyone has different beliefs.
回心转意 to change one's mind
我希望她能回心转意。 wǒ xī wàng tā néng huí xīn zhuǎn yì I hope she will change her mind.
无产阶级 proletariat
马克思主义重视无产阶级。 mǎ kè sī zhǔ yì zhòng shì wú chǎn jiē jí Marxism values the proletariat.
共产主义 communism
共产主义是他的理想。 gòng chǎn zhǔ yì shì tā de lǐ xiǎng Communism is his ideal.
天主教 Catholicism
他从小信天主教。 tā cóng xiǎo xìn tiān zhǔ jiào He has believed in Catholicism since childhood.
上帝 God
上帝保佑你。 shàng dì bǎo yòu nǐ God bless you.
个体 individual
我们要尊重每一个个体。 wǒ men yào zūn zhòng měi yī ge gè tǐ We must respect every individual.
