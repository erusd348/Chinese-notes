📚 Grammar
主流 mainstream
儒家思想是中国传统文化的主流。 rú jiā sī xiǎng shì zhōng guó chuán tǒng wén huà de zhǔ liú Confucianism is the mainstream of traditional Chinese culture.
传入 to be introduced; to spread into
佛教是从印度传入中国的。 fó jiào shì cóng yìn dù chuán rù zhōng guó de Buddhism was introduced to China from India.
儒家思想在汉朝传入朝鲜和日本。 rú jiā sī xiǎng zài hàn cháo chuán rù cháo xiǎn hé rì běn Confucianism spread to Korea and Japan during the Han dynasty.
流行 popular; to be in vogue
道家思想在魏晋时期很流行。 dào jiā sī xiǎng zài wèi jìn shí qī hěn liú xíng Daoism was very popular during the Wei and Jin periods.
儒家思想 Confucianism
儒家思想影响了中国两千多年。 rú jiā sī xiǎng yǐng xiǎng le zhōng guó liǎng qiān duō nián Confucianism has influenced China for over 2,000 years.
韩国和日本也深受儒家思想的影响。 hán guó hé rì běn yě shēn shòu rú jiā sī xiǎng de yǐng xiǎng Korea and Japan were also deeply influenced by Confucianism.
当官的 government official(s)
在古代，当官的需要学习四书五经。 zài gǔ dài, dāng guān de xū yào xué xí sì shū wǔ jīng In ancient times, government officials needed to study the Four Books and Five Classics.
三纲五常 three cardinal guides and five virtues
三纲五常在封建社会维护了社会秩序。 sān gāng wǔ cháng zài fēng jiàn shè huì wéi hù le shè huì zhì xù The three cardinal guides and five virtues maintained social order in feudal society.
仁 义 礼 智 信 benevolence, righteousness, propriety, wisdom, faithfulness
儒家认为，仁、义、礼、智、信是做人的基本道德。 rú jiā rèn wéi, rén, yì, lǐ, zhì, xìn shì zuò rén de jī běn dào dé Confucianism holds that benevolence, righteousness, propriety, wisdom, and faithfulness are basic morals for being a person.
忠 义 勇 礼 诚 名誉 loyalty, righteousness, courage, propriety, honesty, honor (bushido values)
日本的武士道强调了忠、义、勇、礼、诚、名誉。 rì běn de wǔ shì dào qiáng diào le zhōng, yì, yǒng, lǐ, chéng, míng yù Japanese Bushido emphasized loyalty, righteousness, courage, propriety, honesty, and honor.
固化 to solidify; to become fixed
等级制度在古代社会逐渐固化了。 děng jí zhì dù zài gǔ dài shè huì zhú jiàn gù huà le The hierarchy gradually solidified in ancient society.
压迫 to oppress; oppression
农民受到地主的压迫。 nóng mín shòu dào dì zhǔ de yā pò Peasants were oppressed by landowners.
存天理，灭人欲 preserve heavenly principles, eliminate human desires
存天理，灭人欲是宋明理学的重要思想。 cún tiān lǐ, miè rén yù shì sòng míng lǐ xué de zhòng yào sī xiǎng "Preserve heavenly principles, eliminate human desires" is an important idea in Song-Ming Neo-Confucianism.
格物 to investigate things
格物是儒家获取知识的方法。 gé wù shì rú jiā huò qǔ zhī shi de fāng fǎ Investigating things is the Confucian method of acquiring knowledge.
哲学 philosophy
王阳明的心学是中国哲学的重要流派之一。 wáng yáng míng de xīn xué shì zhōng guó zhé xué de zhòng yào liú pài zhī yī Wang Yangming's School of Mind is one of the important schools of Chinese philosophy.
秩序 order; orderliness
儒家重视社会的秩序。 rú jiā zhòng shì shè huì de zhì xù Confucianism values social order.
心外无物 nothing exists outside the mind
王阳明说：心外无物，心外无理。 wáng yáng míng shuō: xīn wài wú wù, xīn wài wú lǐ Wang Yangming said: Nothing exists outside the mind, no principles exist outside the mind.
良知 conscience; innate knowledge
王阳明认为每个人都有良知。 wáng yáng míng rèn wéi měi gè rén dōu yǒu liáng zhī Wang Yangming believed that everyone has innate knowledge (conscience).
知行合一 unity of knowledge and action
王阳明提出知行合一，知识和行动不能分开。 wáng yáng míng tí chū zhī xíng hé yī, zhī shi hé xíng dòng bù néng fēn kāi Wang Yangming proposed the unity of knowledge and action; knowledge and action cannot be separated.
反传统 anti-traditional
五四运动是一场反传统的文化运动。 wǔ sì yùn dòng shì yī chǎng fǎn chuán tǒng de wén huà yùn dòng The May Fourth Movement was an anti-traditional cultural movement.
集合 to gather; to assemble
学生们集合在广场上抗议。 xué shēng men jí hé zài guǎng chǎng shàng kàng yì The students gathered in the square to protest.
暴动 riot; uprising
社会不平等可能导致暴动。 shè huì bù píng děng kě néng dǎo zhì bào dòng Social inequality can lead to riots.
挑战权威 to challenge authority
他敢于挑战权威。 tā gǎn yú tiǎo zhàn quán wēi He dares to challenge authority.
