📚 Grammar
虚荣 vanity
虚荣心会让人做出错误的选择。 xū róng xīn huì ràng rén zuò chū cuò wù de xuǎn zé Vanity can make people make wrong choices.
她买那么贵的包，只是因为虚荣。 tā mǎi nà me guì de bāo, zhǐ shì yīn wèi xū róng She bought such an expensive bag just out of vanity.
发挥作用 to play a role; to bring into effect
在这个项目中，每个人都发挥了重要作用。 zài zhè ge xiàng mù zhōng, měi gè rén dōu fā huī le zhòng yào zuò yòng Everyone played an important role in this project.
良心 / 不安 conscience / uneasy
你没有良心。 nǐ méi yǒu liáng xīn You have no conscience.
他做了坏事以后，良心很不安。 tā zuò le huài shì yǐ hòu, liáng xīn hěn bù ān After he did something bad, his conscience was very uneasy.
纯真 pure; innocent
孩子的笑容很纯真。 hái zi de xiào róng hěn chún zhēn Children's smiles are very pure.
忠诚 loyal; loyalty
狗对人类很忠诚。 gǒu duì rén lèi hěn zhōng chéng Dogs are very loyal to humans。
他对自己信仰很忠诚。 tā duì zì jǐ xìn yǎng hěn zhōng chéng He is very loyal to his beliefs.
旧约 / 新约 / 赞美诗 / 福音 Old Testament / New Testament / hymn / gospel
旧约和《新约》是基督教的两部分。 jiù yuē hé xīn yuē shì jī dū jiào de liǎng bù fen The Old and New Testaments are the two parts of Christianity.
他们在教堂唱赞美诗。 tā men zài jiào táng chàng zàn měi shī They sing hymns in church.
这本书的内容是福音书。 zhè běn shū de nèi róng shì fú yīn shū The content of this book is the Gospel.
战胜 to overcome; to defeat
他战胜了所有的困难。 tā zhàn shèng le suǒ yǒu de kùn nán He overcame all the difficulties.
我们要战胜自己内心的恐惧。 wǒ men yào zhàn shèng zì jǐ nèi xīn de kǒng jù We must overcome our inner fears.
晋升 to be promoted
他在公司工作了三年，终于晋升了。 tā zài gōng sī gōng zuò le sān nián, zhōng yú jìn shēng le He worked at the company for three years and finally got promoted.
这次晋升对他来说很重要。 zhè cì jìn shēng duì tā lái shuō hěn zhòng yào This promotion is very important to him.
禅宗 / 五祖 Zen Buddhism / Fifth Patriarch
禅宗是中国佛教的一个重要宗派。 chán zōng shì zhōng guó fó jiào de yī ge zhòng yào zōng pài Zen is an important school of Chinese Buddhism.
五祖弘忍是禅宗的重要人物。 wǔ zǔ hóng rěn shì chán zōng de zhòng yào rén wù The Fifth Patriarch Hongren is an important figure in Zen.
二元对立 binary opposition; dualism
善与恶不是简单的二元对立。 shàn yǔ è bù shì jiǎn dān de èr yuán duì lì Good and evil are not a simple binary opposition.
在哲学中，很多概念都是二元对立的。 zài zhé xué zhōng, hěn duō gài niàn dōu shì èr yuán duì lì de In philosophy, many concepts are binary oppositions.
