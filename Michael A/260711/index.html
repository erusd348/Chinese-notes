📚 Grammar
欲望 / 原罪 / 伊甸园 desire / original sin / Eden
欲望是佛教认为痛苦的原因之一。 yù wàng shì fó jiào rèn wéi tòng kǔ de yuán yīn zhī yī Desire is considered one of the causes of suffering in Buddhism.
强烈的欲望让人很难保持内心的平静。 qiáng liè de yù wàng ràng rén hěn nán bǎo chí nèi xīn de píng jìng Strong desires make it hard to maintain inner peace.
基督教说人有原罪，亚当和夏娃在伊甸园吃了分别善恶果。 jī dū jiào shuō rén yǒu yuán zuì, yà dāng hé xià wá zài yī diàn yuán chī le fēn bié shàn è guǒ Christianity says humans have original sin; Adam and Eve ate the fruit of knowledge in the Garden of Eden.
绝对 / 相对 / 角度 absolute / relative / perspective
从我的角度看，这个问题没有绝对的对错。 cóng wǒ de jiǎo dù kàn, zhè ge wèn tí méi yǒu jué duì de duì cuò From my perspective, this question has no absolute right or wrong.
真理是绝对的还是相对的？ zhēn lǐ shì jué duì de hái shì xiāng duì de Is truth absolute or relative?
贪嗔痴 / 无常 / 因果 / 因缘 three poisons / impermanence / karma
佛教的三毒是贪、嗔、痴。 fó jiào de sān dú shì tān, chēn, chī The three poisons in Buddhism are greed, anger, and ignorance.
一切事物都是无常的。 yī qiè shì wù dōu shì wú cháng de All things are impermanent.
佛教相信因果和因缘。 fó jiào xiāng xìn yīn guǒ hé yīn yuán Buddhism believes in cause and effect and conditioned arising.
八正道 / 戒定慧 / 禅定 / 冥想 / 解脱 / 涅槃 the path to liberation
八正道是佛教修行的八条道路。 bā zhèng dào shì fó jiào xiū xíng de bā tiáo dào lù The Eightfold Path is the eight paths of Buddhist practice.
戒定慧是佛教修行的三个核心。 jiè dìng huì shì fó jiào xiū xíng de sān ge hé xīn Precepts, concentration, and wisdom are the three cores of Buddhist practice.
禅定和冥想可以帮助人达到内心的平静。 chán dìng hé míng xiǎng kě yǐ bāng zhù rén dá dào nèi xīn de píng jìng Meditation can help people achieve inner peace.
涅槃是佛教修行的最终目标，是欲望的熄灭。 niè pán shì fó jiào xiū xíng de zuì zhōng mù biāo, shì yù wàng de xī miè Nirvana is the ultimate goal of Buddhist practice — the extinguishing of desire.
解脱就是摆脱痛苦和轮回。 jiě tuō jiù shì bǎi tuō tòng kǔ hé lún huí Liberation means breaking free from suffering and reincarnation.
