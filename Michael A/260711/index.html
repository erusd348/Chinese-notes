📚 Grammar
欲望 / 原罪 / 伊甸园 desire / original sin / Eden
强烈的欲望让人很难保持内心的平静。 qiáng liè de yù wàng ràng rén hěn nán bǎo chí nèi xīn de píng jìng Strong desires make it hard to maintain inner peace.
基督教说人有原罪，亚当和夏娃在伊甸园吃了分别善恶果。 jī dū jiào shuō rén yǒu yuán zuì, yà dāng hé xià wá zài yī diàn yuán chī le fēn bié shàn è guǒ Christianity says humans have original sin; Adam and Eve ate the fruit of knowledge in Eden.
绝对 / 相对 / 角度 absolute / relative / perspective
从我的角度看，这个问题没有绝对的对错。 cóng wǒ de jiǎo dù kàn, zhè ge wèn tí méi yǒu jué duì de duì cuò From my perspective, this question has no absolute right or wrong.
主观的看法和客观的事实不一样。 zhǔ guān de kàn fǎ hé kè guān de shì shí bù yī yàng Subjective opinions and objective facts are different.
无常 / 因果 / 因缘 impermanence / karma / conditioned arising
一切事物都是无常的。 yī qiè shì wù dōu shì wú cháng de All things are impermanent.
佛教相信因果和因缘。 fó jiào xiāng xìn yīn guǒ hé yīn yuán Buddhism believes in cause and effect and conditioned arising.
八正道 / 戒定慧 / 禅定 / 冥想 / 解脱 / 涅槃 the path to liberation
涅槃是佛教修行的最终目标，是欲望的熄灭。 niè pán shì fó jiào xiū xíng de zuì zhōng mù biāo, shì yù wàng de xī miè Nirvana is the ultimate goal — the extinguishing of desire.
解脱就是摆脱痛苦和轮回。 jiě tuō jiù shì bǎi tuō tòng kǔ hé lún huí Liberation means breaking free from suffering and reincarnation.
众生 / 无我 / 投胎 / 造物 living beings / no-self / rebirth / creation
众生平等的思想来自于佛教。 zhòng shēng píng děng de sī xiǎng lái zì yú fó jiào The idea that all beings are equal comes from Buddhism.
佛教认为没有一个固定的自我，这就是无我的概念。 fó jiào rèn wéi méi yǒu yī ge gù dìng de zì wǒ, zhè jiù shì wú wǒ de gài niàn Buddhism holds that there is no fixed self — this is the concept of anatta.
投胎是六道轮回的一部分。 tóu tāi shì liù dào lún huí de yī bù fen Rebirth is part of the six realms of reincarnation.
天地不仁 / 弱肉强食 / 宇宙 / 菩萨 nature is impartial / jungle law / universe / bodhisattva
天地不仁以万物为刍狗。 tiān dì bù rén yǐ wàn wù wéi chú gǒu Heaven and Earth are impartial — they treat all things as straw dogs.
弱肉强食是自然界的规律。 ruò ròu qiáng shí shì zì rán jiè de guī lǜ The law of the jungle is the law of nature.
菩萨发愿度一切众生。 pú sà fā yuàn dù yī qiè zhòng shēng Bodhisattvas vow to liberate all sentient beings.
