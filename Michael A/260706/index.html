📚 Grammar
祷告 to pray; prayer
他每天睡觉前都会祷告。 tā měi tiān shuì jiào qián dōu huì dǎo gào He prays every day before bed.
他们在教堂里一起祷告。 tā men zài jiào táng lǐ yī qǐ dǎo gào They pray together in church.
牧师 pastor; minister
我们教会的牧师讲道很好。 wǒ men jiào huì de mù shī jiǎng dào hěn hǎo The pastor at our church gives good sermons.
他是一位很年轻的牧师。 tā shì yī wèi hěn nián qīng de mù shī He is a very young pastor.
神父 priest; father (Catholic)
天主教的神父不能结婚。 tiān zhǔ jiào de shén fù bù néng jié hūn Catholic priests cannot get married.
神父在教堂里做弥撒。 shén fù zài jiào táng lǐ zuò mí sā The priest is holding Mass in the church.
圣餐 holy communion
每个星期天教会都有圣餐。 měi ge xīng qī tiān jiào huì dōu yǒu shèng cān Every Sunday the church has holy communion.
领圣餐是基督教的重要仪式。 lǐng shèng cān shì jī dū jiào de zhòng yào yí shì Receiving holy communion is an important Christian ritual.
新约 New Testament
新约是用希腊文写的。 xīn yuē shì yòng xī là wén xiě de The New Testament was written in Greek.
耶稣的故事记载在新约里。 yē sū de gù shì jì zǎi zài xīn yuē lǐ The story of Jesus is recorded in the New Testament.
仪式 ceremony; ritual
这个仪式很简单。 zhè ge yí shì hěn jiǎn dān This ceremony is very simple.
婚礼仪式在教堂举行。 hūn lǐ yí shì zài jiào táng jǔ xíng The wedding ceremony is held in the church.
祖宗 ancestors; forefathers
中国人很尊敬祖宗。 zhōng guó rén hěn zūn jìng zǔ zōng Chinese people respect their ancestors very much.
这是我们祖宗留下来的传统。 zhè shì wǒ men zǔ zōng liú xià lái de chuán tǒng This is a tradition passed down by our ancestors.
保佑 to bless; to protect
上帝保佑你。 shàng dì bǎo yòu nǐ God bless you.
希望菩萨保佑我们平安。 xī wàng pú sà bǎo yòu wǒ men píng ān Hope the Buddha protects us safely.
模仿 to imitate; to mimic
小孩子喜欢模仿大人的行为。 xiǎo hái zi xǐ huān mó fǎng dà rén de xíng wéi Children like to imitate adults' behavior.
他在模仿老师的语气。 tā zài mó fǎng lǎo shī de yǔ qì He is imitating the teacher's tone.
国教 state religion
有些国家有国教。 yǒu xiē guó jiā yǒu guó jiào Some countries have a state religion.
英国以基督教为国教。 yīng guó yǐ jī dū jiào wéi guó jiào The UK has Christianity as its state religion.
异端 heresy
在中世纪，异端会被处死。 zài zhōng shì jì, yì duān huì bèi chǔ sǐ In the Middle Ages, heretics were executed.
他的观点被认为是异端。 tā de guān diǎn bèi rèn wéi shì yì duān His views were considered heresy.
异教徒 heretic; pagan
历史上有很多异教徒被迫改教。 lì shǐ shàng yǒu hěn duō yì jiào tú bèi pò gǎi jiào Historically, many pagans were forced to convert.
他们称不信他们宗教的人为异教徒。 tā men chēng bù xìn tā men zōng jiào de rén wéi yì jiào tú They call people who don't follow their religion pagans.
宗教 religion
中国有宗教信仰自由。 zhōng guó yǒu zōng jiào xìn yǎng zì yóu China has freedom of religious belief.
你对宗教感兴趣吗？ nǐ duì zōng jiào gǎn xìng qù ma Are you interested in religion?
除掉 to remove; to get rid of
请把这些杂草除掉。 qǐng bǎ zhè xiē zá cǎo chú diào Please remove these weeds.
他想除掉所有的竞争对手。 tā xiǎng chú diào suǒ yǒu de jìng zhēng duì shǒu He wants to get rid of all competitors.
腐败 corruption
这个国家的腐败问题很严重。 zhè ge guó jiā de fǔ bài wèn tí hěn yán zhòng Corruption in this country is a serious problem.
政府正在打击腐败。 zhèng fǔ zhèng zài dǎ jī fǔ bài The government is cracking down on corruption.
解释权 right to interpret (e.g., of law or text)
最终解释权归公司所有。 zuì zhōng jiě shì quán guī gōng sī suǒ yǒu The right of final interpretation belongs to the company.
法律的解释权在法院。 fǎ lǜ de jiě shì quán zài fǎ yuàn The right to interpret the law lies with the court.
因信称义 justification by faith
因信称义是基督教的重要概念。 yīn xìn chēng yì shì jī dū jiào de zhòng yào gài niàn Justification by faith is an important Christian concept.
马丁·路德提出了因信称义。 mǎ dīng lù dé tí chū le yīn xìn chēng yì Martin Luther proposed justification by faith.
动摇 to waver; to shake
他的决心没有动摇。 tā de jué xīn méi yǒu dòng yáo His determination did not waver.
这件事动摇了我的信心。 zhè jiàn shì dòng yáo le wǒ de xìn xīn This incident shook my confidence.
渡过 to get through; to tide over
我们一起渡过这个难关。 wǒ men yī qǐ dù guò zhè ge nán guān Let's get through this difficulty together.
我们终于渡过了危机。 wǒ men zhōng yú dù guò le wēi jī We finally tided over the crisis.
排外 xenophobic; exclusionary
这个城市对外国人不太排外。 zhè ge chéng shì duì wài guó rén bù tài pái wài This city is not very xenophobic toward foreigners.
排外的态度不利于文化交流。 pái wài de tài dù bù lì yú wén huà jiāo liú A xenophobic attitude is not good for cultural exchange.
地狱 hell
坏人死后会下地狱。 huài rén sǐ hòu huì xià dì yù Bad people go to hell after death.
这段经历简直是地狱。 zhè duàn jīng lì jiǎn zhí shì dì yù This experience was simply hell.
名义 in the name of; nominal
他以帮助别人的名义做了很多坏事。 tā yǐ bāng zhù bié rén de míng yì zuò le hěn duō huài shì He did many bad things in the name of helping others.
他只是名义上的领导。 tā zhǐ shì míng yì shàng de lǐng dǎo He is only a nominal leader.
情况 situation; circumstance
你现在的情况怎么样？ nǐ xiàn zài de qíng kuàng zěn me yàng How is your situation now?
根据目前的情况，我们得改变计划。 gēn jù mù qián de qíng kuàng, wǒ men děi gǎi biàn jì huà Based on the current situation, we have to change the plan.
包容 to tolerate; to be inclusive
我们应该包容不同的文化。 wǒ men yīng gāi bāo róng bù tóng de wén huà We should be inclusive of different cultures.
社会需要更多的包容。 shè huì xū yào gèng duō de bāo róng Society needs more tolerance.
出现 to appear; to emerge
问题突然出现了。 wèn tí tū rán chū xiàn le A problem suddenly appeared.
街上出现了一群人。 jiē shàng chū xiàn le yī qún rén A group of people appeared on the street.
受洗 to be baptized; baptism
他在二十岁的时候受洗了。 tā zài èr shí suì de shí hòu shòu xǐ le He was baptized at the age of twenty.
受洗是成为基督徒的仪式。 shòu xǐ shì chéng wéi jī dū tú de yí shì Baptism is the ceremony of becoming a Christian.
虔诚 devout; pious
她是一个非常虔诚的基督徒。 tā shì yī ge fēi cháng qián chéng de jī dū tú She is a very devout Christian.
他每天虔诚地祷告。 tā měi tiān qián chéng de dǎo gào He prays devoutly every day.
