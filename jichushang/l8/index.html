💬 Dialogue
课文 1
师傅：你好!欢迎光临!
爱珍：你好! 我干洗几件衣服。
师傅：好的。两件衬衫，一条裤子，一条裙子，一件外套，一共五件。放这儿吧。
爱珍：师傅，什么时候可以取?
师傅：一般三天之后取，急着穿的话，两天也行。
爱珍：能不能再快点儿? 特别是那条裙子，我急着穿。
师傅：这样的话，每件衣服加五块钱。
爱珍：好吧，我明天中午来取。对了，外套左袖子上有一块儿油渍，洗的时候注意一下。
师傅：好的，我们尽量给您洗，但是不一定能洗干净。
爱珍：一共多少钱?
师傅：八十五块。
课文 2
飞龙：师傅，麻烦你帮我看看我的手机。
师傅：您稍微等一下，这就来。⁴什么毛病?
飞龙：不小心摔了一下，屏幕黑了，什么都不显示了。
师傅：摔得挺厉害的，这儿都裂了。来电话还能响吗?
飞龙：能听见响，可是没法接听。
师傅：那还好，里面没摔坏，换个屏幕就行。
飞龙：换个屏幕多少钱?
师傅：贵的四百，便宜的二百。
飞龙：我正要换新手机，先随便换个便宜的吧。麻烦您快点儿， 我急着用。
师傅：好，过二十分钟来取。
飞龙：好，我出去一趟，取点儿钱。

📝 Notes
**[1] 急着 (jí zhe) — anxiously; in a hurry to**
"急着 + Verb" means to be in a hurry to do something urgently. The verb after "急着" indicates what the person is anxious to do.
① 早上我急着去教室，忘了带钥匙。 (I was in a hurry to get to the classroom this morning and forgot my keys.)
② 我急着要出去，没时间跟你聊天。 (Im in a rush to go out — no time to chat.)
③ 他一进门就急着问："你看见老王了吗？" (As soon as he came in, he anxiously asked: "Have you seen Old Wang?")
NB: Compare with "笑着回答" (answer while smiling) and "坐着上课" (sit while attending class) — these describe the state while doing something, not urgency.
**[2] ……的话 (de huà) — if (colloquial)**
"……的话" is a hypothetical clause marker. It can be used alone or together with "如果", "要是", "假如" before it. Common in spoken Chinese.
① 急着穿的话，两天也行。 (If you need it urgently, two days is fine too.)
② 你去不了的话，可以让别人去。 (If you cant go, you can send someone else.)
③ 明天不下雨的话，我们就去长城。 (If it doesnt rain tomorrow, we will go to the Great Wall.)
**[3] 特别是 (tèbié shì) — especially; particularly**
"特别是" highlights the most prominent or notable item in a group. A noun or verb usually follows.
① 我喜欢运动，特别是游泳。 (I like sports, especially swimming.)
② 这个餐厅的菜都好吃，特别是烤鸭。 (All the dishes at this restaurant are delicious, especially the roast duck.)
③ 她喜欢小动物，特别是小猫。 (She likes small animals, especially kittens.)
**[4] 稍微 (shāowēi) — slightly; a little; briefly**
"稍微" means "a little bit" or "slightly", indicating a small degree or quantity.
(1) 稍微 + Verb (verb often reduplicated, or with "一", or followed by "一会儿/一些/一下"):
① 老师马上就来，你们稍微等一等。 (The teacher will be here soon — wait a moment.)
② 这个问题你稍微想一想就能回答。 (Think about this question a bit and you will be able to answer it.)
(2) 稍微 + Adj/Verb + 一点儿/一些:
③ 请稍微大声一点儿。 (Please speak a bit louder.)
**[5] 趟 (tàng) — measure word for round trips**
"趟" is a verbal measure word used for one complete round trip — going somewhere and coming back. Together with a numeral, it functions as a complement of frequency.
① 我出去一趟。 (Im going out for a bit.)
② 我去过北京两趟。 (Ive been to Beijing twice — round trips.)
③ 他昨天跑了三趟银行。 (He went to the bank three times yesterday.)
**[6] Adj + 多了 — much more (showing big difference)**
"Adjective + 多了" means the difference is big. It expresses a change or comparison. The object of comparison is often stated, but if not, it is implied by context.
① 老百姓的生活的确方便多了。 (Peoples lives are indeed much more convenient.)
② 这孩子比以前胖多了。 (This child is much chubbier than before.)
③ 跟大商场比，有的东西在网上买便宜多了。 (Compared to big shopping malls, some things are much cheaper online.)
**[7] 拿……来说 (ná...lái shuō) — take...for example**
"拿……来说" is a common way to give an example. A noun, noun phrase, or verb phrase can be placed between "拿" and "来说".
① 就拿吃饭来说吧，现在比以前方便多了。 (Take eating for example — it is much more convenient now than before.)
② 我们班每个人都有自己的爱好，拿李钟文来说吧，他是个电脑迷。 (Everyone in our class has their own hobbies. Take Li Zhongwen — he is a computer fanatic.)
③ 不同地方的人有不同的口味，拿四川菜来说，特点是辣。 (People from different places have different tastes. Take Sichuan food — its characteristic is spiciness.)
