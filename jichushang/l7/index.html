李钟文：望月，你看学院发的旅行计划了吗?

望月：我知道了，你想去草原骑马、吃烤肉。行!你去哪儿，我
就去哪儿。咱俩一起去。

课文 3
为了让留学生的学习生活更加丰富，学院要组织大家去外地旅游，一共有三个地方：内蒙古草原、大同和洛阳。上星期办公室给每个同学都发了一份旅行计划，向大家介绍游览内容和时间安排。这几天同学们都在讨论去哪儿旅游。喜欢自然风光的想去草原，喜欢古迹的想去大同，喜欢中国武术的想去洛阳。可是更多的人对三条路线都有兴趣，不知道去哪儿好。
📝 Notes
**[1] 好 (hǎo) — as a result complement**
"好" used as a result complement after a verb indicates that the action has been completed to a satisfactory degree — done properly, done well, or ready.
① 做好准备再出发。 (Prepare well before setting out.)
② 你说好了吗？ (Have you finished saying what you needed to say?)
③ 菜做好了。 (The food is ready/cooked.)
**[2] 除了……(以外) (chúle...yǐwài) — except; besides; in addition to**
This preposition phrase has two meanings:
(1) "除了A以外，B都……" means "except for A, all B..." (excluding A):
① 除了星期天以外，我每天都去图书馆。 (I go to the library every day except Sunday.)
(2) "除了A以外，B也/还……" means "besides A, B also..." (including A):
② 除了故宫以外，我们还去了颐和园。 (Besides the Forbidden City, we also went to the Summer Palace.)
**[3] 说实话 (shuō shíhuà) — to tell the truth; honestly**
"说实话" is a common conversational phrase used at the beginning of a sentence to indicate the speaker is about to express an honest opinion.
① 说实话，我对烤肉也很有兴趣。 (To be honest, Im also very interested in BBQ.)
② 说实话，我不太喜欢这个电影。 (To be honest, I dont really like this movie.)
③ 说实话，你的汉语说得很好。 (To be honest, your Chinese is very good.)
**[4] Interrogative Pronouns as Referentials — 哪儿/谁/什么 + 都/也**
When interrogative pronouns like "哪儿", "谁", "什么" are used with "都" or "也", they take on a referential meaning: "wherever", "whoever", "whatever".
① 哪儿 + Quantifier: 你哪儿都可以去。 (You can go anywhere you want.)
② 第一个"哪儿"表示任何地方，第二个"哪儿"指同一个地方。去哪儿吃都可以。 (Wherever you want to eat is fine.)
③ 他什么都吃。 (He eats everything.)
**[5] Other interrogative pronouns in the same pattern**
Other question words can also be used in the same referential pattern with "都/也".
① 谁想去都可以。 (Anyone who wants to go can.)
② 哪天都行。 (Any day is fine.)
③ 怎么去都可以。 (However you get there is fine.)
**[6] 既……又…… (jì...yòu...) — both...and...**
"既……又……" connects two structures of the same type to indicate two qualities or situations exist simultaneously. Stronger tone than "既……也……".
① 这个公园既漂亮又安静。 (This park is both beautiful and quiet.)
② 他既会说汉语，又会说英语。 (He can speak both Chinese and English.)
③ 这件衣服既便宜又实用。 (This piece of clothing is both cheap and practical.)
**[7] 既……也…… (jì...yě...) — both...and... (lighter tone)**
"既……也……" is similar to "既……又……" but has a milder tone. "也" introduces supplementary or further explanation.
① 他既有经验，也有能力。 (He has both experience and ability.)
② 这里交通既方便，环境也好。 (Transportation here is convenient, and the environment is also good.)
**[8] 等……就…… (děng...jiù...) — once...then...**
"等……就……" means "once something happens, then something else will happen". Used for future events. "等" is similar to "when" or "once".
① 等你去了，你就知道了。 (Once you go, you will understand.)
② 等我有钱了，我就去旅行。 (When I have money, I will travel.)
③ 等他来了，我们就开始。 (Once he arrives, we will start.)
