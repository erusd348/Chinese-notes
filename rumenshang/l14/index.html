💬 Dialogue
💬 D1_
听说四川菜很好吃，咱们去尝尝，好吗？Tīngshuō Sìchuān cài hěn hǎochī, zánmen qù chángchang, hǎo ma?I heard Sichuan food is delicious — let's go try it, OK?
好啊，什么时候去？Hǎo a, shénme shíhou qù?Great! When should we go?
今天晚上怎么样？Jīntiān wǎnshang zěnmeyàng?How about tonight?
今天晚上我有事，明天中午好吗？Jīntiān wǎnshang wǒ yǒu shì, míngtiān zhōngwǔ hǎo ma?I have plans tonight — tomorrow noon?
好。Hǎo.OK.
💬 D2_
这是菜单，请点菜。Zhè shì càidān, qǐng diǎn cài.Here's the menu — please order.
来一个鱼香肉丝，一个麻婆豆腐，再来两碗米饭，一个酸辣汤。Lái yī gè yúxiāng ròusī, yī gè mápó dòufu, zài lái liǎng wǎn mǐfàn, yī gè suānlà tāng.Fish-Fragrant Shredded Pork, Mapo Tofu, 2 bowls rice, Hot & Sour Soup.
要什么饮料？Yào shénme yǐnliào?What drinks?
来一壶茶。Lái yī hú chá.A pot of tea.
请稍等。Qǐng shāo děng.Please wait a moment.
你觉得中国菜好吃吗？Nǐ juéde Zhōngguó cài hǎochī ma?Do you think Chinese food is tasty?
吃是好吃，不过油太多。Chī shì hǎochī, búguò yóu tài duō.It IS tasty, but too oily.
你喜欢吃什么菜？Nǐ xǐhuān chī shénme cài?What food do you like?
我最喜欢吃韩国菜。我喜欢吃辣的。你呢？Wǒ zuì xǐhuān chī Hánguó cài. Wǒ xǐhuān chī là de. Nǐ ne?I like Korean food best. I like spicy food. And you?
我呀，酸的、辣的、咸的、苦的都喜欢吃，就是不喜欢吃甜的。Wǒ ya, suān de, là de, xián de, kǔ de dōu xǐhuān chī, jiùshì bù xǐhuān chī tián de.Me? I like sour, spicy, salty, bitter — just don't like sweet.

📝 Grammar
……是……，但是/不过/就是…… — "It IS..., but..."
Use this concession structure: X + 是 + Adj, 但是/不过/就是 + negative point. Acknowledges a quality, then adds a reservation. 就是 (jiùshì) is the most colloquial.
• 吃是好吃，不过油太多。   Chī shì hǎochī, búguò yóu tài duō.   It IS tasty, but too oily.
• 好是好，就是太远。   Hǎo shì hǎo, jiùshì tài yuǎn.   It IS good, just too far.
好吗 / 吧 — Making Suggestions
好吗 at end = "OK?" (soft). 吧 at end = "let's..." (decisive). Both turn statements into suggestions.
• 咱们去尝尝，好吗？   Zánmen qù chángchang, hǎo ma?   Let's go try it, OK?
• 明天中午好吗？   Míngtiān zhōngwǔ hǎo ma?   How about tomorrow noon?
• 我们去吃饭吧。   Wǒmen qù chīfàn ba.   Let's go eat.
Taste Descriptions
Common taste words: 酸 (suān, sour), 甜 (tián, sweet), 苦 (kǔ, bitter), 辣 (là, spicy), 咸 (xián, salty), 油 (yóu, oily).
• 我喜欢吃辣的。   Wǒ xǐhuān chī là de.   I like spicy food.
• 酸的、辣的、咸的、苦的都喜欢吃。   Suān de, là de, xián de, kǔ de dōu xǐhuān chī.   I like sour, spicy, salty, bitter.
• 就是不喜欢吃甜的。   Jiùshì bù xǐhuān chī tián de.   Just don't like sweet.
