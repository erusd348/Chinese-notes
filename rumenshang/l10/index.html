💬 课文 Dialogue
A: 现在几点？xiàn zài jǐ diǎnWhat time is it now?
B: 现在八点半。xiàn zài bā diǎn bànIt's 8:30.
A: 你早上几点起床？nǐ zǎo shang jǐ diǎn qǐ chuángWhat time do you get up in the morning?
B: 我七点起床。wǒ qī diǎn qǐ chuángI get up at 7.
