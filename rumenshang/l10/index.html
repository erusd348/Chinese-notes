💬 Dialogue
💬 D1_
现在几点？Xiànzài jǐ diǎn?What time is it now?
现在七点一刻。Xiànzài qī diǎn yī kè.It's 7:15.
你几点吃早饭？Nǐ jǐ diǎn chī zǎofàn?What time do you eat breakfast?
我七点半吃早饭。Wǒ qī diǎn bàn chī zǎofàn.I eat breakfast at 7:30.
你什么时候上课？Nǐ shénme shíhou shàngkè?When do you have class?
我从上午八点到十二点上课。Wǒ cóng shàngwǔ bā diǎn dào shí'èr diǎn shàngkè.I have class from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM.
💬 D2_
今天几月几号？Jīntiān jǐ yuè jǐ hào?What's today's date?
今天七月二十二号。Jīntiān qī yuè èrshí'èr hào.Today is July 22nd.
今天星期几？Jīntiān xīngqī jǐ?What day of the week is it?
今天星期三。Jīntiān xīngqīsān.Today is Wednesday.
二十五号是星期天吧？Èrshíwǔ hào shì xīngqītiān ba?The 25th is Sunday, right?
二十五号不是星期天，是星期六。Èrshíwǔ hào bú shì xīngqītiān, shì xīngqīliù.The 25th is not Sunday — it's Saturday.
💬 D3_
星期天咱们去颐和园，怎么样？Xīngqītiān zánmen qù Yíhéyuán, zěnmeyàng?Let's go to the Summer Palace on Sunday, OK?
好啊！什么时候出发？Hǎo a! Shénme shíhou chūfā?Great! What time do we leave?
上午九点，行吗？Shàngwǔ jiǔ diǎn, xíng ma?9:00 AM, OK?
行。咱们几点见面？Xíng. Zánmen jǐ diǎn jiànmiàn?OK. What time shall we meet?
九点差五分，我在学校门口等你。Jiǔ diǎn chà wǔ fēn, wǒ zài xuéxiào ménkǒu děng nǐ.Five to nine, I'll wait for you at the school gate.
📝 Grammar
几点 — Asking the Time
几点 (jǐ diǎn) = "what time?" Answer: number + 点 + minutes.
• 现在几点？   Xiànzài jǐ diǎn?   What time is it now?
• 现在七点一刻。   Xiànzài qī diǎn yī kè.   It's 7:15.
Time Expressions
点 = o'clock, 刻 = quarter-hour, 半 = half, 分 = minute. 差 = "to" (九点差五分 = five to nine).
• 七点一刻   qī diǎn yī kè   7:15
• 九点差五分   jiǔ diǎn chà wǔ fēn   five to nine (8:55)
从...到... — "From... To..."
从 marks start, 到 marks end — for both time and place.
• 从八点到十二点   cóng bā diǎn dào shí'èr diǎn   from 8:00 to 12:00
• 从星期一到星期五   cóng xīngqī yī dào xīngqī wǔ   from Monday to Friday
……怎么样？ — Making Suggestions
Use ……怎么样？ or ……，好吗？ at the end to suggest or invite agreement.
• 咱们去颐和园，怎么样？   Zánmen qù Yíhéyuán, zěnmeyàng?   Let's go to the Summer Palace, OK?
• 上午九点，行吗？   Shàngwǔ jiǔ diǎn, xíng ma?   9:00 AM, OK?
