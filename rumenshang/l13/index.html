💬 Dialogue
A: 我想买个手机，您给我介绍介绍。Wǒ xiǎng mǎi gè shǒujī, nín gěi wǒ jièshao jièshao.I'd like to buy a phone — please tell me about them.
B: 您想买多少钱的？Nín xiǎng mǎi duōshǎo qián de?What price range are you looking for?
A: 两千块左右的。Liǎng qiān kuài zuǒyòu de.Around 2,000 yuan.
B: 您看看这个牌子的，质量又好，价钱又便宜。Nín kànkan zhè ge páizi de, zhìliàng yòu hǎo, jiàqian yòu piányi.Look at this brand — both good quality and affordable.
A: 样子也挺好看。Yàngzi yě tǐng hǎokàn.The design is also quite nice.
A: 这双鞋是多大号的？Zhè shuāng xié shì duō dà hào de?What size are these shoes?
B: 25号的。Èrshíwǔ hào de.Size 25.
A: 我可以试试吗？Wǒ kěyǐ shìshi ma?Can I try them on?
B: 可以。Kěyǐ.Sure.
A: 这双有点儿小，有大一点儿的吗？Zhè shuāng yǒudiǎnr xiǎo, yǒu dà yìdiǎnr de ma?These are a bit small — do you have bigger ones?
B: 有，您再试试25号半的。Yǒu, nín zài shìshi èrshíwǔ hào bàn de.Yes, try these 25.5.
A: 这双不大也不小，挺合适。Zhè shuāng bú dà yě bù xiǎo, tǐng héshì.These are neither too big nor too small — just right.
A: 您要买点儿什么？Nín yào mǎi diǎnr shénme?What would you like to buy?
B: 看看那件白的真丝衬衣。有别的颜色的吗？Kànkan nà jiàn bái de zhēnsī chènyī. Yǒu bié de yánsè de ma?Let me see that white silk shirt. Do you have other colors?
A: 没有，只有这一种颜色。Méiyǒu, zhǐ yǒu zhè yī zhǒng yánsè.No, only this color.
B: 多少钱一件？Duōshǎo qián yī jiàn?How much for one?
A: 一百八。Yībǎi bā.180 yuan.
B: 太贵了，便宜点儿吧。Tài guì le, piányi diǎnr ba.Too expensive — make it cheaper.
A: 您给一百六吧。Nín gěi yībǎi liù ba.Give me 160.
B: 再便宜点儿，一百五怎么样？Zài piányi diǎnr, yībǎi wǔ zěnmeyàng?A bit cheaper — how about 150?
A: 行。Xíng.OK.
📝 Grammar
想 vs. 要 — "Want" vs. "Will/Going To"
想 = want/would like (desire, can be negated: 不想). 要 = want/will (stronger, more definite; negation uses 不想, NOT 不要). 不要 = "don't" (prohibition).
• 我想买个手机。   Wǒ xiǎng mǎi gè shǒujī.   I'd like to buy a phone. (desire)
• 我不想买。   Wǒ bù xiǎng mǎi.   I don't want to buy.
• 您要买点儿什么？   Nín yào mǎi diǎnr shénme?   What would you like to buy? (more definite)
又...又... — "Both... And..."
又 + Adj + 又 + Adj = both qualities simultaneously. Used for two positive or two negative qualities.
• 质量又好，价钱又便宜。   Zhìliàng yòu hǎo, jiàqian yòu piányi.   Both good quality and cheap.
• 又快又舒服。   Yòu kuài yòu shūfu.   Both fast and comfortable.
有点儿 vs. 一点儿
有点儿 + Adj = "a bit too..." (often implies dissatisfaction). 一点儿 follows verbs or nouns: "a little."
• 这双有点儿小。   Zhè shuāng yǒudiǎnr xiǎo.   These are a bit too small.
• 有大一点儿的吗？   Yǒu dà yìdiǎnr de ma?   Do you have bigger ones?
• 便宜点儿吧。   Piányi diǎnr ba.   Make it cheaper.
Verb Reduplication — Softening Tone
Repeating a verb (看看, 试试, 介绍介绍) makes it casual and brief — sounds friendlier.
• 你给我介绍介绍。   Nǐ gěi wǒ jièshao jièshao.   Tell me about them. (casual)
• 我试试。   Wǒ shìshi.   Let me try.
