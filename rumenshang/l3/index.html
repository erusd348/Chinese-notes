💬 课文 Dialogue
A: 你吃什么？nǐ chī shén meWhat do you want to eat?
B: 我吃米饭。wǒ chī mǐ fànI want rice.
A: 你喝什么？nǐ hē shén meWhat do you want to drink?
B: 我喝茶。wǒ hē cháI drink tea.
