📝 Grammar
什么 — Asking "What"
什么 (shénme) is placed where the answer would go — the word order stays the same as a statement.
• 你吃什么？   Nǐ chī shénme?   What do you eat?
• 你喝什么？   Nǐ hē shénme?   What do you drink?
• 你买什么？   Nǐ mǎi shénme?   What do you buy?
S + V + O — Basic Sentence Order
Chinese follows Subject-Verb-Object order. The question word simply replaces the thing you're asking about.
• 我吃饺子。   Wǒ chī jiǎozi.   I eat dumplings.
• 我喝啤酒。   Wǒ hē píjiǔ.   I drink beer.
• 我买词典。   Wǒ mǎi cídiǎn.   I buy a dictionary.
