📝 Grammar
怎么 — Asking "How" (Method/Way)
怎么 (zěnme) + verb = "how to (do something)?" Used for asking directions, methods, and ways of doing things.
• 去邮局怎么走？   Qù yóujú zěnme zǒu?   How do I get to the post office?
• 这个字怎么写？   Zhè ge zì zěnme xiě?   How do you write this character?
先...然后... — "First... Then..." (Sequencing)
先 (xiān) for the first action, 然后 (ránhòu) for the next. Use when giving step-by-step directions or instructions.
• 先一直走，然后往右拐。   Xiān yìzhí zǒu, ránhòu wǎng yòu guǎi.   First go straight, then turn right.
• 先坐公共汽车，然后坐地铁。   Xiān zuò gōnggòng qìchē, ránhòu zuò dìtiě.   First take the bus, then take the subway.
往 + Direction — "Towards..."
往 (wǎng) + direction word = "towards (direction)." Used with 拐 (turn), 走 (walk), and other movement verbs.
• 往右拐   wǎng yòu guǎi   turn right
• 往前走   wǎng qián zǒu   walk forward
还是 vs. 或者 — "Or" in Questions vs. Statements
还是 (háishì) = "or" in questions (choice). 或者 (huòzhě) = "or" in statements (alternatives).
• 坐公共汽车还是坐地铁？   Zuò gōnggòngqìchē háishì zuò dìtiě?   Take the bus or the subway?
• 坐地铁或者坐出租车。   Zuò dìtiě huòzhě zuò chūzūchē.   Take the subway or take a taxi.
应该 — "Should"
应该 (yīnggāi) + verb = "should (do something)." Expresses obligation or recommendation.
• 你应该坐地铁。   Nǐ yīnggāi zuò dìtiě.   You should take the subway.
• 我应该送礼物吗？   Wǒ yīnggāi sòng lǐwù ma?   Should I give a gift?
