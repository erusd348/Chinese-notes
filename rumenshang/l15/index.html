💬 Dialogue
A: 去邮局怎么走？Qù yóujú zěnme zǒu?How do I get to the post office?
B: 一直往前走，到十字路口往右拐。Yīzhí wǎng qián zǒu, dào shízì lùkǒu wǎng yòu guǎi.Go straight, turn right at the intersection.
A: 离这儿多远？Lí zhèr duō yuǎn?How far is it from here?
B: 二百多米。Èrbǎi duō mǐ.A little over 200 meters.
A: 从这儿到百货大楼有多远？Cóng zhèr dào bǎihuò dàlóu yǒu duō yuǎn?How far is it from here to the department store?
B: 十三四公里。Shísānsì gōnglǐ.About 13 or 14 km.
A: 怎么坐车？Zěnme zuò chē?How do I take the bus?
B: 过马路，先坐2路公共汽车然后换地铁。Guò mǎlù, xiān zuò èr lù gōnggòng qìchē ránhòu huàn dìtiě.Cross the street, first take bus #2, then transfer to the subway.
A: 请问，我去天安门，应该走哪条路？Qǐngwèn, wǒ qù Tiān'ānmén, yīnggāi zǒu nǎ tiáo lù?Excuse me, which road should I take to Tiananmen?
B: 走这条路或者那条路都行。Zǒu zhè tiáo lù huòzhě nà tiáo lù dōu xíng.Either this road or that road works.
A: 哪条路近？Nǎ tiáo lù jìn?Which road is closer?
B: 这条路近，不过有点儿乱。Zhè tiáo lù jìn, búguò yǒudiǎnr luàn.This road is closer, but it's a bit chaotic.
A: 我骑车技术不太高，还是走那条路吧。Wǒ qíchē jìshù bú tài gāo, háishì zǒu nà tiáo lù ba.My cycling skills aren't great — I'd better take that road.
A: 周末咱们去大同，好吗？Zhōumò zánmen qù Dàtóng, hǎo ma?Let's go to Datong this weekend, OK?
B: 好啊。坐火车还是坐飞机？Hǎo a. Zuò huǒchē háishì zuò fēijī?Great! By train or by plane?
A: 坐飞机吧，又快又舒服。Zuò fēijī ba, yòu kuài yòu shūfu.Let's fly — it's both fast and comfortable.
B: 可是坐飞机太贵了，还是坐火车吧。Kěshì zuò fēijī tài guì le, háishì zuò huǒchē ba.But flying is too expensive — let's take the train instead.
📝 Grammar
往 + Direction + Verb — "Towards..."
往 (wǎng) + direction + verb = "do (verb) towards (direction)." Used with 走 (walk), 拐 (turn), etc.
• 往前走。   Wǎng qián zǒu.   Go forward.
• 往右拐。   Wǎng yòu guǎi.   Turn right.
多 + Adjective — Asking "How...?"
多 + Adj = "how (adj)?" Common uses: 多远 (how far), 多大 (how old/big), 多高 (how tall).
• 离这儿多远？   Lí zhèr duō yuǎn?   How far from here?
• 二百多米。   Èrbǎi duō mǐ.   A little over 200 meters.
• 有多远？   Yǒu duō yuǎn?   How far is it?
Approximate Numbers (概数)
Several ways: (1) Adjacent numbers: 十三四 (13–14), 二三 (2–3), 二三十 (20–30). (2) Number + 多: 二百多 (200+), 十三四公里 (13–14 km). Note measure word position: 二十多块 vs. 二十块多.
• 十三四公里   shísānsì gōnglǐ   about 13–14 km
• 二百多米   èrbǎi duō mǐ   a little over 200 m
• 二十多块   èrshí duō kuài   a little over 20 yuan
还是……吧 — "Better..." (Preference)
Pattern: 还是 + option + 吧 = "better (pick this option)." Used when weighing two choices and settling on the safer/better one.
• 还是走那条路吧。   Háishì zǒu nà tiáo lù ba.   Better take that road.
• 还是坐火车吧。   Háishì zuò huǒchē ba.   Better take the train.
