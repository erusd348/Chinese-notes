💬 课文 Dialogue
A: 去邮局怎么走？qù yóu jú zěn me zǒuHow do I get to the post office?
B: 一直往前走，到路口往右拐。yì zhí wǎng qián zǒu, dào lù kǒu wǎng yòu guǎiGo straight, turn right at the intersection.
A: 远不远？yuǎn bu yuǎnIs it far?
B: 不太远。bú tài yuǎnNot too far.
A: 坐几路车？zuò jǐ lù chēWhich bus should I take?
B: 坐5路。zuò wǔ lùTake bus No. 5.
📝 语法 Grammar
怎么 + VHow to do something
Direction: 往 + direction + VToward + direction + verb
