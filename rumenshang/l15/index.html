💬 Dialogue
💬 D1_
去邮局怎么走？Qù yóujú zěnme zǒu?How do I get to the post office?
一直往前走，到十字路口往右拐。Yīzhí wǎng qián zǒu, dào shízì lùkǒu wǎng yòu guǎi.Go straight, turn right at the intersection.
离这儿多远？Lí zhèr duō yuǎn?How far is it from here?
二百多米。Èrbǎi duō mǐ.A little over 200 meters.
💬 D2_
从这儿到百货大楼有多远？Cóng zhèr dào bǎihuò dàlóu yǒu duō yuǎn?How far to the department store?
十三四公里。Shísānsì gōnglǐ.About 13 or 14 km.
怎么坐车？Zěnme zuò chē?How do I take the bus?
过马路，先坐2路公共汽车然后换地铁。Guò mǎlù, xiān zuò èr lù gōnggòng qìchē ránhòu huàn dìtiě.Cross the street, take bus #2, transfer to subway.
💬 D3_
请问，我去天安门，应该走哪条路？Qǐngwèn, wǒ qù Tiān'ānmén, yīnggāi zǒu nǎ tiáo lù?Excuse me, which road to Tiananmen?
走这条路或者那条路都行。Zǒu zhè tiáo lù huòzhě nà tiáo lù dōu xíng.Either this road or that road works.
哪条路近？Nǎ tiáo lù jìn?Which road is closer?
这条路近，不过有点儿乱。Zhè tiáo lù jìn, búguò yǒudiǎnr luàn.This road is closer, but it's a bit chaotic.
我骑车技术不太高，还是走那条路吧。Wǒ qíchē jìshù bú tài gāo, háishì zǒu nà tiáo lù ba.My cycling skills aren't great — I'd better take that road.
💬 D4_
周末咱们去大同，好吗？Zhōumò zánmen qù Dàtóng, hǎo ma?Let's go to Datong this weekend, OK?
好啊。坐火车还是坐飞机？Hǎo a. Zuò huǒchē háishì zuò fēijī?Great! By train or by plane?
坐飞机吧，又快又舒服。Zuò fēijī ba, yòu kuài yòu shūfu.Let's fly — it's both fast and comfortable.
可是坐飞机太贵了，还是坐火车吧。Kěshì zuò fēijī tài guì le, háishì zuò huǒchē ba.But flying is too expensive — let's take the train instead.
📝 Grammar
往 + Direction + Verb — "Towards..."
往 (wǎng) + direction + verb = "do (verb) towards (direction)."
• 往前走。   Wǎng qián zǒu.   Go forward.
• 往右拐。   Wǎng yòu guǎi.   Turn right.
Approximate Numbers (概数)
Two ways: (1) Adjacent numbers: 十三四 (13–14), 二三 (2–3), 二三十 (20–30). (2) Number + 多: 二百多 (200+). Note: for decimals like 200.5, 多 goes AFTER the measure word — 二百块多. Think of the measure word as the decimal point: the whole number part comes before, the fractional part after.
• 十三四公里   shísānsì gōnglǐ   about 13–14 km
• 二百多米   èrbǎi duō mǐ   a little over 200 m
• 二百块多   èrbǎi kuài duō   200-something yuan (200.5)
还是……吧 — "Better..." (Preference)
Pattern: 还是 + option + 吧 = "better (pick this option)." Used when weighing two choices.
• 还是走那条路吧。   Háishì zǒu nà tiáo lù ba.   Better take that road.
• 还是坐火车吧。   Háishì zuò huǒchē ba.   Better take the train.
