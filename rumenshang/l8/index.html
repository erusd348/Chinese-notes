💬 Dialogue
💬 D1_
你是哪国人？Nǐ shì nǎ guó rén?Which country are you from?
我是德国人。Wǒ shì Déguó rén.I'm German.
你叫什么名字？Nǐ jiào shénme míngzi?What's your name?
我叫保罗。请问，您贵姓？Wǒ jiào Bǎoluó. Qǐngwèn, nín guìxìng?I'm Paul. May I ask your surname?
我姓王。Wǒ xìng Wáng.My surname is Wang.
💬 D2_
你学习什么？Nǐ xuéxí shénme?What do you study?
我学习汉语。Wǒ xuéxí Hànyǔ.I study Chinese.
你在哪儿学习？Nǐ zài nǎr xuéxí?Where do you study?
我在北京语言大学学习。Wǒ zài Běijīng Yǔyán Dàxué xuéxí.I study at Beijing Language and Culture University.
你们班有多少学生？Nǐmen bān yǒu duōshǎo xuésheng?How many students in your class?
我们班有十五个学生。Wǒmen bān yǒu shíwǔ gè xuésheng.Our class has 15 students.
几位老师教你们？Jǐ wèi lǎoshī jiāo nǐmen?How many teachers teach you?
三位老师教我们。Sān wèi lǎoshī jiāo wǒmen.Three teachers teach us.
💬 D3_
你住在哪儿？Nǐ zhù zài nǎr?Where do you live?
我住在留学生宿舍。Wǒ zhù zài liúxuéshēng sùshè.I live in the international student dorm.
你住在几号楼？Nǐ zhù zài jǐ hào lóu?Which building do you live in?
我住在七号楼。Wǒ zhù zài qī hào lóu.I live in Building 7.
你的房间号是多少？Nǐ de fángjiān hào shì duōshǎo?What's your room number?
我的房间号是109。Wǒ de fángjiān hào shì yāo líng jiǔ.My room number is 109.
你的电话号码是多少？Nǐ de diànhuà hàomǎ shì duōshǎo?What's your phone number?
我的电话号码是82307531。Wǒ de diànhuà hàomǎ shì bā èr sān líng qī wǔ sān yāo.My phone number is 82307531.
💬 D4_
每天下午你做什么？Měi tiān xiàwǔ nǐ zuò shénme?What do you do every afternoon?
有时候在宿舍休息，有时候去图书馆学习。Yǒu shíhou zài sùshè xiūxi, yǒu shíhou qù túshūguǎn xuéxí.Sometimes rest in the dorm, sometimes study at the library.
你常常跟谁一起学习？Nǐ chángcháng gēn shéi yìqǐ xuéxí?Who do you often study with?
我跟我的中国朋友一起学习。Wǒ gēn wǒ de Zhōngguó péngyou yìqǐ xuéxí.I study with my Chinese friend.
📝 Grammar
哪 — Asking "Which"
哪 (nǎ) + noun = "which (noun)?" Common uses: 哪国人 (which nationality), 哪个房间 (which room), 哪儿 (where).
• 你是哪国人？   Nǐ shì nǎ guó rén?   Which country are you from?
• 你在哪个房间？   Nǐ zài nǎ ge fángjiān?   Which room are you in?
• 你是哪国人？   Nǐ shì nǎ guó rén?   What's your nationality?
几 vs. 多少 — How Many
几 (jǐ) for small numbers (usually < 10) — must be followed by a measure word (几个, 几位, 几号). 多少 (duōshǎo) for larger or unknown quantities — measure word is optional.
• 几位老师？   Jǐ wèi lǎoshī?   How many teachers? (needs 位)
• 你们班有多少学生？   Nǐmen bān yǒu duōshǎo xuésheng?   How many students? (可以省略个)
• 你的电话号码是多少？   Nǐ de diànhuà hàomǎ shì duōshǎo?   What's your phone number? (no measure word)
贵姓 — Polite Forms
贵姓 (guìxìng) = polite way to ask a surname. Answer with just the surname.
• 您贵姓？   Nín guìxìng?   What's your surname?
• 我姓王。   Wǒ xìng Wáng.   My surname is Wang.
