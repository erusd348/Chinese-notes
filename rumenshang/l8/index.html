第8课 你是哪国人

Lesson 8 · Which Country Are You From

📖 生词 Vocabulary
哪nǎwhich
国guócountry
中国zhōng guóChina
美国měi guóAmerica
英国yīng guóUK
日本rì běnJapan
叫jiàoto be called
名字míng ziname
姓xìngsurname / to be surnamed
贵姓guì xìng(polite) your surname
💬 课文 Dialogue
A: 你是哪国人？nǐ shì nǎ guó rénWhich country are you from?
B: 我是美国人。wǒ shì měi guó rénI am American.
A: 您贵姓？nín guì xìngMay I ask your surname?
B: 我姓王。wǒ xìng wángMy surname is Wang.
📝 语法 Grammar
是 + nationalityTo be from a country
您贵姓 - polite formPolite way to ask someone's surname
