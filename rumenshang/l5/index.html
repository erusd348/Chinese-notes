💬 Dialogue
请问，图书馆在哪儿？Qǐngwèn, túshūguǎn zài nǎr?Excuse me, where is the library?
就在那儿。Jiù zài nàr.It's right there.
请问，邮局在哪儿？Qǐngwèn, yóujú zài nǎr?Excuse me, where is the post office?

📝 Grammar
在 — Expressing Location
在 (zài) means "to be at/in/on". Structure: Subject + 在 + Location.
• 图书馆在哪儿？   Túshūguǎn zài nǎr?   Where is the library?
• 图书馆在那儿。   Túshūguǎn zài nàr.   The library is there.
哪儿 — Asking "Where"
哪儿 (nǎr) is the question word for location. Word order: Subject + 在 + 哪儿?
• 银行在哪儿？   Yínháng zài nǎr?   Where is the bank?
• 医院在哪儿？   Yīyuàn zài nǎr?   Where is the hospital?
去 + Place — "Going to..."
去 (qù) + place = "to go to". No preposition needed.
• 你去哪儿？   Nǐ qù nǎr?   Where are you going?
• 我去天安门。   Wǒ qù Tiān'ānmén.   I'm going to Tiananmen.
