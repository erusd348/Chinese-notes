💬 课文 Dialogue
A: 请问，图书馆在哪儿？Qǐngwèn, túshūguǎn zài nǎrExcuse me, where is the library?
B: 就在那儿。Jiù zài nàrIt's right over there.
A: 请问，邮局在哪儿？Qǐngwèn, yóujú zài nǎrExcuse me, where is the post office?
B: 对不起，我不知道。Duìbuqǐ, wǒ bù zhīdàoSorry, I don't know.
A: 你去哪儿？Nǐ qù nǎrWhere are you going?
B: 我去天安门。Wǒ qù Tiān'ānménI'm going to Tian'anmen.
