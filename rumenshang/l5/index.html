第5课 图书馆在哪儿

Lesson 5 · Where Is the Library

📖 生词 Vocabulary
请问qǐng wènexcuse me / may I ask
在zàito be at / in / on
哪儿nǎrwhere
图书馆tú shū guǎnlibrary
邮局yóu júpost office
银行yín hángbank
医院yī yuànhospital
商店shāng diànshop / store
知道zhī dàoto know
对不起duì bu qǐsorry
💬 课文 Dialogue
A: 请问，图书馆在哪儿？qǐng wèn, tú shū guǎn zài nǎrExcuse me, where is the library?
B: 就在那儿。jiù zài nàrIt's right over there.
A: 请问，邮局在哪儿？qǐng wèn, yóu jú zài nǎrExcuse me, where is the post office?
B: 对不起，我不知道。duì bu qǐ, wǒ bù zhī dàoSorry, I don't know.
📝 语法 Grammar
在 + placeTo be located at a place
请问 - polite question starterExcuse me, may I ask...
