课文 Dialogue
A: 你好吗？A: Nǐ hǎo ma?A: How are you?
B: 我很好。你呢？B: Wǒ hěn hǎo. Nǐ ne?B: I'm fine. And you?
A: 我也很好。A: Wǒ yě hěn hǎo.A: I'm fine too.
A: 你爸爸、妈妈都好？A: Nǐ bàba, māma dōu hǎo?A: Are your dad and mom both well?
B: 他们也都很好。B: Tāmen yě dōu hěn hǎo.B: They're also both well.
A: 你忙吗？A: Nǐ máng ma?A: Are you busy?
B: 我不忙。B: Wǒ bù máng.B: I'm not busy.
语法 Grammar
你好吗Common greeting, used between people who already know each other
……呢Used to ask the same question as asked before
也 / 都"也" and "都" are used after the subject, before the verb/adjective
