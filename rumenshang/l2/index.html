💬 Dialogue
你好吗？Nǐ hǎo ma?How are you?
我很好。你呢？Wǒ hěn hǎo. Nǐ ne?I'm fine. And you?
我也很好。Wǒ yě hěn hǎo.I'm also fine.
你爸爸妈妈都好吗？Nǐ bàba māma dōu hǎo ma?Are your parents both well?
他们也都很好。Tāmen yě dōu hěn hǎo.They're also both fine.
你忙吗？Nǐ máng ma?Are you busy?
我不忙。Wǒ bù máng.I'm not busy.
📝 Grammar
吗 — Yes/No Questions
Add 吗 at the end of a statement to turn it into a yes/no question. No word order change needed.
• 你好吗？   Nǐ hǎo ma?   How are you?
• 你忙吗？   Nǐ máng ma?   Are you busy?
• 你累吗？   Nǐ lèi ma?   Are you tired?
• 你饿吗？   Nǐ è ma?   Are you hungry?
• 你渴吗？   Nǐ kě ma?   Are you thirsty?
呢 — Asking the Same Question Back
Use 呢 at the end of a short phrase to bounce a question back: "And you?" / "What about...?"
• A: 你好吗？ B: 我很好。你呢？   A: Nǐ hǎo ma? B: Wǒ hěn hǎo. Nǐ ne?   And you?
• A: 你忙吗？ B: 我不忙。你呢？   A: Nǐ máng ma? B: Wǒ bù máng. Nǐ ne?   And you?
也 and 都 — Word Order
也 (also) and 都 (both/all) come AFTER the subject, BEFORE the verb/adjective. When both appear, 也 comes before 都.
• 我也很好。   Wǒ yě hěn hǎo.   I'm also fine.
• 他们也都很好。   Tāmen yě dōu hěn hǎo.   They are also all fine.
