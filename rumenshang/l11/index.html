📝 Grammar
Location Words — Directions & Positions
Chinese location words come in pairs: 边 (-bian) or 面 (-miàn) can be added to direction words to form location nouns.
• 北边、南边、东边、西边   běibian, nánbian, dōngbian, xībian   north, south, east, west
• 左边、右边、中间   zuǒbian, yòubian, zhōngjiān   left, right, middle
• 前边、后边、上边、下边   qiánbian, hòubian, shàngbian, xiàbian   front, back, above, below
在 + Location — Describing Where Something Is
Structure: Subject + 在 + Location Word. The location word can be a place name or a position relative to something.
• 办公楼在教学楼北边。   Bàngōnglóu zài jiàoxuélóu běibian.   The office building is north of the teaching building.
• 饭馆在超市对面。   Fànguǎn zài chāoshì duìmiàn.   The restaurant is opposite the supermarket.
• 台灯在桌子上边。   Táidēng zài zhuōzi shàngbian.   The desk lamp is on the desk.
有 — Expressing Existence ("There Is")
Structure: Location + 有 + Object. This pattern states that something exists at a location.
• 附近有电影院吗？   Fùjìn yǒu diànyǐngyuàn ma?   Is there a movie theater nearby?
• 学校附近有超市。   Xuéxiào fùjìn yǒu chāoshì.   There's a supermarket near the school.
