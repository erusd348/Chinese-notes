💬 课文 Dialogue
A: 办公楼在哪儿？bàn gōng lóu zài nǎrWhere is the office building?
B: 办公楼在教学楼北边。bàn gōng lóu zài jiào xué lóu běi bianThe office building is north of the teaching building.
A: 图书馆在食堂旁边吗？tú shū guǎn zài shí táng páng biān maIs the library next to the cafeteria?
