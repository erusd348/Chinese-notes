💬 Dialogue
💬 D1_
我们学校里边有教学楼、办公楼、图书馆和留学生宿舍楼。Wǒmen xuéxiào lǐbian yǒu jiàoxuélóu, bàngōnglóu, túshūguǎn hé liúxuéshēng sùshè lóu.Our school has a teaching building, office building, library and intl. student dorm.
办公楼在教学楼北边。Bàngōnglóu zài jiàoxuélóu běibian.The office building is north of the teaching building.
图书馆在办公楼西边。Túshūguǎn zài bàngōnglóu xībian.The library is west of the office building.
留学生宿舍楼在图书馆南边。Liúxuéshēng sùshè lóu zài túshūguǎn nánbian.The intl. student dorm is south of the library.
💬 D2_
学校附近有很多饭馆和商店。Xuéxiào fùjìn yǒu hěn duō fànguǎn hé shāngdiàn.There are many restaurants and shops near the school.
东边还有一个电影院。Dōngbian hái yǒu yī gè diànyǐngyuàn.There's also a movie theater to the east.
电影院对面是一个药店。Diànyǐngyuàn duìmiàn shì yī gè yàodiàn.Opposite the theater is a pharmacy.
药店旁边是一个超市。Yàodiàn pángbiān shì yī gè chāoshì.Next to the pharmacy is a supermarket.

左边是小雨，右边是英男。Zuǒbian shì Xiǎoyǔ, yòubian shì Yīngnán.Xiaoyu is on the left, Yingnan on the right.

保罗在哪儿？保罗在西蒙和英男后边。Bǎoluó zài nǎr? Bǎoluó zài Xīméng hé Yīngnán hòubian.Where's Paul? Paul is behind Simon and Yingnan.
💬 D4_
这是莉莉的桌子。Zhè shì Lìlì de zhuōzi.This is Lily's desk.
桌子上边有一本书、一个台灯，还有一张莉莉男朋友的照片。Zhuōzi shàngbian yǒu yī běn shū, yī gè táidēng, hái yǒu yī zhāng Lìlì nánpéngyou de zhàopiàn.On the desk: a book, a lamp, and a photo of Lily's boyfriend.
桌子下边是莉莉的书包。Zhuōzi xiàbian shì Lìlì de shūbāo.Under the desk is Lily's schoolbag.

📝 Grammar
Existential Sentences — 有 vs. 是
Both express existence, but differently: Location + 有 + Object (there is/are, non-specific); Location + 是 + Object (identifies what is there, more specific).
• 学校附近有很多饭馆。   Xuéxiào fùjìn yǒu hěn duō fànguǎn.   There are many restaurants near the school. (存在)
• 电影院对面是一个药店。   Diànyǐngyuàn duìmiàn shì yī gè yàodiàn.   Opposite the theater is a pharmacy. (确认)
• 药店旁边是一个超市。   Yàodiàn pángbiān shì yī gè chāoshì.   Next to the pharmacy is a supermarket.
在 + Location — Stating Position
Structure: Subject + 在 + Location. The location can be a direction word or a position.
• 办公楼在教学楼北边。   Bàngōnglóu zài jiàoxuélóu běibian.   The office building is north of the teaching building.
• 图书馆在办公楼西边。   Túshūguǎn zài bàngōnglóu xībian.   The library is west of the office building.
• 西蒙在小雨和英男中间。   Xīméng zài Xiǎoyǔ hé Yīngnán zhōngjiān.   Simon is between Xiaoyu and Yingnan.
Location Words as Modifiers — Use 的
When a location word modifies a noun, use 的: Noun + Location + 的 + Noun.
• 桌子上边的书   zhuōzi shàngbian de shū   the book on the desk
• 抽屉里的巧克力   chōuti li de qiǎokèlì   the chocolate in the drawer
• 桌子下边的书包   zhuōzi xiàbian de shūbāo   the schoolbag under the desk
