💬 Dialogue
💬 D1_
最近你身体好吗？Zuìjìn nǐ shēntǐ hǎo ma?How's your health recently?
我身体很好。Wǒ shēntǐ hěn hǎo.My health is very good.
你学习忙吗？Nǐ xuéxí máng ma?Are you busy with studies?
我学习比较忙。Wǒ xuéxí bǐjiào máng.My studies are pretty busy.
你成绩好吗？Nǐ chéngjì hǎo ma?Are your grades good?
马马虎虎。Mǎmǎhūhū.So-so.
你们班同学学习努力吗？Nǐmen bān tóngxué xuéxí nǔlì ma?Do your classmates study hard?
他们学习非常努力。Tāmen xuéxí fēicháng nǔlì.They study extremely hard.
💬 D2_
你的宿舍大不大？Nǐ de sùshè dà bu dà?Is your dorm big?
我的宿舍不太大。Wǒ de sùshè bú tài dà.My dorm is not very big.
宿舍里有没有空调？Sùshè li yǒu méiyǒu kōngtiáo?Is there AC in the dorm?
宿舍里有空调。Sùshè li yǒu kōngtiáo.There is AC in the dorm.
你有没有同屋？Nǐ yǒu méiyǒu tóngwū?Do you have a roommate?
我没有同屋。Wǒ méiyǒu tóngwū.I don't have a roommate.
你的宿舍离教室远不远？Nǐ de sùshè lí jiàoshì yuǎn bu yuǎn?Is your dorm far from the classroom?
我的宿舍离教室很近。Wǒ de sùshè lí jiàoshì hěn jìn.My dorm is very close to the classroom.
💬 D3_
你们学校留学生多不多？Nǐmen xuéxiào liúxuéshēng duō bu duō?Are there many international students at your school?
我们学校留学生挺多的。Wǒmen xuéxiào liúxuéshēng tǐng duō de.Our school has quite a lot.
你们学校食堂怎么样？Nǐmen xuéxiào shítáng zěnmeyàng?How's your school cafeteria?
我们学校食堂不错。Wǒmen xuéxiào shítáng búcuò.Our school cafeteria is pretty good.
📝 Grammar
S + (Subject + Predicate) — Topic-Comment
Topic first, then a full comment about it. Common with body parts, attributes: 身体好, 个子高. The 的 can often be dropped — 我身体很好 = 我的身体很好.
• 你身体好吗？   Nǐ shēntǐ hǎo ma?   How's your health?
• 我身体很好。   Wǒ shēntǐ hěn hǎo.   My health is good.
• 他个子很高。   Tā gèzi hěn gāo.   He is tall.
吗 — Yes/No Questions (Review)
Add 吗 at the end of any statement to turn it into a yes/no question. Also review: A-不-A is another way (大不大？有没有？).
• 你学习忙吗？   Nǐ xuéxí máng ma?   Are you busy with studies?
• 宿舍大不大？   Sùshè dà bu dà?   Is the dorm big?
• 有没有空调？   Yǒu méiyǒu kōngtiáo?   Is there AC?
……怎么样？ — Asking for Opinions/Situations
Use 怎么样 (zěnmeyàng) at the end to ask "How is...?" or "What about...?" — asks for an opinion or description.
• 你们学校食堂怎么样？   Nǐmen xuéxiào shítáng zěnmeyàng?   How's your school cafeteria?
• 我们学校食堂不错。   Wǒmen xuéxiào shítáng búcuò.   Our cafeteria is pretty good.
