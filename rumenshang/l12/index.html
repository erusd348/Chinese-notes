💬 课文 Dialogue
A: 你要红的还是要蓝的？nǐ yào hóng de hái shì yào lán deDo you want red or blue?
B: 我要红的。wǒ yào hóng deI want the red one.
A: 这件衣服怎么样？zhè jiàn yī fu zěn me yàngHow about this piece of clothing?
📝 语法 Grammar
的 - noun phraseAdj + 的 = the [adj] one
还是 - alternative questionA or B? (asking to choose)
