📝 Grammar
иҝҳжҳҜ вҖ” Choice Questions ("Or")
иҝҳжҳҜ is "or" in questions only. Answer by picking one. In statements, use жҲ–иҖ….
• иҰҒзәўзҡ„иҝҳжҳҜиҰҒи“қзҡ„пјҹ   YГ o hГіng de hГЎishГ¬ yГ o lГЎn de?   Do you want red or blue?
• дёҖж ·дёҖж”ҜгҖӮ   YД« yГ ng yД« zhД«.   One of each.
зҡ„ вҖ” Nominalization ("the...one")
Adj + зҡ„ turns an adjective into a noun: зәўзҡ„ = "the red one", еӨ§зҡ„ = "the big one", еҲ«зҡ„ = "other things".
• зәўзҡ„   hГіng de   the red one
• еӨ§зҡ„   dГ  de   the big one(s)
• иҝҳиҰҒеҲ«зҡ„еҗ—пјҹ   HГЎi yГ o biГ© de ma?   Anything else?
дәҶ вҖ” Change of Situation / New Circumstance
At sentence end, дәҶ marks a change or new situation вҖ” NOT completion. It signals something is different now.
• дёҚиҰҒдәҶгҖӮ   BГә yГ o le.   (I) don't want (anymore).
• дёҚеҗғдәҶгҖӮ   BГ№ chД« le.   (I'm) not eating (anymore).
