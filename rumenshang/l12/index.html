💬 Dialogue
💬 D1_
买两支圆珠笔。Mǎi liǎng zhī yuánzhūbǐ.I'd like two ballpoint pens.
要红的还是要蓝的？Yào hóng de háishì yào lán de?Do you want the red one or the blue one?
一样一支。Yī yàng yī zhī.One of each.
还要别的吗？Hái yào bié de ma?Anything else?
不要了。Bú yào le.That's all.
💬 D2_
请问，哪儿卖啤酒？Qǐngwèn, nǎr mài píjiǔ?Excuse me, where can I buy beer?
那儿卖。Nàr mài.Over there.
有青岛啤酒吗？Yǒu Qīngdǎo píjiǔ ma?Do you have Tsingtao beer?
有，要几瓶？Yǒu, yào jǐ píng?Yes, how many bottles?
要四瓶，再要两听可口可乐。Yào sì píng, zài yào liǎng tīng Kěkǒukělè.Four bottles, and two cans of Coca-Cola.
💬 D3_
一共多少钱？Yīgòng duōshǎo qián?How much in total?
十九块四。Shíjiǔ kuài sì.19.40 yuan.
给你钱。Gěi nǐ qián.Here's the money.
您有零钱吗？Nín yǒu língqián ma?Do you have change?
没有。Méiyǒu.No.
收您五十块，找您三十块零六毛。请数一下儿。Shōu nín wǔshí kuài, zhǎo nín sānshí kuài líng liù máo. Qǐng shǔ yīxiàr.Received 50 yuan — change is 30.60. Please count it.
💬 D4_
橘子多少钱一斤？Júzi duōshǎo qián yī jīn?How much per jin for the oranges?
大的三块钱一斤，小的十块钱四斤。Dà de sān kuài qián yī jīn, xiǎo de shí kuài qián sì jīn.Big ones 3 yuan/jin, small ones 10 yuan for 4 jin.
甜不甜？Tián bu tián?Are they sweet?
您尝一下儿，不甜不要钱。Nín cháng yīxiàr, bù tián bú yào qián.Taste one — if not sweet, no charge.
💬 D5_
西红柿怎么卖？Xīhóngshì zěnme mài?How do you sell the tomatoes?
一斤三块八。Yī jīn sān kuài bā.3.80 yuan per jin.
新鲜不新鲜？Xīnxiān bu xīnxiān?Are they fresh?
这是今天早上摘的，新鲜极了。Zhè shì jīntiān zǎoshang zhāi de, xīnxiān jí le.These were picked this morning — extremely fresh.
📝 Grammar
还是 — Choice Questions ("Or")
还是 is "or" in questions only. Answer by picking one. In statements, use 或者.
• 要红的还是要蓝的？   Yào hóng de háishì yào lán de?   Do you want red or blue?
• 一样一支。   Yī yàng yī zhī.   One of each.
的 — Nominalization ("the...one")
Adj + 的 turns an adjective into a noun: 红的 = "the red one", 大的 = "the big one", 别的 = "other things".
• 红的   hóng de   the red one
• 大的   dà de   the big one(s)
• 还要别的吗？   Hái yào bié de ma?   Anything else?
了 — Change of Situation / New Circumstance
At sentence end, 了 marks a change or new situation — NOT completion. It signals something is different now.
• 不要了。   Bú yào le.   (I) don't want (anymore).
• 不吃了。   Bù chī le.   (I'm) not eating (anymore).
