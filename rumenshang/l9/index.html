💬 Dialogue
A: 你家有几口人？Nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ kǒu rénHow many people are in your family?
B: 我家有四口人。Wǒ jiā yǒu sì kǒu rénThere are four people in my family.
A: 你做什么工作？Nǐ zuò shénme gōngzuòWhat do you do?

📝 Grammar
有 — Possession & Family Members
有 (yǒu) also expresses "to have" — possession, including family members. Negative: 没有.
• 你家有几口人？   Nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ kǒu rén?   How many people in your family?
• 我没有兄弟。   Wǒ méiyǒu xiōngdì.   I don't have brothers.
口 — Measure Word for Family
口 (kǒu) is the special measure word for counting family members. It literally means "mouth."
• 我家有三口人。   Wǒ jiā yǒu sān kǒu rén.   There are three people in my family.
• 四口人   sì kǒu rén   four family members
多 + Adjective — "How + Adj"
Use 多 before an adjective to ask "how (adj)?" — e.g., 多大 = how old/big, 多远 = how far.
• 你今年多大？   Nǐ jīnnián duō dà?   How old are you this year?
• 你多大年纪？   Nǐ duō dà niánjì?   How old are you? (formal)
