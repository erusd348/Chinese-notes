💬 Dialogue
💬 D1_
直美，你想不想家？Zhíměi, nǐ xiǎng bu xiǎng jiā?Naomi, do you miss home?
当然想。Dāngrán xiǎng.Of course I do.
你家有几口人？Nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ kǒu rén?How many people in your family?
我家有五口人。Wǒ jiā yǒu wǔ kǒu rén.There are five people in my family.
你家有什么人？Nǐ jiā yǒu shénme rén?Who are in your family?
爸爸、妈妈、两个哥哥和我。Bàba, māma, liǎng gè gēge hé wǒ.Dad, Mom, two older brothers and me.
你有没有兄弟姐妹？Nǐ yǒu méiyǒu xiōngdì jiěmèi?Do you have siblings?
我没有兄弟姐妹，我是独生女。Wǒ méiyǒu xiōngdì jiěmèi, wǒ shì dúshēngnǚ.I don't have siblings — I'm an only daughter.
💬 D2_
你父亲在哪儿工作？Nǐ fùqīn zài nǎr gōngzuò?Where does your father work?
他在医院工作，他是医生。Tā zài yīyuàn gōngzuò, tā shì yīshēng.He works at a hospital — he's a doctor.
你母亲呢？Nǐ mǔqīn ne?And your mother?
她不工作。Tā bù gōngzuò.She doesn't work.
你两个哥哥做什么工作？Nǐ liǎng gè gēge zuò shénme gōngzuò?What do your two brothers do?
他们都是公司职员。你是做什么工作的？Tāmen dōu shì gōngsī zhíyuán. Nǐ shì zuò shénme gōngzuò de?They're both office workers. What do you do?
我是记者，这是我的名片。Wǒ shì jìzhě, zhè shì wǒ de míngpiàn.I'm a journalist — here's my business card.
💬 D3_
你父母今年多大年纪？Nǐ fùmǔ jīnnián duō dà niánjì?How old are your parents?
我父亲今年六十岁，我母亲今年五十八。Wǒ fùqīn jīnnián liùshí suì, wǒ mǔqīn jīnnián wǔshíbā.My father is 60, my mother is 58.
你哥哥今年多大？Nǐ gēge jīnnián duō dà?How old are your brothers?
大哥三十二，二哥二十九。Dàgē sānshí'èr, èrgē èrshíjiǔ.The eldest is 32, the second is 29.
你呢？Nǐ ne?And you?
这是秘密。Zhè shì mìmì.That's a secret.
💬 D4_
这是谁的孩子？Zhè shì shéi de háizi?Whose child is this?
这是我大哥的儿子。Zhè shì wǒ dàgē de érzi.This is my eldest brother's son.
今年几岁？Jīnnián jǐ suì?How old is he?
今年五岁。Jīnnián wǔ suì.He's five.
这孩子真可爱。Zhè háizi zhēn kě'ài.This child is really cute.
📝 Grammar
有 — Possession (Review)
有 (yǒu) = have. 没有 (méiyǒu) = don't have. Family members use the measure word 口 (kǒu, literally "mouth").
• 你家有几口人？   Nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ kǒu rén?   How many people in your family?
• 我家有五口人。   Wǒ jiā yǒu wǔ kǒu rén.   There are five people in my family.
• 我没有兄弟姐妹。   Wǒ méiyǒu xiōngdì jiěmèi.   I don't have siblings.
Asking About Profession
Use 做什么工作 or 是做什么工作的 to ask about someone's job. Answer: 是 + profession.
• 你做什么工作？   Nǐ zuò shénme gōngzuò?   What do you do?
• 你是做什么工作的？   Nǐ shì zuò shénme gōngzuò de?   What's your profession?
• 我是记者。   Wǒ shì jìzhě.   I'm a journalist.
• 他们是公司职员。   Tāmen shì gōngsī zhíyuán.   They're office workers.
Asking About Age
Three forms depending on who you're asking: 您多大年纪？ (elderly), 你多大？ (adults), 你几岁？ (children < 10).
• 您多大年纪？   Nín duō dà niánjì?   How old are you? (polite/elderly)
• 你多大？   Nǐ duō dà?   How old are you? (adult)
• 你几岁？   Nǐ jǐ suì?   How old are you? (child)
