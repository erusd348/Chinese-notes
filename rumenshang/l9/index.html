第9课 你家有几口人

Lesson 9 · How Many People in Your Family

📖 生词 Vocabulary
家jiāfamily / home
口kǒu(measure word for family members)
爸爸bà badad
妈妈mā mamom
哥哥gē geolder brother
姐姐jiě jieolder sister
弟弟dì diyounger brother
妹妹mèi meiyounger sister
工作gōng zuòto work / job
职员zhí yuánstaff / employee
💬 课文 Dialogue
A: 你家有几口人？nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ kǒu rénHow many people are in your family?
B: 我家有四口人。wǒ jiā yǒu sì kǒu rénThere are four people in my family.
A: 你做什么工作？nǐ zuò shén me gōng zuòWhat do you do for work?
B: 我是老师。wǒ shì lǎo shīI am a teacher.
📝 语法 Grammar
有 - indicating possessionTo have / there is
几 vs 多少How many (small vs any number)
