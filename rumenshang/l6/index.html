📝 Grammar
жҳҜ вҖ” "To Be" (Identification)
жҳҜ (shГ¬) connects two nouns to show identity. For adjectives, use еҫҲ вҖ” do NOT use жҳҜ with adjectives.
• жҲ‘жҳҜз•ҷеӯҰз”ҹгҖӮ   WЗ’ shГ¬ liГәxuГ©shД“ng.   I am an international student.
• иҝҷжҳҜжҲ‘д»¬зҡ„иҖҒеёҲгҖӮ   ZhГЁ shГ¬ wЗ’men de lЗҺoshД«.   This is our teacher.
• д»–д»¬йғҪжҳҜз•ҷеӯҰз”ҹгҖӮ   TДҒmen dЕҚu shГ¬ liГәxuГ©shД“ng.   They are all international students.
зҡ„ вҖ” Possession & Modification
зҡ„ (de) shows possession: A + зҡ„ + B = "A's B". With close family, teachers, or institutions, зҡ„ can often be dropped.
• жҲ‘д»¬зҡ„ж•ҷе®Ө   wЗ’men de jiГ oshГ¬   our classroom
• жҲ‘зҡ„жұүиҜӯд№Ұ   wЗ’ de HГ nyЗ” shЕ«   my Chinese book
• жҲ‘зҲёзҲё   wЗ’ bГ ba   my dad (no зҡ„ needed)
• жҲ‘иҖҒеёҲ   wЗ’ lЗҺoshД«   my teacher (no зҡ„ needed)
• жҲ‘д»¬зҸӯ   wЗ’men bДҒn   our class (no зҡ„ needed)
• жҲ‘еҗҢеӯҰзҡ„иҜҚе…ё   wЗ’ tГіngxuГ© de cГӯdiЗҺn   my classmate's dictionary
еҫҲ + Adjective вҖ” Describing with Adjectives
Adjectives don't need жҳҜ. Use еҫҲ before the adjective. еҫҲ is often used as a linker even when you don't mean "very."
