💬 课文 Dialogue
A: 你好，认识你很高兴。nǐ hǎo, rèn shi nǐ hěn gāo xìngHello, nice to meet you.
B: 我来介绍一下儿，这是我朋友。wǒ lái jiè shào yī xià r, zhè shì wǒ péng youLet me introduce. This is my friend.
A: 这是我们的老师。zhè shì wǒ men de lǎo shīThis is our teacher.
