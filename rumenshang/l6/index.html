💬 课文 Dialogue
A: 你好，认识你们很高兴。Nǐ hǎo, rènshi nǐmen hěn gāoxìngHello, nice to meet you.
B: 我来介绍一下儿。Wǒ lái jièshào yíxiàrLet me introduce.
B: 这是我们的老师。Zhè shì wǒmen de lǎoshīThis is our teacher.
