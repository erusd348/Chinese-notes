📚 Grammar
星期天/日 Sunday
我们今天才来。 wǒ men jīn tiān cái lái We just came today.
我星期天陪我的父母去教会。 wǒ xīng qī tiān péi wǒ de fù mǔ qù jiào huì I go to church with my parents on Sundays.
才 only then; just
我毕业了才找到工作。 wǒ bì yè le cái zhǎo dào gōng zuò I only found a job after graduating.
我洗了澡才睡觉。 wǒ xǐ le zǎo cái shuì jiào I go to sleep only after showering.
尊重 to respect
我尊重他的信仰。 wǒ zūn zhòng tā de xìn yǎng I respect his faith.
你应该尊重老师。 nǐ yīng gāi zūn zhòng lǎo shī You should respect the teacher.
既然 since, given that
既然你来了，我们就开始吧。 jì rán nǐ lái le, wǒ men jiù kāi shǐ ba Since you're here, let's start.
既然你不信，就不用勉强。 jì rán nǐ bù xìn, jiù bù yòng miǎn qiǎng Since you don't believe, don't force it.
虔诚 pious, devout
他是一个很虔诚的基督徒。 tā shì yí gè hěn qián chéng de jī dū tú He is a very devout Christian.
上大学以后，我没那么虔诚了。 shàng dà xué yǐ hòu, wǒ méi nà me qián chéng le After starting college, I wasn't as devout.
信仰 faith, belief
每个人都有不同的信仰。 měi gè rén dōu yǒu bù tóng de xìn yǎng Everyone has different beliefs.
他的信仰对他很重要。 tā de xìn yǎng duì tā hěn zhòng yào His faith is very important to him.
传统 / 套路 tradition / routine
每个国家都有不同的传统。 měi gè guó jiā dōu yǒu bù tóng de chuán tǒng Every country has different traditions.
我不喜欢套路。 wǒ bù xǐ huān tào lù I don't like routine/pattern.
知识 knowledge
这本书让我学到了很多知识。 zhè běn shū ràng wǒ xué dào le hěn duō zhī shi This book helped me learn a lot of knowledge.
📖 课文：教会活动 essay: church activity
今天星期天，我跟我家人来了北加州。 jīn tiān xīng qī tiān, wǒ gēn wǒ jiā rén lái le běi jiā zhōu Today is Sunday, I came to Northern California with my family.
我们来参加一个教会的活动。 wǒ men lái cān jiā yí gè jiào huì de huó dòng We came to participate in a church activity.
这个活动一共有5天。 zhè gè huó dòng yí gòng yǒu wǔ tiān This activity lasts 5 days in total.
主题是耶稣基督的无尽恩典。 zhǔ tí shì yē sū jī dū de wú jìn ēn diǎn The theme is the endless grace of Jesus Christ.
每天有3个不同的弟兄讲话。 měi tiān yǒu sān gè bù tóng de dì xiōng jiǎng huà Every day, 3 different brothers give speeches.
讲话后有一个小组会。 jiǎng huà hòu yǒu yí gè xiǎo zǔ huì After the speeches, there is a small group meeting.
在会上，我们要跟其他的弟兄姊妹讨论讲话的内容。 zài huì shàng, wǒ men yào gēn qí tā de dì xiōng zǐ mèi tǎo lùn jiǎng huà de nèi róng At the meeting, we discuss the content of the speeches with other brothers and sisters.
在我说的时候，我会先引用弟兄的话，然后用我的生活来举例。 zài wǒ shuō de shí hou, wǒ huì xiān yǐn yòng dì xiōng de huà, rán hòu yòng wǒ de shēng huó lái jǔ lì When I speak, I first quote the brother's words, then use my life as an example.
讨论完以后，今天的活动就结束了。 tǎo lùn wán yǐ hòu, jīn tiān de huó dòng jiù jié shù le After the discussion, today's activity ends.
我小的时候是一个很虔诚的基督徒。 wǒ xiǎo de shí hou shì yí gè hěn qián chéng de jī dū tú When I was young, I was a very devout Christian.
上大学以后我没那么虔诚了。 shàng dà xué yǐ hòu wǒ méi nà me qián chéng le After college, I wasn't as devout.
因为我没那么多时间去教会了。 yīn wèi wǒ méi nà me duō shí jiān qù jiào huì le Because I didn't have much time to go to church.
现在我毕业了，我回到了家里。 xiàn zài wǒ bì yè le, wǒ huí dào le jiā lǐ Now I've graduated, I've returned home.
我每个星期天陪我的父母去教会。 wǒ měi gè xīng qī tiān péi wǒ de fù mǔ qù jiào huì I accompany my parents to church every Sunday.
因为我尊重他们的信仰。 yīn wèi wǒ zūn zhòng tā men de xìn yǎng Because I respect their faith.
