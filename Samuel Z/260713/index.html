📚 Grammar
不用 no need to
我不用上学。 wǒ bù yòng shàng xué I don't need to go to school.
我不用早起。 wǒ bù yòng zǎo qǐ I don't need to wake up early.
刚 just did
我刚从大学毕业。 wǒ gāng cóng dà xué bì yè I just graduated from college.
感兴趣 / 以前 interested / before
我小的时候，我觉得沈阳的空气质量不太好。 wǒ xiǎo de shí hou, wǒ jué de shěn yáng de kōng qì zhì liàng bù tài hǎo When I was little, I felt Shenyang's air quality was not great.
他很快要退休了。 tā hěn kuài yào tuì xiū le He's going to retire soon.
他以前在Amazon工作。 tā yǐ qián zài Amazon gōng zuò He used to work at Amazon before.
我妈妈生我以后就没工作了。 wǒ mā ma shēng wǒ yǐ hòu jiù méi gōng zuò le My mom hasn't worked since having me.
被 passive
我被别人打中的时候，我觉得很烦。 wǒ bèi bié rén dǎ zhòng de shí hou, wǒ jué de hěn fán When I get hit by others, I feel annoyed.
不仅……而且…… not only...but also
游戏不仅是一个娱乐，而且是一个很好的学习方式。 yóu xì bù jǐn shì yī ge yú lè, ér qiě shì yī ge hěn hǎo de xué xí fāng shì Games are not only entertainment, but also a great way to learn.
📖 课文1：游戏经历 text 1: gaming experience
我高中的时候玩得很多。我的排位在美国是前两千。 wǒ gāo zhōng de shí hou wán de hěn duō. wǒ de pái wèi zài měi guó shì qián liǎng qiān I played a lot in high school. My rank in the US was in the top 2000.
上大学以后，我玩得很少，只是偶尔跟朋友一起玩。 shàng dà xué yǐ hòu, wǒ wán de hěn shǎo, zhǐ shì ǒu ěr gēn péng you yī qǐ wán After starting college, I play very little, only occasionally with friends.
📖 课文2：游戏的意义 text 2: the meaning of gaming
我玩射击游戏的时候，我的注意力很集中。 wǒ wán shè jī yóu xì de shí hou, wǒ de zhù yì lì hěn jí zhōng When I play shooter games, my concentration is very focused.
我打中目标的时候，我觉得很爽。 wǒ dǎ zhòng mù biāo de shí hou, wǒ jué de hěn shuǎng When I hit the target, I feel great.
这个射击游戏还要队友之间的合作。 zhè ge shè jī yóu xì hái yào duì yǒu zhī jiān de hé zuò This shooter game also requires cooperation between teammates.
所以我得到了很多快乐，而且我学会了怎么跟别人合作。 suǒ yǐ wǒ dé dào le hěn duō kuài lè, ér qiě wǒ xué huì le zěn me gēn bié rén hé zuò So I got a lot of happiness, and moreover I learned how to cooperate with others.
