📚 Grammar
附近 nearby
我的家人都住在附近。 wǒ de jiā rén dōu zhù zài fù jìn My family members all live nearby.
她家附近有一个很大的超市。 tā jiā fù jìn yǒu yī ge hěn dà de chāo shì There's a big supermarket near her home.
亲人 family member
我妈妈这边的亲人都在美国。 wǒ mā ma zhè biān de qīn rén dōu zài měi guó My mom's side of the family are all in the US.
我爸爸这边的亲人都在沈阳。 wǒ bà ba zhè biān de qīn rén dōu zài shěn yáng My dad's side of the family are all in Shenyang.
A 离 B A is [distance] from B
他们家离我们家很近。 tā men jiā lí wǒ men jiā hěn jìn Their home is very close to ours.
后来 later; afterwards
我妈妈先来，我爸爸后来才来。 wǒ mā ma xiān lái, wǒ bà ba hòu lái cái lái My mom came first, my dad only came later.
我在大学的时候经常跟我的朋友一起玩。 wǒ zài dà xué de shí hou jīng cháng gēn wǒ de péng you yī qǐ wán In college I often hung out with my friends.
兴奋 excited
我看到篮球比赛的时候很兴奋。 wǒ kàn dào lán qiú bǐ sài de shí hou hěn xīng fèn I get very excited when I watch basketball games.
他赢了比赛，非常兴奋。 tā yíng le bǐ sài, fēi cháng xīng fèn He won the match and was extremely excited.
明星 / 球星 star / sports star
库里是我最喜欢的篮球明星。 kù lǐ shì wǒ zuì xǐ huan de lán qiú míng xīng Curry is my favorite basketball star.
很多球星都很有钱。 hěn duō qiú xīng dōu hěn yǒu qián Many sports stars are very rich.
程序员 / 律师 programmer / lawyer
我的理想是当一个程序员。 wǒ de lǐ xiǎng shì dāng yī ge chéng xù yuán My dream is to be a programmer.
律师的工作很忙，但是收入很高。 lǜ shī de gōng zuò hěn máng, dàn shì shōu rù hěn gāo Lawyers are very busy, but the income is high.
菜鸟 noob; beginner
这个游戏我玩得不好，我还是个菜鸟。 zhè ge yóu xì wǒ wán de bù hǎo, wǒ hái shì ge cài niǎo I don't play this game well, I'm still a noob.
比赛 / 球队 match / team
明天有篮球比赛。 míng tiān yǒu lán qiú bǐ sài There's a basketball game tomorrow.
我们都有喜欢的球队。 wǒ men dōu yǒu xǐ huan de qiú duì We each have a favorite team.
又……又…… (structure) both...and...
这个菜又香又好吃。 zhè ge cài yòu xiāng yòu hǎo chī This dish is both fragrant and delicious.
他又高又帅又有钱。 tā yòu gāo yòu shuài yòu yǒu qián He is tall, handsome, and rich.
理想 dream; aspiration
我的理想是当一个好程序员。 wǒ de lǐ xiǎng shì dāng yī ge hǎo chéng xù yuán My dream is to be a good programmer.
每个人都有不同的理想。 měi ge rén dōu yǒu bù tóng de lǐ xiǎng Everyone has different dreams.
赌博 gambling
我看到他们输了很多钱，所以我觉得我不要赌博。 wǒ kàn dào tā men shū le hěn duō qián, suǒ yǐ wǒ jué de wǒ bù yào dǔ bó I saw them lose a lot of money, so I think I shouldn't gamble.
课文 lesson text
我从小很喜欢体育。我小的时候玩了很多体育游戏，比如2K20。 wǒ cóng xiǎo hěn xǐ huan tǐ yù. wǒ xiǎo de shí hou wán le hěn duō tǐ yù yóu xì, bǐ rú 2K20. I've loved sports since I was young. When I was little, I played a lot of sports games, like 2K20.
虽然我的技术不太好，但是我很喜欢玩。 suī rán wǒ de jì shù bù tài hǎo, dàn shì wǒ hěn xǐ huan wán. Even though my skills aren't great, I really like playing.
我还喜欢看篮球比赛。 wǒ hái xǐ huan kàn lán qiú bǐ sài I also like watching basketball games.
我经常和我的朋友们讨论体育。我们都有一个喜欢的球队。 wǒ jīng cháng hé wǒ de péng you men tǎo lùn tǐ yù. wǒ men dōu yǒu yī ge xǐ huan de qiú duì. I often discuss sports with my friends. We each have a favorite team.
我觉得我不要赌博。 wǒ jué de wǒ bù yào dǔ bó I think I shouldn't gamble.
