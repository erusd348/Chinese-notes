📚 Grammar
听不懂 don't understand (result complement)
我听不懂。 wǒ tīng bù dǒng I don't understand.
我懂了。 wǒ dǒng le I got it!
可以再说一下吗？ can you say it again?
可以再说一下吗？ kě yǐ zài shuō yī xià ma Can you say it again?
可以慢一点说吗？ kě yǐ màn yī diǎn shuō ma Can you speak more slowly?
高兴 happy
高兴 gāo xìng happy
高 tall; height
你多高？ nǐ duō gāo How tall are you?
认识 to know (someone)
我很高兴认识你。 wǒ hěn gāo xìng rèn shì nǐ I'm happy to know you.
来 / 介绍 to come / to introduce
我来介绍一下。 wǒ lái jiè shào yī xià Let me introduce.
我父母来看我了。 wǒ fù mǔ lái kàn wǒ le My parents came to see me.
我父母来波士顿了。 wǒ fù mǔ lái bō shì dùn le My parents came to Boston.
来 + V come and do (to indicate one's turn to do sth)
你来看一下。 nǐ lái kàn yī xià Come and take a look.
我来做晚饭。 wǒ lái zuò wǎn fàn I'll make dinner.
你来说一下！ nǐ lái shuō yī xià You say it!
你来读一下！ nǐ lái dú yī xià You read it out loud!
你来听一下。 nǐ lái tīng yī xià Listen to this.
学习 to study
我爱学习。 wǒ ài xué xí I love to study.
上班 / 下班 go to work / finish work
你下班了吗？ nǐ xià bān le ma Are you done with work?
我下班了。 wǒ xià bān le I'm done with work.
我没下班。 wǒ méi xià bān I'm not done with work.
📖 课文：自我介绍 self-introduction
你好，很高兴认识你，我来介绍一下。 nǐ hǎo, hěn gāo xìng rèn shì nǐ, wǒ lái jiè shào yī xià Hello, nice to meet you, let me introduce myself.
我叫Morghan，我住在美国。 wǒ jiào Morghan, wǒ zhù zài měi guó My name is Morghan, I live in the US.
我今年30岁。我是软件开发师。 wǒ jīn nián sān shí suì. wǒ shì ruǎn jiàn kāi fā shī I'm 30 years old. I'm a software developer.
我喜欢壁球、散步和做饭。 wǒ xǐ huān bì qiú, sàn bù hé zuò fàn I like squash, walking, and cooking.
我常常去健身房健身。 wǒ cháng cháng qù jiàn shēn fáng jiàn shēn I often go to the gym.
我喜欢吃猪肉、苹果和面条。 wǒ xǐ huān chī zhū ròu, píng guǒ hé miàn tiáo I like to eat pork, apples, and noodles.
喜欢喝咖啡和水。 xǐ huān hē kā fēi hé shuǐ I like to drink coffee and water.
这是我的男朋友，他叫Si Xing。 zhè shì wǒ de nán péng yǒu, tā jiào Si Xing This is my boyfriend, his name is Si Xing.
他今年36岁，他是数据科学家。 tā jīn nián sān shí liù suì, tā shì shù jù kē xué jiā He's 36 years old, he's a data scientist.
他喜欢骑行。 tā xǐ huān qí xíng He likes cycling.
我们在一起两年了。 wǒ men zài yī qǐ liǎng nián le We've been together for two years.
