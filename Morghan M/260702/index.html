📚 Grammar
没(有) + V negative for completed actions (haven't done / didn't do)
你吃晚饭了吗？ nǐ chī wǎn fàn le ma Did you eat dinner?
我没吃。 wǒ méi chī I haven't eaten.
我没有睡觉。 wǒ méi yǒu shuì jiào I didn't sleep.
S + 在 + Location subject + at + location
杯子在哪儿？ bēi zi zài nǎr Where is the cup?
杯子在桌子上。 bēi zi zài zhuō zi shàng The cup is on the table.
你在哪儿？ nǐ zài nǎr Where are you?
我在家。 wǒ zài jiā I am at home.
你在家做什么？ nǐ zài jiā zuò shén me What are you doing at home?
我在家吃饭。 wǒ zài jiā chī fàn I am eating at home.
Time + Location + Action specifying when and where
我早上在家吃了酸奶。 wǒ zǎo shang zài jiā chī le suān nǎi I ate yogurt at home in the morning.
你做饭吗？ nǐ zuò fàn ma Do you cook?
你做饭好吃吗？ nǐ zuò fàn hǎo chī ma Is your cooking delicious?
对，很好吃。 duì, hěn hǎo chī Yes, it's very delicious.
你平常早上吃什么？ nǐ píng cháng zǎo shang chī shén me What do you usually eat in the morning?
我平常早上吃燕麦粥、面包和苹果。 wǒ píng cháng zǎo shang chī yàn mài zhōu, miàn bāo hé píng guǒ I usually eat oatmeal, bread, and apple for breakfast.
你一天喝多少杯咖啡？ nǐ yī tiān hē duō shǎo bēi kā fēi How many cups of coffee do you drink a day?
我一天喝一杯咖啡。 wǒ yī tiān hē yī bēi kā fēi I drink one cup of coffee a day.
你平常中午吃什么？ nǐ píng cháng zhōng wǔ chī shén me What do you usually eat for lunch?
我平常中午吃沙拉和酸奶。 wǒ píng cháng zhōng wǔ chī shā lā hé suān nǎi I usually eat salad and yogurt for lunch.
我下午去健身房健身。 wǒ xià wǔ qù jiàn shēn fáng jiàn shēn I go to the gym to work out in the afternoon.
我下午去我朋友家聊天。 wǒ xià wǔ qù wǒ péng you jiā liáo tiān I go to my friend's house to chat in the afternoon.
我去中国留学。 wǒ qù zhōng guó liú xué I'm going to study abroad in China.
我在华盛顿州上大学。 wǒ zài huá shèng dùn zhōu shàng dà xué I go to university in Washington state.
我今天不上学。 wǒ jīn tiān bù shàng xué I'm not going to school today.
我今天不上班。 wǒ jīn tiān bù shàng bān I'm not going to work today.
