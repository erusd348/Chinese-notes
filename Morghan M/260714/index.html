📚 Grammar
去……了 (structure) went to...
我去看我妈妈了。 wǒ qù kàn wǒ mā ma le I went to see my mom.
我去海边沙滩散步了。 wǒ qù hǎi biān shā tān sàn bù le I went for a walk at the beach.
平常 / 可以 usually / can
我平常去公园散步。 wǒ píng cháng qù gōng yuán sàn bù I usually go for a walk in the park.
我可以在家办公。 wǒ kě yǐ zài jiā bàn gōng I can work from home.
你中午可以去食堂吃饭。 nǐ zhōng wǔ kě yǐ qù shí táng chī fàn You can go to the canteen to eat at noon.
地址 / 有多少 address / how many
我家在一号楼三楼301室。 wǒ jiā zài yī hào lóu sān lóu sān líng yī shì My home is in Building 1, 3rd floor, Room 301.
我的宿舍有三个人。 wǒ de sù shè yǒu sān ge rén My dorm has three people.
你有多少个室友？ nǐ yǒu duō shao ge shì yǒu How many roommates do you have?
我有两个室友。 wǒ yǒu liǎng ge shì yǒu I have two roommates.
你们关系好吗？ nǐ men guān xi hǎo ma Is your relationship good?
我们关系很好。 wǒ men guān xi hěn hǎo Our relationship is good.
其他 other
我喜欢去图书馆看科幻小说。 wǒ xǐ huan qù tú shū guǎn kàn kē huàn xiǎo shuō I like going to the library to read sci-fi novels.
我们公司有八百人。 wǒ men gōng sī yǒu bā bǎi rén Our company has 800 people.
