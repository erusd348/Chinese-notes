📚 Grammar
很多 a lot of
中国有很多人。 zhōng guó yǒu hěn duō rén China has a lot of people.
很少 very few; rarely
我们公司人很少。 wǒ men gōng sī rén hěn shǎo Our company has very few people.
我的钱很少。 wǒ de qián hěn shǎo I have very little money.
S + Adv + Adj subject + adverb + adjective
我很好。 wǒ hěn hǎo I'm fine.
我很饿。 wǒ hěn è I'm very hungry.
S + V + N subject + verb + noun
我吃饭。 wǒ chī fàn I eat.
他是好人。 tā shì hǎo rén He is a good person.
换 to exchange; to swap
我换了一个手机。 wǒ huàn le yī ge shǒu jī I changed my phone.
我换了一个女朋友。 wǒ huàn le yī ge nǚ péng you I changed my girlfriend.
我想换钱。 wǒ xiǎng huàn qián I want to exchange money.
我要换钱。 wǒ yào huàn qián I need to exchange money.
你要换多少钱？ nǐ yào huàn duō shǎo qián How much money do you want to exchange?
我要换一百美元。 wǒ yào huàn yī bǎi měi yuán I want to exchange 100 US dollars.
