📚 Grammar
地方 places
我去银行取钱。 wǒ qù yín háng qǔ qián I go to the bank to withdraw money.
我去银行换钱。 wǒ qù yín háng huàn qián I go to the bank to exchange money.
我家有一个院子。 wǒ jiā yǒu yī ge yuàn zi My home has a yard.
他去医院看医生。 tā qù yī yuàn kàn yī shēng He goes to the hospital to see a doctor.
在中国，医生没有很多钱。 zài zhōng guó, yī shēng méi yǒu hěn duō qián In China, doctors don't have a lot of money.
在哪儿 / 那儿 where / there
我知道咖啡店在哪儿。 wǒ zhī dào kā fēi diàn zài nǎr I know where the coffee shop is.
咖啡店在那儿。 kā fēi diàn zài nàr The coffee shop is over there.
请问，洗手间在哪儿？ qǐng wèn, xǐ shǒu jiān zài nǎr Excuse me, where is the restroom?
就在那儿。 jiù zài nàr It's right over there.
就是 exactly is
我就是Morghan。 wǒ jiù shì Morghan I am Morghan.
我就是他的女朋友。 wǒ jiù shì tā de nǚ péng you I am his girlfriend.
